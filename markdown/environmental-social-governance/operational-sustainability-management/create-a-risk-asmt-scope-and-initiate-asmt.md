# Create a risk assessment scope and initiate an assessment

Create a risk assessment scope to define and identify risks for an entity. Identify assessors and approvers for assessments and define the frequency of assessments. After creating the scope, initiate the risk assessments.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_esg\_risk\_mgmt.risk\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Operational Sustainability Management** &gt; **Risk assessment planning** &gt; **Scoped entities**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.
