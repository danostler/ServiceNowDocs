# Setup checklist for the ESG Management \(formerly ESG Management\) application

This checklist includes the set up tasks that you are required to complete in your ServiceNow AI Platform® instance. When you have completed these tasks, the base system is ready for operation.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

Consider creating and printing a PDF of this checklist topic. You can then check off tasks as you complete them. To generate a PDF, click the **Save As PDF** \[Omitted image "save-as-pdf.png"\] Alt text: Save as PDF iconicon and at the top of the topic and click **Selected topic**.
