# Success blueprint template

Use the success blueprint template to create a template with predefined objectives and outcomes for a specific product model. This feature helps streamline the process of associating objectives and outcomes for a product when a success blueprint for that engagement is created.

With the success blueprint template, the customer success managers can ensure that the appropriate objectives and outcomes are being mapped or tagged for engagements in their portfolio. They can:

-   Create common success objectives and outcomes, and ​configure them to meet customer needs during run-time.
-   Organize objectives, outcomes, and initiatives with a timeline view for easier access.

The template provides a standardized approach for creating objectives and outcomes and reduces manual overhead. It also provides guidelines for customer success managers to discuss and identify objectives that are important for the customers. See [Create a success blueprint template](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/acct-lifecycle-events/account-lifecycle-create-blueprint-template.md) for details.
