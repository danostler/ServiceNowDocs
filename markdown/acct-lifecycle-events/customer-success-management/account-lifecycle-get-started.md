# Getting started with Customer Success Management

Learn how to activate Customer Success Management and review the plugins that are activated with it.

-   **[Activate Customer Success Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-activate.md)**  
The Customer Success Management \(com.sn\_acct\_lc\) plugin is available as a separate subscription. Activating this plugin also activates the related plugins required to use the application.
-   **[Plugins activated with Customer Success Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-required-plugins.md)**  
Activating Customer Success Management installs the following related plugins, if they aren't already active.
-   **[Read-only field enhancements](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-read-only.md)**  
Read-only field protections have been moved from the client side to the server side. This prevents users from manipulating data through client-side methods.
