# Zurich Customer Success Management

- [Customer Success Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-events-landing.md) -- The ServiceNow Customer Success Management application enables service providers to streamline, automate, and measure critical workflows across the entire enterprise customer journey—from onboarding and adoption to renewals.
  - [Explore](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-explore.md) -- Customer Success Management enables technology industry providers to create a structured onboarding experience, define and track objectives, outcomes, milestones, and plans to achieve shared goals.

It enables collaboration, transparency, and insight for both internal staff members and external customers involved in the onboarding process.

      - [Health framework](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifeycle-health-frmwk.md) -- Define key business and operational indicators and their impact on the overall health of an engagement.

      - [Data Context Engine](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-setup-metric-data.md) -- The Data Context Engine breaks down silos by aggregating data from multiple sources—both internal and external—collecting it in one place to enable data-driven, actionable decisions.
      - [Data Context Engine (DCE) Insights](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-dce-insights.md) -- DCE Insights is a Data Context Engine feature that enables the visualization of identified metrics for product and capability usage records.

This feature helps streamline the process of associating objectives and outcomes for a product when a success blueprint for that engagement is created.
