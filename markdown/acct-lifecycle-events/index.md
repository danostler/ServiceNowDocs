# Australia Customer Success Management

- [Customer Success Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-events-landing.md) -- The ServiceNow Customer Success Management application enables service providers to streamline, automate, and measure critical workflows across the entire enterprise customer journey—from onboarding and adoption to renewals.
  - [Explore](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-explore.md) -- Customer Success Management enables technology industry providers to create a structured onboarding experience, define and track objectives, outcomes, milestones, and plans to achieve shared goals.
    - [Account onboarding](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-playbook-overview.md) -- The account onboarding feature enables technology industry providers to define a structured, repeatable, onboarding experience. It enables collaboration, transparency, and insight for both internal staff members and external customers involved in the onboarding process.
      - [Onboarding playbook](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-onboard-playbook.md) -- The onboarding playbook has preconfigured activities to help bootstrap the creation of the onboarding experience.

      - [Data import and validation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-data-import-valid.md) -- Use this feature to streamline the migration and onboarding of customer data into the ServiceNow AI Platform.

      - [Engagements](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-engagement.md) -- Engagements can document and track interactions between the service provider and customer accounts throughout the customer success journey.
      - [Health framework](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifeycle-health-frmwk.md) -- Define key business and operational indicators and their impact on the overall health of an engagement.

      - [Touchpoints](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-touchpoints.md) -- Customer success agents can schedule regular touchpoints during the life cycle of an engagement to evaluate progress, provide feedback, and offer guidance.
      - [Product adoption and capability usage](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-product-adopt.md) -- Product adoption and capability usage measure how effectively customers are adopting or engaging with a product.
        - [Product adoption roadmap](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-par-roadmap.md) -- Guide customers through product adoption with roadmaps that define phased approaches, sequence capabilities, and track progress.
      - [Data Context Engine](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-setup-metric-data.md) -- The Data Context Engine aggregates data from multiple sources—both internal and external—collecting it in one place to support configuration decisions based on aggregated data .
        - [Data Context Engine (DCE) Insights](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-dce-insights.md) -- DCE Insights is a Data Context Engine feature that enables the visualization of identified metrics for product and capability usage records.
      - [Success blueprint](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-success-blueprint.md) -- A success blueprint captures what success looks like for a customer, how it is measured, and which activities drive it. It organizes objectives, outcomes, and initiatives that a customer success team tracks for a single engagement.
      - [Playbooks](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-cust-succ-playbook.md) -- Customer success agents can use playbooks to define planned and unplanned activities, success case tasks, and renewal processes.
        - [Customer play](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-success-playbook.md) -- Use the customer play as the starting point to define the customer play processes in your organization. This playbook can contain one or more success case tasks with predefined activities.
        - [Internal play](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-internal-playbook.md) -- Create an internal play to define planned or unplanned activities that you don't want to include the customer in during an engagement lifecycle. An internal play can contain one or more play tasks with predefined activities.
        - [Renewal play](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/acct-lifecycle-events/customer-success-management/account-lifecycle-renewal-playbook.md) -- Use the renewal play to define the account renewal processes in your organization. This playbook is used to drive contract renewals and improve customer retention.
