# View your employee goals

View your goals by accessing your career profile. Your goals that appear in the Goals widget are filtered into the following categories: active, complete, archive.

## Before you begin

Role required: employee \[sn\_egd\_act.employee\]

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  From the User options drop-down list, select **Profile**.

    **Tip:** The User options drop-down list is an icon that contains your user's profile picture.

    The employee profile appears.

3.  Select the **Career** tab.

    The career profile appears.

4.  Select **Goals** in the navigation menu to navigate directly to the Goals widget.

5.  Select the **Active**, **Completed**, or **Archived** tabs in the Goals widget to view your goals by the category in which they're filtered.
