# Suppress outbound notification for services leveraging Universal Request

You can configure outbound email notifications to define the emails a requester must receive when a universal request is created. For example, if your service \(department\) ticket is associated with a universal request, you can customize the notifications to send only the emails that are relevant to the requester.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

**Note:**

Service emails, such as, service ticket creation, notes or state changes, can be suppressed from being distributed.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System Notification** &gt; **Notifications**.

2.  Open your service specific notification that you want to customize.

3.  In the **When to send** tab, under the condition builder, add a new condition step as shown.

    \[Omitted image "ur-suppress-notification.png"\] Alt text: Suppress notification for Services

4.  Click **Update**.
