# Complete a legal matter task through Mobile Agent

When you finish working on an assigned matter task, you can mark the task as complete.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user

## Procedure

1.  In the Mobile Agent, navigate to the **Assigned Tasks** list.

2.  Tap an assigned task.

3.  In the **Details** tab, tap **Mark as Complete**.


## Result

The matter task is completed and its state updates to Review. It sent to the matter owner for approval.
