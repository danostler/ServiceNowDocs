# HR Service Delivery for Healthcare reference

Reference topics provide additional information about the lists and forms that you use to configure and administer HR Service Delivery for Healthcare.

-   **[Installed with HR Service Delivery for Healthcare](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/installed-with-hr-hc.md)**  
Several types of components are installed with activation of the HR Service Delivery for Healthcare plugin, including tables and user roles.
-   **[Healthcare professional profile form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-service-healthcare.md)**  
Access and update confidential HR data of an healthcare professional, such as name, personal contact information, email addresses, professional liability insurance details, and medical degree, using the Healthcare professional profile form.
-   **[Professional ID form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hc-professional-profile.md)**  
Access, update, and validate the Professional ID details of a healthcare professional in the Professional ID form.
-   **[Professional liability Insurances form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-prof-liab.md)**  
Access, update, and validate the Professional liability Insurance details of a healthcare professional in the Professional liability form.
-   **[Professional reference form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-professional-ref.md)**  
Access and validate the Professional reference details of a healthcare professional in the Professional reference form.
-   **[Professional speciality form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hc-prof-spec.md)**  
Review and update the Professional speciality details of a healthcare professional in the Professional speciality form.
-   **[Malpractice history form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-mal-his.md)**  
Access, update, and validate the Malpractice history details of a healthcare professional in the Malpractice history form.
-   **[Education and Training form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-edu-tra.md)**  
Access, update and validate the Education and training details of a healthcare professional in the Education and training form.
-   **[Practice location form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-prac-loc.md)**  
Access, update, and validate the practice location details of a healthcare professional in the Practice location form.
-   **[Employment history form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-emp-det.md)**  
Access, update, and validate the Employment history details of a healthcare professional in the Employment history form.
-   **[Languages Spoken form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-emp-lan.md)**  
Access, update, and validate the language details of a healthcare professional in the Languages form.
-   **[Hospital Affiliations form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-hos-aff.md)**  
Access, review and validate the Hospital Affiliation details of a healthcare professional in the Hospital Affiliations form.
-   **[Payers Enrollment form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-payer-en.md)**  
Access, review and validate the Payers Enrollment details of a healthcare professional in the Payer enrollment form.
-   **[Healthcare Employee Onboarding form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery-for-healthcare/hr-emp-onboarding.md)**  
A hiring agent or a manager places an onboarding request for a healthcare professional via the Healthcare Employee onboarding form in Employee Center.
