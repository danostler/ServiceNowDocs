# Workplace Advisor Overview

Workplace Advisor is an AI-powered solution designed to help Space Planners and Facilities Managers optimize workplace management through intelligent insights and conversational support.

The Minimum Viable Product \(MVP\) version focuses on two primary capabilities:

## Natural Language Q&amp;A via Now Assist Panel

Ask workplace reservation related questions in natural language and receive contextual responses in Now Assist panel. Examples of queries include:

-   Reservation details
-   Meeting durations
-   Reservation check-in details

## Insight Cards on Workplace Central Dashboard

The system generates Insight Cards for key performance indicators on workplace operations such as occupancy, space utilization, reservation trends, and environmental metrics.
