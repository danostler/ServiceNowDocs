# Employee Presence Calendar Widget form

You can use the Employee Presence Calendar Widget form to add a widget to the calendar section on the Presence dashboard.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Name|Name of the section.|
|Order|Order in which the section is displayed on the Presence dashboard.|
|Description|Description of the section.|
|Widget|Widget that is displayed in the section.|
|User criteria|User criteria to decide which users can see the section.|
|Widget Parameters|Parameters passed to the widget.|
|Active|Option to activate the section on the Presence dashboard.|
