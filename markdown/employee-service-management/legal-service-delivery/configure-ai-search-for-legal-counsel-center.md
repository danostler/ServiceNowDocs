# Configure AI Search for Legal Counsel Center

Configure your AI Search implementation for Legal Counsel Center Workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Now Experience Framework** &gt; **UI Builder**.

2.  Search and select **Legal Counsel Center** from the Experiences section of the UI Builder homepage.

3.  Select **Settings** to open the experience settings.

4.  Search and select the **Search source** drop-down in the Global search section.

5.  Select **Legal Counsel Center AI Search** from the drop-down.

6.  Select **Save**.

7.  Go back to the homepage and navigate to **All** &gt; **AI Search** &gt; **AI Search Index** &gt; **Indexed Sources**.

8.  The **AI Search Indexed Sources** page is displayed.

    Search for the following indexed sources:

    -   Legal Requests
    -   Legal Matters
    -   Legal Matter Task
9.  Select each of them to open.

10. Select **Index All Tables** for each of them.

    This process generates the indexes for all the tables. You need not perform any task on the opened page and you can close the page.


## Result

The AI Search for Legal Counsel Center is configured and is available for consumption in the Legal Counsel Center Workspace.
