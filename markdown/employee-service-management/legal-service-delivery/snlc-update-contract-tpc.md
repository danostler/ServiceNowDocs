# Modify a third-party review request

As a requester, modify a legal request to update the request details or replace the attached contract document with an updated version.

## Before you begin

You can modify a request until it is in the Draft state.

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user and sn\_cm\_core.contract\_user

## Procedure

1.  Open the third-party contract review request.

3.  Select **View all requests**.
4.  Open your submitted legal request from the list.
5.  On the Standard Ticket page, from the Actions menu, select **Edit Request**.
