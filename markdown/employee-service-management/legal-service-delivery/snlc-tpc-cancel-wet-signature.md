# Cancel the wet signature process from the Employee Center

Cancel a wet signature process for a Non-disclosure agreement legal requests from the Employee Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user and sn\_cm\_core.contract\_user

## About this task

You can cancel the signature process from the Employee Center under following conditions:

-   The legal request is of type Non-disclosure agreement.
-   The signature type of the Non-disclosure agreement legal request is Wet signature.
-   The state of the contract request should be Awaiting signature.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center**

2.  Select **My Requests** from the header.

3.  Open the legal request for which you want to cancel the signature.

4.  On the request page, from the Actions menu, select **Cancel signature**.

5.  On the confirmation window, select **Cancel signature** to confirm the cancellation.


## Result

The contract status updates to Signature cancelled, and a notification email is sent to all signatories.
