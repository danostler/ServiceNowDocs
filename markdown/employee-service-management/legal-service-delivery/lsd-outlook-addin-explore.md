# Explore Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery

Learn more about Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery through a sample workflow and review the benefits it can provide.

## Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery overview

Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery enables legal fulfiller to submit legal requests and mattersfrom within Microsoft Outlook. Legal fulfiller get seamless experience without having to leave the Microsoft Outlook client.

## Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery users

|User|Description|
|----|-----------|
|Legal request fulfiller|Legal request fulfiller can submit legal request, view details and add attachments for the assigned legal requests directly from the Microsoft Outlook.|
|Legal matter fulfiller|Legal matter fulfiller can submit legal matters, view details and add attachments for the assigned legal matters directly from the Microsoft Outlook.|

## Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery workflow

1.  The legal administrator enables intake forms for ServiceNow for Legal add-in.
2.  The legal request fulfiller perform the following actions:
    1.  Selects an email in Microsoft Outlook for which legal request needs to be submitted.
    2.  Opens Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery.
    3.  Logs into Microsoft Outlook Add-In for Legal Service Delivery.
    4.  Selects option to submit a new request.
    5.  Fills in the required information and submits the legal request.
