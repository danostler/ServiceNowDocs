# View and track amendment requests as a legal user

View the details of an amendment request after it has been submitted and track the activities in the request.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user and sn\_cm\_core.contract\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center**

2.  Select **My Requests** option on the header menu.

3.  Open your submitted amendment request.

4.  View the details of the amendment request.
