# Share requested information for internal review

As a contract fulfiller, share information requested by the reviewer to proceed with the contract document review.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_cm\_core.contract\_fulfiller

## About this task

The work notes added when requesting information are available in the Activity stream. Review the information needed and gather the information to be sent to the reviewer.
