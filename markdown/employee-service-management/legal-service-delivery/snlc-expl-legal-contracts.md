# Contract Management Pro for Legal Service Delivery

Contract Management Pro for Legal Service Delivery enables you to submit, review, finalize, and manage legal contract requests. The legal department can manage and process these submitted legal requests for contracts from a centralized location.

## Features

-   Workflow for tracking, approval, and execution of requests.
-   Support word contract templates for own paper.
-   Support for adding multiple contract and supporting documents in a third-party contract review request.
-   Integration with external storage providers to store final signed contract documents.
-   Support for electronic and wet signatures.
-   Centralized repository for all signed contract documents.

## Benefits

-   Legal support for reviewing and finalizing non-disclosure agreements, third-party legal requestsand amendment requests.
-   Ability to track, prioritize, and work on multiple requests concurrently with the Legal Counsel Center.
-   Contract templates for uniformity across legal contracts and reduced rework.
-   Automatic notifications to monitor contracts and take timely action.
-   Multiple document support for third-party contract review enables reviewing and finalizing multiple contract documents from single request.
