# Review daily log responses

Review daily contact log responses from employees who received a push notification requesting a list of contacts that they interacted with at the workplace.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_imt\_checkin.checkin\_admin or sn\_imt\_tracing.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Contact Tracing** &gt; **Daily Contact Log** &gt; **Contact Logs**.

    Responses to notifications are listed in the Daily Log Acknowledgements list. Because employees respond to the notification by submitting their daily contact log, their records are updated with their acknowledgement status.

    -   If employees have responded, the status is **Acknowledged**.
    -   If employees have not responded yet, the status is **Not Acknowledged**.
    The **Notification count** field shows the number of times the notification is sent to the employees who have not yet responded. You can [send the notification](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/contact-tracing/configure-outreach-notif-daily-log.md) again only to employees who have not yet responded.
