# Create a new Indoor Mapping space

Create a Indoor Mapping space in Workplace Indoor Mapping.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Space Mapping** &gt; **Indoor Maps Administration** &gt; **New Indoor Mapping Space**.
