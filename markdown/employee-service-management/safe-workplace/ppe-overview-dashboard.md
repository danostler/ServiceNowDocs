# PPE Inventory Overview dashboard

Use the PPE Inventory Overview dashboard to track PPE items that are in stock, on order, and assigned to employees.

To view the dashboard, navigate to **PPE Inventory Management** &gt; **Overview Dashboard**.



## End users and roles

|End user and goal|Required role|
|-----------------|-------------|
|PPE Inventory Management admin: Track PPE in stock and assigned over time. Plan ahead to anticipate PPE needs and ensure that all employees have access to the appropriate PPE.|sn\_imt\_ppe.ppe\_admin|

## Reports

|Title|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|PPE Items Consumed|Number of consumable PPE items assigned to employees.|
|PPE Items In Stock|Number of PPE items in stock.|
|PPE Items On Order|Number of PPE items on order.|
|PPE Items In Transit|Number of PPE items in transit.|
|PPE Items In Use|Number of hardware PPE items assigned to employees.|
|PPE Items Missing|Number of PPE items that are missing.|
|PPE Stockrooms By Type|Number of each type of stockroom that stores PPE.|
|Consumables Distributed By User|Number of consumable PPE items assigned to each employee.|
|Consumables In Stock By Location|Number of consumable PPE items in stock at each location.|
|Consumable Models|All models for consumable PPE.|
|Stock Rules|All stock rules for PPE.|
