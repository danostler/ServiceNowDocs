# Register with External Legal Service Center

To submit a request for legal services, register yourself with the External Legal Service Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ext\_portal.ext\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the External Legal Service Center home page by accessing your instance URL and adding a /elp suffix.

    For example, https://example.com/elp.

2.  Select **Register**.

3.  On the Account sign up form, fill in the fields.

    First name, Last name, and Email are required fields.

4.  Select **Sign Up**.

    A verification email is sent to the email address that you provided.

5.  Select **Verify Account** from the verification email.

    -   After your details have been verified, the self-registration request is approved.
    -   On approval of the self-registration request, you receive an email to set the password.
6.  In the set password email, select **Set a new password** to set a new password for your account.

7.  Set a password for your account.

    Your self-registration is complete. You can now log in to the External Legal Service Center.
