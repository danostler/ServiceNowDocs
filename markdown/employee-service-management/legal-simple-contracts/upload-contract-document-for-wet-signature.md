# Add a manually signed contract

As a legal requester, on receiving the signed contract document from the signatories, add the digital version to the legal request record.

## Before you begin

You must have a PDF version of the signed contract.

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Service Portal**.

    **Note:** If you’re using [Employee Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/employee-center-landing-page.md), you can submit a legal request by navigating to the Employee Center portal and selecting a request from the **Legal services** &gt; **Browse Legal services** menu.

2.  Select **My Requests** option in the form context menu.

3.  Select **View all requests** from the drop-down list.

4.  Select the legal request from the list.

5.  Select **Upload Contract** and navigate to the file.

6.  Select **Open**.

7.  If you have uploaded an incorrect file, upload the correct document by selecting **Reupload Contract** from the **Actions** drop-down list.

8.  Select **Close Complete** to close the legal request.

    **Note:** You can’t make any changes to the legal request after the legal request is closed.


## Result

When the legal request is closed, an email with the final contract document attached is sent to the signatories.
