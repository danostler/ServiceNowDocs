# Result of migration from Legal Simple Contracts to Contract Management Pro for Legal Service Delivery

On the running the script to migrate data from Legal Simple Contracts to Contract Management Pro for Legal Service Delivery, the data is copied along with other changes made to Legal Simple Contracts modules, Legal Counsel Center, and the Legal Executive dashboard.

-   After migration, the following modules of Legal Simple Contracts are marked as legacy:
    -   **Simple Contracts Admin**
    -   **Simple Contracts**
    -   **Legal Contracts**
-   Legal contract requests that are not yet complete continue to be visible in the Legal Counsel Center.
-   In the Legal Counsel Center, the migrated contract repository records are displayed in the All Contracts list in Legal Contracts, which you can access by navigating to **Lists** &gt; **Legal Contracts** &gt; **All Contracts**.
-   In the Legal Executive dashboard, legacy contract requests from Legal Simple Contracts are displayed in the following tabs:
    -   Contracts\(legacy\) tab
    -   Contracts tab: Navigate to the Legacy contract requests section to view the contract requests.
-   Any new contract repository records created due to completion of a legal request are automatically migrated to Contract Management Pro for Legal Service Delivery.
