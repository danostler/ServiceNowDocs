# Define a user as an internal signer

Define a user as an internal signer so that the signer can be added to a contract template to automatically populate in a contract document.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_config

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Administration** &gt; **Simple Contracts Admin** &gt; **Internal Signatories**.

2.  Define or modify an internal signer.

    -   To define a new internal signer, click **New**.
    -   To modify an existing internal signer, open the internal signer from the list.
3.  On the form, fill in the fields.
