# Third Party Contract Review form

The Third Party Contract Review form is used to submit a legal request to the legal department to get the third-party contracts reviewed.

[Submit a legal request for a third-party contract review](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/legal-simple-contracts/submit-legal-request-tpc-review.md).
