# Delete outside counsel engagement request

As a legal fulfiller, you can delete outside counsel engagement request that is no longer required.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_fulfiller, sn\_lg\_matter.matter\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Open a legal matter.

Method

</th><th align="left" id="d241172e54">

Action

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td id="d241172e60">

**Using Legal Counsel Center homepage**

</td><td>

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**
2.  Select the **View All** option on the Matters widget.
3.  From the list, open a legal matter record.
4.  Select the **Outside Counsels** tab.


</td></tr><tr><td id="d241172e104">

**Using Legal Counsel Center lists**

</td><td>

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**
2.  Select the list icon \(\[Omitted image "lsd-lcc-list-icon.png"\] Alt text: List icon\).
3.  In the Lists tab, navigate to **Matters** &gt; **All**.
4.  From the list, open a legal matter record.
5.  Select the **Outside Counsels** tab.


</td></tr><tr><td id="d241172e163">

**Using platform view**

</td><td>

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Matter** &gt; **Matter** &gt; **All**
2.  From the list, open a legal matter.
3.  Select the **Outside Counsels** related list.


</td></tr></tbody>
</table>2.  Select the check box of the outside counsel engagement request that you want to delete.

3.  Select **Remove**.

    If you are in platform view, select **Delete** from the Action on the selected rows drop-down.
