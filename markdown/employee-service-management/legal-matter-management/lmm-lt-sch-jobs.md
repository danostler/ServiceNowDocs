# Scheduled jobs installed for Legal Tracker

Scheduled jobs that are installed when the Legal Tracker spoke is enabled. These jobs are used to fetch data from Legal Tracker.

|Scheduled job|Data synced to the table|Active|Description|
|-------------|------------------------|------|-----------|
|Fetch AP Routes Data|Legal Tracker AP Route \[sn\_lg\_matter\_legal\_tr acker\_ap\_route\]|True|Retrieves AP Routes data from the Legal Tracker portal.|
|Fetch Country Data|Legal Matter Country \[sn\_lg\_matter\_legal\_m atter\_country\]|True|Retrieves Country data from the Legal Tracker portal.|
|Fetch Firms Data|Outside Firm \[sn\_lg\_matter\_outside\_ firm\]|True|Retrieves Firms data from Legal Tracker portal.|
|Fetch Matter Financials|Legal Tracker Matter \[sn\_lg\_matter\_legal\_tr acker\_matter\]|True|Retrieves Matter financial data from the Legal Tracker portal.|
|Fetch Matter Templates Data|Legal Tracker Matter Template \[sn\_lg\_matter\_legal\_tr acker\_matter\_template\]|False|Retrieves Matter Templates data from the Legal Tracker portal.|
|Fetch Organizational Unit Data|Legal Tracker Organizational Unit \[sn\_lg\_matter\_legal\_tr acker\_organizational\_ unit\]|True|Retrieves Organizational Unit data from the Legal Tracker portal.|
|Fetch Practice Group Data|LegalTracker Practice Group \[sn\_lg\_matter\_legal\_tr acker\_practice\_group\]|True|Retrieves Practice Group data from the Legal Tracker portal.|
|Fetch Substantive Law Data|Legal Tracker Substantive Law \[sn\_lg\_matter\_legal\_tr acker\_substantive\_law\]|True|Retrieves Substantive Law data from the Legal Tracker portal.|
