# Submit outside counsel engagement request for approval

As a legal fulfiller, submit an outside counsel engagement request for approval.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_matter.matter\_fulfiller, sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Open a legal matter.
