# Remove document access of users in a legal matter

Remove existing users' access to documents attached to a legal matter's artifact when they are no longer required to view those documents.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_matter.matter\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  In the Legal Counsel Center tab, select the list icon \(\[Omitted image "lsd-lcc-list-icon.png"\] Alt text: List icon\).

3.  In the **Lists** tab, under **Matters**, click **All**.

4.  Open a legal matter for which you want to remove users' access to the documents attached to it.

5.  In the **Documents Access** tab, select users for whom you want to remove document access.

6.  Click **Remove Access**.


## Result

The document access of the selected users is removed, and they're also removed from the list. The user will no longer be able to view the matter's documents.
