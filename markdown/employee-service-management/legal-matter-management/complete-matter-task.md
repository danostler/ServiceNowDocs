# Complete a legal matter task

When you finish working on an assigned matter task, mark the task as complete.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  In the Legal Counsel Center tab, select the list icon \[Omitted image "lsd-lcc-list-icon.png"\] Alt text:.

3.  In the **Lists** tab, under **My Work**, click **Tasks to do**.

4.  In the My Tasks list, open the task.

5.  In the **Activity** section of the task, enter comments or add attachments.

6.  Click **Mark as Complete**.


## Result

The matter task is completed and its state updates to Review. It sent to the matter owner for approval.
