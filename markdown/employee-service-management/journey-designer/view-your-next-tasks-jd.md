# View and update your next tasks

View your next tasks using the **Your next tasks** widget on the My journeys page to understand the next steps in your journey.

## Before you begin

Role required: employee

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Journeys** &gt; **My Journeys**.

2.  Your next tasks widget will show you the next ten tasks that need your attention.

3.  Select a task that you want to update.

4.  Select **Complete** if the task is complete or select **Mark as in-progress** if you are still working on the task.
