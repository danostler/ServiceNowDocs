# Exploring Health and Safety Core

Health and Safety Core enables the organizations to streamline and automate essential business functions across the Health and Safety applications.

## Health and Safety Core overview

The Health and Safety Core application provides the core date model for Health and Safety. This data models consists of common components and data structure to enable the functioning of related Health and Safety applications.

-   Health and Safety Workspace is used by safety agents to perform their safety tasks. All health and safety applications that are installed use the Health and Safety Workspace.
-   Health and Safety dashboard provides a centralized overview of your organization’s safety performance. It helps safety teams monitor, analyze, and manage key safety metrics such as incidents, injuries, risks, and corrective actions across various locations and time periods.
-   Health and Safety user profile provides safety teams access to all the information associated with health and safety records in a central location.
-   Safety teams can manage safety meetings and tasks such as scheduling a meeting or recording notes.
-   Report field mapping enables the safety agents to generate PDF reports for the records.
-   Health and Safety documents enable the safety teams to view documents associated with a safety record.
-   Health and Safety action scheduler helps to create scheduled actions across the organization.
-   Action Planner enables safety teams to view, create, and manage corrective and preventive actions on any Health and Safety record without navigating away from the page.
