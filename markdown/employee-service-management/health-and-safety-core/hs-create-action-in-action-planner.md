# Create and manage a safety action in the Action planner

Create and manage safety actions in context while reviewing a Health and Safety record, without opening a separate Action form.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ohs\_im.action\_writer

## About this task

With Action planner, you can manage actions in context while reviewing a Health and Safety record. The Action planner is available on any Health and Safety record that has the **Actions** tab.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Health and Safety Workspace**.

2.  Open a safety record such as a safety incident or a risk assessment.

3.  Select the Action Planner icon \[Omitted image "health-safety-action-planner.png"\] Alt text: Action Planner icon\) from the contextual sidebar panel.

    Any existing actions linked to this record are displayed as cards.

4.  In the Action planner sidebar, select **Add new action**.

5.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    For information on field descriptions, see [Health and Safety action form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-action-form.md).

6.  Select **Save**.

    The action is created and appears as a card in the **Created actions** section of the action planner.

7.  To edit or open an action separately, select an option in the **Created actions** section.

## Result

-   The action appears as a card in the **Created actions** section of the action planner.
-   The action also appears in the list of actions in the **Actions** tab of the associated Health and Safety record. It is also listed in the **Actions** list on Health and Safety Workspace.
