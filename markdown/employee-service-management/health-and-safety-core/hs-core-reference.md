# Health and Safety Core reference

Learn about the components, such as the roles, tables, plugins, and store applications, that are installed when the Health and Safety Core data model is installed.

-   **[Components installed with Health and Safety Core](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/components-installed-hs-core.md)**  
Several types of components are installed with installation of the Health and Safety Core data model, including tables, user roles, and scheduled jobs. This data model is automatically installed with Health and Safety applications.
-   **[Health and Safety action form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-action-form.md)**  
Safety agents can use the Health and Safety action form to assign an action for a safety incident or observation.
-   **[Health and safety action schedule form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-health-safety-action-schedule-form.md)**  
Safety team can use the action schedule form for actions to be created and assigned based on defined conditions.
-   **[Health and Safety profile form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-health-safety-profile-form.md)**  
Safety manager can use the health and safety profile form to assign the Health and Safety profile to employees, visitors, and contractor workers.
-   **[Health and Safety document version form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-document-form.md)**  
Safety team such as safety agent and contractor coordinator can use the Health and Safety document version form to add or modify a safety-related document.
-   **[Health and Safety report field mapping form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-report-field-mapping-form.md)**  
A safety report mapping manager can create a mapping report for generating reports using the report field mapping form.
-   **[Health and Safety meeting form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-safety-meeting-form.md)**  
A safety team such as a safety meeting manager can use the Health and Safety meeting form to add or modify a safety-related meeting.
-   **[Health and Safety meeting attendee form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-core/hs-safety-meeting-attendee-form.md)**  
A safety team such as a safety meeting manager can use the Health and Safety meeting form to add an external attendee or a single internal attendee to a safety meeting.
