# Manage scenarios using Space Planning

Create, copy, or delete space planning scenarios.

## Before you begin

To manage scenarios, verify that the following plugins are installed:

-   Workplace Core
-   Workplace Space Management
-   Indoor Mapping \(for map-based administration\)
-   Workplace Case Management
-   Workplace Central
-   Workplace Service Delivery components

Role required: sn\_wsd\_spcmgmt.space\_planner, sn\_wsd\_spcmgmt.scenario\_reader \(read only; to view a scenario\)

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Central** &gt; **Workplace Central**.

2.  On the side navigation, select the **Space Planning** module.

    The Space Planning workspace opens with the **Floor plan** tab selected by default.

3.  Select the **Scenarios** tab.

    The list of scenarios opens with details like their name, location, group by value, state, and owner.

4.  Manage the scenarios based on your requirement.
