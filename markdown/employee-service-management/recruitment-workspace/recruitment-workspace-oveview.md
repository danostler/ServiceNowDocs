# Get overview of recruitment workload

Recruiters can use the Recruitment workspace overview section to have enhanced visibility into their workload that prospects to effective prioritization of work.

Leverage the transparency in data visibility that helps prioritization of work. Use the overview section to manage your active, on hold, or unassigned requisitions.

You can view the requisitions by their respective states on the home page and act on it, as required.

As a recruiter:

-   View and prioritize your requisitions under **Assigned to me**.
-   Assign an unassigned requisition to yourself, another recruiter, or to an assignment group.

The overview section displays a number of **Active requisitions**, **Active job requisition requests by state**, **Requisitions - recruitment coordinator**, **On hold requisitions**, and **Unassigned requisitions**. It also shows the trend of the requisitions by state, whether the numbers have increased or decreased.

\[Omitted image "recruitment-dashboard.png"\] Alt text: Recruitment overview section shows the requisitions by states and their trends in a card view

Select any section to view all the related requisitions in it.

The requisitions are visible in card view and list view.

When you select a card, it opens the job requisition details page.

## Filter and sort options

Use the following filter and sort options to shift through the job requisitions.

-   Use **All managers** to filter by the hiring manager.
-   Use **All states** to filter by state of the job requisitions or view all.
-   Use **Sort by State** to sort the job requisition by date.

    All the job requisitions display the individual state of that requisition both in card and list views.

    -   To view the most recently updated job requisitions, choose **Updated \(newest to oldest\)**.
    -   To view the oldest updated job requisitions, choose **updated \(oldest to newest\)**.
    -   To view the latest job requisitions, which have been opened, choose **Opened date \(newest to oldest\)**.
    -   To view the oldest job requisitions, which have been opened, choose **Opened date \(oldest to newest\)**.

## Search

Use the search bar to search for a requisition.

You can view the requisition and its associated job applications.

## Application stages

View all the applications associated with a job requisition. The application data comes from the designated application tracking system \(ATS\) in your organization.
