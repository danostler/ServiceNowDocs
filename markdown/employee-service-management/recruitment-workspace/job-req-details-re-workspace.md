# Track a job requisition in the Recruitment workspace

Track applications on a job requisition, view job details, manage the hiring team, and add or remove interview phases.

See details of a job description and communicate with the hiring manager and the applicants.

The **Overview** tab is the landing page for a job requisition and has the following sections:

-   Cards show the count of applications in various states. Select any of the cards to show the list of applicants in that state.
-   The Applications section lists the applications on the job requisition. Select one or more applications and select **Request hiring manager response** to send an email notification to the hiring manager requesting recommendation for the applications.
-   The Hiring team section shows all the hiring team members for the job requisition. You can add or edit the hiring team members from here.
-   The Job details section shows the summary of the job requisition.

The **Details** tab provides all the detailed field values given at the time of job requisition request. Post messages in the Activity section to collaborate with the hiring manager and the approver for the requisition.

The **Job Description** tab shows the job description and the job requirement summary for the job requisition. You can modify the job description and send it for approval by selecting **Submit for approval**.

The **Hiring team** tab lists all the members who are part of the hiring team and their details. You can add or remove members from the hiring team by selecting **Add** or **Remove** respectively. You can also request for or view the scheduling preferences of the hiring team members by selecting **Request scheduling preferences** and **View scheduling preferences** respectively.

The **Interview phases** tab lists all the interview phases set up for the job requisition. You can add or remove interview phases from this tab by selecting **Add phase** or **Remove** respectively.

The **Approvers** tab shows the name of the approver and the state of the approval request for easy tracking.

-   You can view the approval details when the recruiter has submitted the requisition for approval.
-   You can see any comment that is added to the approval request by the approver.

The **Prospects** tab shows all the applicants for the job requisition. You can add applicants from here and also create prospects.
