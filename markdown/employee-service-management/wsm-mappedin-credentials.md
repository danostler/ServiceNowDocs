# Maintain Workplace Service Delivery Mappedin credentials

To configure and work with Mappedin ensure that you have a valid login credential. Work with the Mappedin to obtain these credentials.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_mappedin.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Space Mapping** &gt; **Mappedin Administration** &gt; **Credentials**.

2.  Click **Mappedin Credentials**.

    These credentials are the Client ID and Client Secret that enables you to render a map.

3.  Enter the information provided by Mappedin.

4.  Click **Update**.

5.  Click **Mappedin Export Credentials**.

    These credentials are the Client ID and Client Secret that enables you to export a map.

6.  Enter the information provided by Mappedin.

7.  Click **Update**.
