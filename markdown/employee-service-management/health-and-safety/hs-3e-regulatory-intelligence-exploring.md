# Explore 3E Regulatory Intelligence

3E Regulatory Intelligence is an API provided by 3E that works by retrieving regulatory list regulations from the 3E server to individual chemical substances.

When the regulatory list is retrieved and the sync is successful, the regulations are organized by regulatory list and displayed on the **Regulatory** tab of the substance record. This provides chemical managers with a consolidated view of applicable regulatory requirements, in-force dates, and conformance status.

For more information about the 3E Regulatory Intelligence API and subscription requirements, see [3E Regulatory Intelligence API](https://www.3eco.com/3e-solutions/product-stewardship/3e-regulatory-intelligence-api/#cta-form-block) on the 3E website.

## Benefits

With 3E Regulatory Intelligence you can obtain the following benefits:

-   Automatically retrieve and sync regulatory list data for chemicals and substances using CAS \(Chemical Abstracts Service\) number.
-   Consolidate regulatory obligations across multiple regulatory lists in a single view.
-   Track upcoming, newly applicable, and expired regulations across the chemical inventory.
-   Monitor regulatory conformance status at the substance, chemical, and chemical group level.
-   Reduce manual effort in sourcing and maintaining regulatory data for hazardous chemicals.
