# Configure the application registry for 3E Regulatory Intelligence

Configure 3E Regulatory Intelligence with Health and Safety Environmental Management to retrieve regulatory list data from 3E server and populate the Regulatory tab on chemical substance records.

## Before you begin

Verify that you have received the API credentials to integrate 3E database with Health and Safety Environmental Management. 3E service provider supplies these credentials to the organizations that have an account with them.

Role required: admin

## About this task

For more information about the 3E Regulatory Intelligence API and subscription requirements, see [3E Regulatory Intelligence API](https://www.3eco.com/3e-solutions/product-stewardship/3e-regulatory-intelligence-api/#cta-form-block) on the 3E website.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System OAuth** &gt; **Application Registry**.

2.  Search and select **3E Regulatory provider**.

3.  In the **Client ID** and **Client secret** field, enter the credentials provided by 3E service provider.

    These credentials are unique to your account with 3E service provider.

4.  Select **Update**.
