# Access safety knowledge articles through the Now Mobile app

Access your company's health and safety knowledge base on your mobile device using the ServiceNow Now Mobile app.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ohs\_im.requestor

## Procedure

1.  Open the Now Mobile app on your mobile device.

2.  In the navigation bar, tap **Safety**.

3.  Open the required knowledge article from one of these areas on the page.

    -   In the **Browse from all topics** section on the page, tap **Safety knowledge articles** and then tap the required knowledge article.
    -   In the **Safety knowledge articles** section on the page, tap the required knowledge article.
