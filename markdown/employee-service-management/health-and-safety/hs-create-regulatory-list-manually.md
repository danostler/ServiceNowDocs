# Create a regulatory list manually

If you don't have an integration with 3E, you can create a regulatory list manually to review regulatory requirements, in-force dates, and conformance status of the chemical substance.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hs\_chm.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Health and Safety** &gt; **Health and Safety Workspace**.

2.  Select **Environmental Management** \(\[Omitted image "icon-hs-envt-mgmt.png"\] Alt text: environmental management\) icon.

3.  Add regulations to the regulatory list.

5.  Select **Save**.


## Result

The regulatory list appears in the Regulations by regulatory list section of the **Regulatory** tab of the chemical substance.
