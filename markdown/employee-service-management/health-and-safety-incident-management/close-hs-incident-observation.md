# Close a safety incident or observation

Close a safety incident or observation assigned to you when you complete all activities for resolving it.

## Before you begin

Ensure that you've completed all related activities before closing the safety incident or observation.

Role required: sn\_ohs\_im.manager or sn\_ohs\_im.agent

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Health and Safety** &gt; **Health and Safety Workspace**.

2.  Select the incident management icon \(\[Omitted image "list-icon-hs.png"\] Alt text: Incident Management icon.\) and navigate to the **Lists** tab.

3.  Open a safety incident or observation to close.

    -   For the safety incidents list, select **Safety Incidents** and then **Assigned to me**.
    -   For the safety observations list, select **Safety Observations** and then **Assigned to me**.
4.  Select the safety incident or observation to close.

5.  Close the incident or observation.

    -   For a safety incident, select **Close incident** and then select **Resolve**.
    -   For an observation, select **Close observation** and then **Confirm**.

## Result

The incident or observation moves to the Closed Complete state.
