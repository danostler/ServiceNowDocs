# Health and safety incident form

Safety managers and safety agents can use the Health and Safety incident form to create an incident record or to work on a safety incident.

For more information, see [Work on a safety incident](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/health-and-safety-incident-management/work-hs-incident-observation.md).
