# Access the Applicant Center

Log in to the Applicant Center without a password and explore the portal easily.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.external\_applicant or sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.internal\_applicant

## About this task

An applicant can log in to the Applicant Center without the need for a password to make the process simpler and more accessible. Applicants receiving an invitation to log in to the Applicant Center is a base system behaviour. You can determine at which state, the email is sent to the applicant's registered email address with the magic link.

## Procedure

1.  Go to your registered email address and open the email notification.

2.  Select **View Applicant Center**.

    The magic link takes you to the Applicant Center home page.

3.  If the link expires, select **Send a new invite** from the pop-up window prompt.

    \[Omitted image "applicant-new-link-invite.png"\] Alt text: Pop-up window displays login is expired with a magic link re-generation link

4.  In the **Email** field, enter your registered email address.

5.  A new log in email is sent to the registered email address.

6.  Select **View Applicant Center** to re-login to the Applicant Center.
