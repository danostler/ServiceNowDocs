# Request to reschedule interview

As an applicant, request to reschedule a proposed or scheduled interview to the recruiter.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.external\_applicant or sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.internal\_applicant

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to Applicant Center.

    For more information, see [Access the Applicant Center](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/applicant-center/view-applicant-center.md).

2.  On the Applicant Center home page, under the Keep track of what’s going on section, locate the proposed or scheduled interview.

3.  Select **Request reschedule**.

4.  On the Reschedule interview form, fill in the fields.

    For a description of the field values, see [Request reschedule form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/applicant-center/request-reschedule-applicant-interviewer-form.md).

5.  Select **Send**.
