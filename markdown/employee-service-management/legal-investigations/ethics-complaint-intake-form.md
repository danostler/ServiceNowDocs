# Ethics complaints intake form

Employees can use the Ethics Complaints intake form to report an ethics violation incident.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Complaint type|Type of complaint or allegation that violated the ethics policy of the organisation.|
|Detailed description|Detailed information about the incident, such as where and when it occurred.|
|Involved parties|Users who are the subject of the complaint.|
|Provide the approximate date and time of the incident|Tentative date when the incident occurred.|

Add involved parties from the Add Row dialog box that appears by clicking the **Add** button in the **Involved Parties** section of the Ethics Complaints form.
