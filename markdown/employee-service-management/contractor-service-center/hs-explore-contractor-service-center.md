# Exploring Contractor Service Center

The Contractor Service Center is a centralized hub to manage and support contractors. It helps streamline contractor workflow, improve efficiency, and provide secure and high-quality functioning of various safety tasks.

## Contractor Service Center overview

This application is a self-service center where contractors can complete any tasks that are assigned to them by the safety contractor coordinator. They can perform various safety tasks such as report a Health and Safety incident, request permit to work, upload documents, and create JSAs. They have easy access to site safety tools, protocols, and documentations.

## Contractor Service Center users

|User|Description|
|----|-----------|
|Contractor|Uses the contractor service center to complete pre-qualification tasks or submit documents.|

## Contractor Service Center workflow

The contractor begins by using the link provided in the onboarding email to access the Contractor Service Center. Using the username and password included in the same email, the contractor logs into the system. Upon first login, the contractor is prompted to update their password for security purposes. Once logged in, the contractor can view and complete all tasks assigned by the safety contractor coordinator, including uploading any required documents. Additionally, the contractor can use the self-service features within the Contractor Service Center to create and submit driving requests as needed.
