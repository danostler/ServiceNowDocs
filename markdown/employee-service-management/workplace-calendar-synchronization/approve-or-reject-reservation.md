# Approve a reservation

Review employee reservations raised using Workplace Reservation Management and approve or reject them.

## Before you begin

Role required: approver\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **My Approvals**.

2.  Select the reservation that you want to approve or reject.

3.  Review the details of the reservation.

4.  Approve or reject the reservation by selecting Approve or Reject.

    **Note:** If approval for a reservation is rejected, a delete request is sent to the room calendar to release the room.
