# Edit existing Journey Accelerator plans

Edit existing Journey Accelerator plans by adding, removing, or rearranging stages and tasks to meet the needs of the employee and your team.

## Before you begin

Role required: All employees with direct reports

## Procedure

1.  In the Employee Center portal, click **Plans**.

2.  Click the plan that you want to edit.

3.  Update, move, or delete stages or to-dos.

    When you're done, click **Exit editing**.

    **Note:**

    -   Deleting tasks or stages permanently removes them from the plan.
    -   Plans immediately update with your saved edits.
