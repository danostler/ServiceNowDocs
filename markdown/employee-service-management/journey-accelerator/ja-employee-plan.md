# Employees view and complete action plans

View, manage, and track your action plan progress from the Employee Center portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: Any employee assigned to a plan has access to the plan. You can be assigned as the employee or mentor.

## About this task

Your manager creates plans for different employee transition events. When you can access your plan from the Employee Center portal, you see only your assigned tasks if your plan is in the Ready or Complete state.

**Note:** Tasks in your plan are also listed under To-dos. The To-dos list does not show the stage that contains the task.

## Procedure

1.  In the Employee Center portal, click **Plans**.

2.  Click the plan that you want to work on.

    Stages contain the tasks for you to complete.

3.  To view details about a particular task and to manage its progress, click that task.

    You can manually update the status of a task and add additional details when needed. The different statuses are as follows:

    -   **Mark as in progress**: Updates that record for the task so that others can see that you started working on the task.
    -   **Complete**: Shows that you completed the task.
    -   **Comments**: Sends messages to the plan manager.
    -   **Attachments**: Adds files, such as certificates of completion, for training or other documents.
