# Monitor Journey Accelerator plans

Monitor plans and to-do tasks from the Journey Accelerator dashboard.

## Before you begin

Role required: Reader \[sn\_ja.reader\]

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Journey Accelerator** &gt; **Journey Accelerator Dashboard**.

2.  Click the items in the dashboard for more details.

    Here is some of the information you can see about Journey Accelerator plans and to-dos.

    -   Overdue Plan To-dos
    -   Plans in Draft State
    -   Plans in Ready State
    -   Plans Published in Last 6 Months
    -   To-dos Created in Last 6 Months - From Template \(True\) Vs. Plan Created \(False\)
