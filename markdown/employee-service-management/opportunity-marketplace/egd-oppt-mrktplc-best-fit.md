# Opportunity Marketplace best fit recommendations

Based on your current roles, aspirations, and skills Opportunity Marketplace \(OPM\) recommends opportunities for you.

## Based on your current role

OPM looks at your current role and skills then compares it to the available published opportunities.

Within an opportunity you can see more information related to your role and what is used in making the recommendation. Select **Match analysis** to view details about how you were matched to the opportunity. Select **View details** to view information about the opportunity.

## Based on your aspirations

OPM reviews any aspirations that you created in Talent Development and identifies potential opportunities that match with your aspirations.

## Based on your skills

The OPM recommender looks at the skills that are in your profile to compare your skills needed for the opportunities.
