# Internal jobs

Opportunity Marketplace enable you to create, publish, and maintain internal job posting. You can explore open roles \(positions\) within your company.

Recommendations are based on your current role \(job position\), aspirations, and skills.

Internal jobs are tracked in the Job Posting \[sn\_ta\_hiring\_core\_job\_posting\] table. You can view the best match analysis based on your current role, aspirations, or skills.

Artificial Intelligence \(AI\) is used in search to make sure the most relevant opportunities are returned.
