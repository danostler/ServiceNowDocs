# View and manage role applicants from Opportunity Marketplace

Role applicants are employees who have applied to a role that is part of an opportunity. As an Opportunity Marketplace \[sn\_opp\_market.admin\], you can view and manage role applicant records.

## Before you begin

Role required: The creator or owner of the opportunity.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Opportunity Marketplace** &gt; **Role Applicants**.

    The role applicant records contain several fields. Here are some of the fields that help you to identify a role applicant. The listed fields are linked to their respective records for the applicant.

<table id="table_uk1_5gw_jcc"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

**Employee**

</td><td>

The names of the applicants links to their **Employee Profile**, see [Employee Profile table](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/employee-profile.md).

</td></tr><tr><td>

**Role**

</td><td>

A role that is listed in an opportunity. Opportunities can 1 or more roles depending on the opportunity type.

</td></tr><tr><td>

**State**

</td><td>

The **State** of a role applicant indicates the progression of the applicant in the process.-   **New applicant**: A new applicant that hasn’t been looked at by the opportunity owner.
-   **Pending review** The opportunity owner has not yet viewed the applicant's information.
-   **Accepted** An applicant that has been accepted for the role in an opportunity.
-   **Declined** An applicant that has been declined for a role.
-   **Canceled** An applicant that is no longer part of the opportunity role review process.


</td></tr></tbody>
</table>2.  Select the field that you want to update.

3.  Select the green check mark to apply and save your changes.
