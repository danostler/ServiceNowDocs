# Sign up \(apply\) for an opportunity

Apply for an opportunity from the opportunity details page.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

**Note:** Access is based on user criteria.

## About this task

Once you find an opportunity that you’re interested in, you must sign up and apply for the opportunity.

## Procedure

1.  From the Employee Center go to **Opportunity marketplace** &gt; **Other opportunities**.

    Look at the opportunities on the **Other opportunities** page to find the opportunity role you want to apply for.

    **Tip:** Select the heart next to the vertical ellipses to add the opportunity to **Your Favorites** tab in the in the Employee Center portal.

2.  Select **View details** for the opportunity role that you’re interested in applying for.

    Opportunities with multiple roles don't display the **View details** button. You must open the opportunity and select **View details** for a role in the opportunity.

3.  From the role details page, select **Sign up**.

4.  Fill in the form to apply for the opportunity role.
