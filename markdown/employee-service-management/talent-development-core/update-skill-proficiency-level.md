# Update your skill proficiency level

Update and track your proficiency level for each skill that you list in your skills profile by using the Skills Foundation application.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_skills\_int.emp

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  In the Employee Center, navigate to **Profile** &gt; **Career** tab.

3.  Select the Skills widget.

4.  On the skills pill, select the menu icon \[Omitted image "icon-menu.png"\] Alt text: Shortcut menu.

5.  Select **Change skill proficiency**.

6.  Move the horizontal slider to adjust your skill proficiency level.

    Moving the slider to the right-hand side increases the proficiency level, and moving it to the left-hand side decreases the proficiency level.

7.  Select **Update**.


## Result

Your skill proficiency level is successfully updated in your skills profile.
