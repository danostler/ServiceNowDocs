# View achievement credential templates

View achievement credential templates in Achievements and Credentials.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lep.achievement\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Achievements and Credentials** &gt; **Achievement Credential Templates**.

2.  Select an existing record.

3.  On the achievement credential template page, view the details stored in the following format.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Name|Name of the credential template.|
    |Credential type|Information about the type of credential.|
    |Source|Source of the credential. For example: Credly.|
    |Issuer|Details of the issuer of the credential template.|
    |Image URL|URL of the credential source.|
    |Achievement template skills|Skills attached to the template. To attach a new skill to the template, see .|
