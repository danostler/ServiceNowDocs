# View and work on delegated legal duties

Delegate legal duties relating to your assigned legal requests, legal matters, legal matter tasks, and approvals to another user when you might not have time or are unavailable.

## Before you begin

Role required: legal\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  Click the list icon \(\[Omitted image "lsd-lcc-list-icon.png"\] Alt text: List icon\).

3.  In the **Delegate to me**, click a list item to see records assigned to the user who delegated you their duties.

    -   Approvals: Opens a list of all delegated approvals for legal requests, legal matters, and legal matter tasks
    -   Requests: Opens a list of all delegated legal requests
    -   Matters: Opens a list of all delegated legal matters
    -   Matter Tasks: Opens a list of all delegated legal matter tasks
4.  Open a record from the list to view and work on it.
