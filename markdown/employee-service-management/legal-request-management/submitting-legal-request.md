# Using Legal Request Management

Employees can submit a legal request to seek services from the legal department and track its status.

## Overview of using Legal Request Management

To submit and track legal requests refer to the following tasks.

-   **[Submit a legal request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/submit-legal-request.md)**  
Submit a legal request for yourself or on behalf of another user to seek services from the legal department.
-   **[View and track legal request details](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/view-legal-request-details.md)**  
View the details of a legal request after it has been submitted and track request activities.
-   **[Update a submitted legal request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/update-legal-request.md)**  
Update request details, add comments, or upload documents in a legal request you submitted while it is still in the New or Assigned state. You can cancel a submitted request irrespective of its state.
-   **[Reopen a closed legal request](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/reopen-legal-request-requester.md)**  
Reopen a request if you're not satisfied with the resolution given by the legal team.
-   **[Managing legal requests](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/legal-request-management/managing-legal-request.md)**  
As a member of the legal department, you work on legal requests submitted by employees to resolve their issues or to answer their queries.
