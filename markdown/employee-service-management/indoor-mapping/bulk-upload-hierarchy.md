# Bulk hierarchy upload

Use the Bulk hierarchy upload feature to import campus, building, and floor details by uploading Excel or CSV files.

As a map admin, you can use an Excel template to provide information like campus names, building names, floor details, and coordinates for multiple locations. After you upload the information, the system checks for errors and you can fix and reupload the file.

After uploading the hierarchical data, you can upload floor plans for all the locations. The locations are then displayed on a map based on the uploaded coordinates.
