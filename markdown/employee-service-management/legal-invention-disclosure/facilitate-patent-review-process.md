# Facilitate the patent review process

Update the tracking information through the final resolution of the patent review process after you receive the resolution of the patent application review by an outside counsel.

## Before you begin

An email from outside counsel with the decision on patent filing has been received.

Role required: sn\_lg\_ip.patent\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  Based on the resolution of the patent application review by the outside counsel, either close the request or indicate that the patent was filed.
