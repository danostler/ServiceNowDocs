# Legal Invention Disclosure form

List of fields in the Legal Invention Disclosure request in the Legal Service Portal.

<table id="table_sjz_dhn_fyb"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Requester name

</td><td>

Name of the requester. The name is auto-populated with the current requester name but can be modified if requesting for another user.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Inventors

</td><td>

Names and details of inventors.

</td></tr><tr><td>

What title would you like to use to describe your invention

</td><td>

Title of the invention.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Briefly describe what problem your invention solves

</td><td>

Description of the issue that the invention will help to resolve.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Describe the invention

</td><td>

Detailed description of the invention.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Approximate date of conception of invention

</td><td>

Date when the invention was conceived. The date must be either the current date or earlier.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Invention publicly disclosed?

</td><td>

Indicates whether the invention has been disclosed publicly. If the choice is **Yes**, additional fields request detailed information about the disclosure.-   **Invention disclosure method** - How the invention was publicly disclosed.
-   **First public disclosure date** - Date when the first public disclosure of the invention was made.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Invention was implemented into a customer product or is planned on being implemented into a customer product?

</td><td>

Indicates whether the invention is already used anywhere. If the choice is **Yes**, additional fields request detailed information about the implementation.-   **Past or future date of release of the product** - Past or future date when the product releases.
-   **Specify the name of the product** - Name of the product where the invention is used.

</td></tr></tbody>
