# Legal Content Review form

List of fields in the Legal Content Review request in the Legal Service Portal.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Description|Description of the Legal Content Review request.|
|Content owner|The owner of the Legal Content Review request.|
|Content type|Select from the list of content type from the drop-down list.|
|Content link|Provide any link to the content, if available.|
|Purpose|Purpose of the Legal Content Review request.|
|Requested approval date|Approval date for the Legal Content Review request.|
|Add attachments|Attachments to add to the request.|
