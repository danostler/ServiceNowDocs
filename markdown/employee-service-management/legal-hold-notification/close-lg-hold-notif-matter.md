# Close a legal hold matter

Close a legal hold matter once the legal hold is officially lifted.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_hold.legal\_hold\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Legal Request** &gt; **Legal Counsel Center**.

2.  Select the List icon \(\[Omitted image "lsd-lcc-list-icon.png"\] Alt text: List icon\).

3.  In the **Default lists** tab, navigate to **Matters** &gt; **All**

4.  Select the legal hold matter that you want to close.

5.  Select **Close complete**.

6.  Enter your comments in the Close complete matter pop-up window.

7.  Select **OK**.


## Result

All the custodians receive the mail stating that the legal hold is released.

The message **Legal matter has been closed** appears in the **Activity** section.

In the legal hold matter page, the state changes from Work in progress to Closed Complete.
