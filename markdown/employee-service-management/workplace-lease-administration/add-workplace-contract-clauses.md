# Add workplace contract clauses in Core UI

Add workplace contract clauses, including insurance clause, parking clause, warranty clauses to store the lease contract clauses.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_la.manager or sn\_wsd\_la.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Lease Administration** &gt; **Workplace Contracts**.

2.  Select a contract.

3.  In the **Workplace Contract Clauses** related list, select **New**.

4.  On the Workplace Contract Clause form, fill in the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Clause type|Type of clause.|
    |Workplace contract|Workplace contract reference.|
    |Silent|Yes or No|
    |Clause summary|Description of the clause.|

5.  Select **Submit**.


## Result

The contract clauses are added to the contract.
