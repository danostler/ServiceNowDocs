# Add a workplace contract service to a contract in Core UI

Workplace contract services are the common services offered as part of the lease contract. You can add and track the services along with their associated costs.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_la.manager or sn\_wsd\_la.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Lease Administration** &gt; **Workplace Contracts**.

2.  Select the contract.

3.  In the **Workplace Contract Services** related list, select **New**

4.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Name|Name of the contract service.|
    |Cost|The currency and cost of the contract service.|
    |Workplace Contract|The contract name is auto-filled.|
    |Description|Description of the contract service.|
    |Domain|The domain for the service.|

5.  If the contract service type you want does not appear in the **Workplace Contract Service type** field drop-down list, create it.

    1.  Right-click **Category** and select **Configure choices**.

    2.  On the Configure Category Choices form, in the **Enter a new item field**, enter the name of the category that you want to add.

    3.  Select **Add**.

6.  Select the desired category from the **Workplace Contract Service type** field drop-down list.

7.  Select **Submit**.
