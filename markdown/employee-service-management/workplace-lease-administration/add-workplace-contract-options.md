# Add workplace contract options in Core UI

Add workplace contract options such as option to renew, option to sub lease, option to terminate to store the lease contract options.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wsd\_la.manager or sn\_wsd\_la.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workplace Lease Administration** &gt; **Workplace Contracts**.

2.  Select a contract.

3.  In the **Workplace Contract Options** related list, select **New**.

4.  On the Workplace Contract Option form, fill in the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Option number|Number indicating the contract option.|
    |Option type|Type of option.|
    |Workplace contract|Workplace contract reference.|
    |Effective date|Date when the option becomes effective.|
    |Must exercise by|Date by when the option can be exercised by the tenant.|
    |Renewal expiration|Renewal term expiration date.|
    |Notice period|Notice period to exercise an option|
    |Comment|Description of the contract option.|

5.  Select **Submit**.


## Result

The contract options are added to the contract.
