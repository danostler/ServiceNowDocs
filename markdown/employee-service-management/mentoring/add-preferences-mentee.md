# Add mentee preferences

As a mentee, add your preferences to find the right mentor to guide you through your career.

## Before you begin

Enroll as a mentee. For more information, see [Enroll as a mentor or mentee](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/mentoring/enroll-mentoring.md)

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  On the mentee preferences wizard, answer the required questions.

2.  Use the **Search skills** option to add skills that a mentor can help you with.

    To add skills, see [Search and add skills](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/mentoring/search-add-skills-mentoring.md).

3.  Select **Next**.

4.  On the **What are you looking for from your mentor?** question, set preferences that will help you match with the right mentor.

    **Note:** Select at least one preference.

6.  On the **What are some of your other mentor preferences** question, add or remove options as per your choice.

    |Other preferences|
    |-----------------|
    |Which department you want your mentor to be from?|Option to add the department you want your mentor from.|
    |Which roles are you looking for your mentor to be in?|Option to search for mentors in particular roles.|
    |Which timezones work best for you?|Option to add appropriate timezones.|
    |Which locations work best for you?|Option to add an appropriate location to meet with your mentor.|
    |What languages do you communicate in?|Option to select preferred languages.|

7.  Select **Enroll**.

    **Note:** You can also save your preferences by select **Save as draft**, if you are not ready to enroll as a mentee yet.
