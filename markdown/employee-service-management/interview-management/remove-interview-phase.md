# Remove interview phase

As a recruiter, remove interview phases that you had set up once they aren’t needed anymore.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Recruitment workspace**.

2.  Select the job requisition that you want to remove interview phases from.

3.  On the **Interview phases** tab, select the interview phase you want to remove.

4.  Select **Remove**.

5.  On the confirmation window, select **Remove**.
