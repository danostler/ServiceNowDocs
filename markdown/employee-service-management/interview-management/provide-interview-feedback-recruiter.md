# Provide interview feedback

As a recruiter or recruitment coordinator, provide feedback for interviews that you're part of.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter or sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruitment\_coordinator

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Recruitment workspace**.

2.  Select the required job requisition.

3.  Find the applicant and interview.

    1.  In the **Overview** tab, under the Applications section, select the applicant with whom the interview is scheduled.

    2.  On the application record page, select the **Job interviews** tab.

    3.  Select the required interview record.

    4.  On the interview record page, select the **Feedback** tab.

    5.  Select the feedback record assigned to you.

    You can also select the feedback record from the **Interview feedback** tab on the application record page.

4.  If the interview was completed, do the following.

    1.  From the **Rating** drop-down list, select a rating for the applicant.

    2.  In the **Comments** field, add the interview feedback details.

    3.  In the **Private notes** field, enter any notes that you'd like to take for yourself about the interview.

        These notes are only visible to you. They are also available after submitting the feedback.

    4.  Save or submit the feedback.

        -   To save the feedback as a draft and submit it later, select **Save**
        -   To submit and share the feedback with the recruiter and hiring manager, select **Submit feedback**.
5.  If the interview didn't happen either because you or the applicant couldn't attend it, or for any other reason, then do the following.

    1.  Select **Interview incomplete**.

    2.  From the **Reason** drop-down list, select why the interview didn't happen.

    3.  In the **Comment** field, enter the details to support the reason, if any.

    4.  Select **Submit**.
