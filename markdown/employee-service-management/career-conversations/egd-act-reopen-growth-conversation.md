# Reopen a closed growth conversation

Use the Career Conversations app to reopen a closed growth conversation to modify talking points and action items.

## Before you begin

You must provide your employees with a license to enable access to the Career Conversations app. For more information about employee licensing, refer to [License Talent Development Core users](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/talent-development-core/egd-employee-profile-licensing.md).

Role required: manager \[sn\_egd\_act.manager\]

## About this task

When a conversation is closed, either manually by a manager or automatically by the system, it transitions into a read-only mode. In this state, both employees and managers can review the talking points and action items, but they cannot make any modifications. To continue the discussion or update any details, the conversation must be reopened by a manager. This ensures that all changes are authorized and maintains the integrity of the conversation history. Once reopened, the conversation becomes editable, allowing the manager and employees to add new points, update action items, or make any necessary adjustments.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  From the Employee Center, navigate to **Your team** &gt; **Conversations**.

3.  Use the **Your team's conversation** widget to filter by **Past** conversations and select the conversation that you want to reopen.

4.  Click **Reopen conversation**.

    \[Omitted image "reopen-growth-conversation.png"\] Alt text: A completed conversation between an employee and a manager with the option to reopen the conversation


## What to do next

Modify the action items, notes, and skills, then close the conversation when you are done.
