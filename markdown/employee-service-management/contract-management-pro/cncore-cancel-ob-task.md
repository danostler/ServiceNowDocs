# Cancel an obligation task

Cancel an open obligation task in Obligation Management that is no longer required.

## About this task

**Note:** Obligation tasks that are not submitted or awaiting approval are automatically canceled when you deactivate the parent obligation record.

## Before you begin

The task to be cancelled must be in Open, Submitted, or Awaiting approval state.

You must be the group manager of the approver group or the approver of the obligation task.

Role required: sn\_cm\_obligation.obligation\_fulfiller

## Procedure

1.  Open an obligation task from the workspace that you are using.
