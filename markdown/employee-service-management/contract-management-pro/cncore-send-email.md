# Send email to stakeholders

As a contract fulfiller, set up an email to stakeholders to request review of the contract document reviewed and confirm the changes.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_cm\_core.contract\_fulfiller

## About this task

The option to compose an email is available only when the State is Awaiting approval, Awaiting signature, Contract signed or Work in progress.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to your workspace.

2.  Open the contract request.

3.  Select the More Actions \[Omitted image "more-actions-move-schedule.png"\] Alt text: More actions icon icon.

4.  Select **Compose email**.

5.  In the Compose email pop-up, select the documents to be attached

6.  In the **New Email Draft** tab, add recipients' email addresses in the **To**, **CC**, and **BCC** fields as appropriate.

7.  Enter the subject of the email in the **Subject** field.

    By default, the short description of the request is populated in the Subject field.

8.  Compose an email body.

## Result

After the email is sent, it is added to the activity stream on the request form. Emails you receive from others are also added to the activity stream.

To see a list of available default email notifications, go to **All** &gt; **System Notifications** &gt; **Notifications** and search for the **Contracts Support** category.
