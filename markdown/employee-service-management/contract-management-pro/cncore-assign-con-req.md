# Assign a contract request

As a contract fulfiller or contract administrator, assign a contract request to yourself or to someone in your assignment group.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_cm\_core.contract\_fulfiller, sn\_cm\_core.contract\_admin

## About this task

When a contract user submits a request, it is automatically assigned to a group or user as per the default assignment rule. If the request is assigned to a contract support group, any member from the group can assign the contract request to themselves or assign it to another member of the group.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to your workspace.

2.  Open the contract request.

3.  In the list of unassigned requests, open a request by clicking the request number.

4.  Assign the request to yourself or to another member from the assigned group.

    -   To assign the request to yourself, click **Assign to me**.
    -   To assign the request to another member, on the **Details** tab, select a user in the **Assigned to** field and click **Save**.
