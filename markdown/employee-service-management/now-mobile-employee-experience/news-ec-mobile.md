# News in Now Mobile

News articles are available in Now Mobile to provide easy access to consumable content, on-the-go.

Get the news experience on mobile with Employee Center on the Now Mobile app. View news articles on-the-go on your phone.

View news articles from the Featured news section or go to the **News center** tab. Scroll through the Featured news carousel to view all the featured articles on the News center MESP page.

You can select any news article to see the entire article open on the news details page. Scroll down to read the entire article on your phone. The news details page shows when the article was last updated. It also displays the **Favorites** icon \[Omitted image "favorite-icon.png"\] Alt text: Heart-shaped Favorites icon. to add it to your list of favorite articles.

\[Omitted image "news-nm.jpg"\] Alt text: News center tab highlighted within a red square box at the bottom of the news center page in Now Mobile.

If you want to browse the articles by categories, you can use the Categories section at the bottom of the news details page. All the categories associated to that article are displayed at the bottom of the page. You can also access all the categories at the bottom of the News center page, to search articles by categories. Use **Show more** to load more categories listed on the page.

You can associate a news article to a topic page to see it on the Related news section of that topic page in mobile.

Apart from the news articles, also access videos and calendar from the **News Center** tab.

For more information on news articles on the Employee Center portal, see [News articles](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/ec-news-articles.md).
