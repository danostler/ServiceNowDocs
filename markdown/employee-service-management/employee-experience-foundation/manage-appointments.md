# Manage your appointments

Update or cancel upcoming appointments or provide feedback for completed appointments.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  From **My active items**, select **Appointments**.

3.  Select an **Appointment category** and **Appointment state** to filter the list of appointments.

4.  Select an appointment to open the Appointment details page.

5.  From the context menu, select an option based on your requirement.

    **Note:** You can perform these actions from the Appointment list page or the Appointment details page.
