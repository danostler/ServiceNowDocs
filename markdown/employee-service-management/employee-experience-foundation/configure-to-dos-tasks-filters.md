# Create filters or filter groups

With these filters and filter groups, you enable employees to easily filter, organize, discover, and fulfill the tasks in a few clicks.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_sp.esc\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **To-dos filters**.

2.  Click **New** or open a record.

3.  Fill in the fields on the form.

## What to do next

Proceed to associate the categories, filters, and conditions from the related list. From the related list, select category for the filter. Select a category from the list available from the picker dialog box.

**Note:** Associate a filter to only one category or filter group.

If you do not select any specific category or filter group, the filter is not visible on the **My tasks** page.

Complete the following mapping per your requirement.

-   For filter group, map **Associated categories** and **Filters**. Also, go to the **Associated filters** related list, click **New**, and fill in the form to add more filters.
-   For filters, map **Associated categories** and **Associated conditions**. For sub-filters, map only **Associated conditions** as you cannot map a sub-filter to a category.

Based on your mapping, you make the filters available for employees to use on the **My tasks** page.
