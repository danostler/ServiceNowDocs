# Connect content to topic pages with the topic list selector

Get a uniform Service Catalog item-to-topic association experience with the topic list selector on Employee Center.

## Before you begin

Role required: catalog\_builder\_editor, taxonomy\_admin

User criteria required: Taxonomy Manager, Taxonomy Contributor, Topic Manager, Topic Contributor.

## About this task

You can associate any Service Catalog item to multiple topic pages using the topic selector component. The feature enables easy content-to-topic association for the Catalog Builder.

**Note:** The topic list selector feature is available for customers starting with Australia release.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Catalog** &gt; **Catalog Builder** and click **Create a new catalog item**.

2.  Click **Continue** on the getting started window.

3.  Choose a Service Catalog item template and click **use this item template**.

4.  Go to **Location** on the create record producer window.

5.  Click **Edit selected topics** on the **Topics** card to add or remove topic pages associated to the Service Catalog item.

    -   Select the **Taxonomy** and **Available topics** from the list to associate with your selected Service Catalog item.
    -   Drill down until eight levels of sub-tree topics to associate with your Service Catalog item.

## Result

-   Choose multiple topics altogether in any active taxonomy to associate with a Service Catalog item.
-   Add up to eight levels of sub-tree topics to the Service Catalog item.
