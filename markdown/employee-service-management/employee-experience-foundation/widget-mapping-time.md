# Customize the time off widget

Customize the time off widget on the Employee Profile to display it to your preference.

## Before you begin

The kiosk data display for the **Time off** tab is location-based. You can customize it for different users based on their locations. For more information see, [Customize data display in Kiosks](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/create-implementation-kiosk.md).

Role required: esc\_admin/sn\_ex\_sp\_pro\_kiosk.user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Profile** &gt; **Profile Tabs**.

2.  Select the **Time off** from the list of tabs.

    **Note:** The **Time off** tab is available in the base system.

3.  In the Tab Widget mapping section, select **Time off** to open the record.

    You can edit the **widget\_title** within the **Widget Parameters**.


## Result

The **Time off** tab is visible on your Employee Profile.
