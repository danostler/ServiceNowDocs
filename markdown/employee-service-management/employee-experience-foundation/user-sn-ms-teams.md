# Use ITSM and HRSD integrations with Microsoft Teams

Employees and agents can collaborate to receive service and resolve issues effectively from Microsoft Teams.

-   **[Employee actions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/employee-sn-ms-teams.md)**  
Respond to the approval notifications and the comments against the service-related tickets.
-   **[Agent actions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/agent-sn-ms-teams.md)**  
Agents can work on the service-related tickets created by the employees.
