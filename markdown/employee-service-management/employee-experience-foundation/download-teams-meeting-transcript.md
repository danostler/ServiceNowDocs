# Download a Microsoft Teams meeting transcript

Download a Microsoft Teams meeting transcript from a ServiceNow record to view the meeting content offline.

## Before you begin

Role required: Any role with read access to the record containing the conference call

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the record that contains the conference call, such as an incident.

2.  In the Collaborate side panel, locate the conference call section.

3.  In the Call transcript section, select **Download transcript**.
