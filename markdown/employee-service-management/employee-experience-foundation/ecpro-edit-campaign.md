# Editing a published campaign

If you need to make changes to a published campaign, review this information to understand your options. Editing a campaign pauses the campaign; any content that is not yet available is suspended until the campaign is published again. The campaign, campaign bundles, and campaign content are editable.

Review the section that applies to your situation:

<table id="table_fdx_3td_kxb"><thead><tr><th>

Type of edit

</th><th>

Information

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Edit or add new campaign content

</td><td>

Add new content and modify existing content while the campaign is visible to the audience. Click **Edit Campaign**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Edit campaign stage/bundle

</td><td>

Edit the stage/bundle name and details such as start time. Click **Edit Campaign**.**Note:** You cannot change the trigger type of published campaign bundle.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Add new campaign stage/bundle

</td><td>

Add a new campaign stage/bundle to a published campaign. Click **Edit Campaign**

</td></tr><tr><td>

Add or remove users from the audience

</td><td>

Modify the campaign audience or refine the audience for specific content. Click **Edit Campaign**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Pause a campaign

</td><td>

Pause a campaign to suspend undelivered content. After you publish the campaign, the changed content is delivered to users who did not see the originally published content. Click **Edit Campaign**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Cancel a campaign

</td><td>

Cancelling a campaign deactivates undelivered content. Click **Cancel Campaign** from the Campaign page.

</td></tr></tbody>
</table>
