# Create a content category

Create a content category to tag news articles.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_cd.content\_admin

## About this task

Content categories organize news articles and make them easier to find. When you create a content category, the system creates a page in the News Center to display all the news articles tagged with that category. A news article can be tagged with one or more content categories.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Content Publishing** &gt; **Re-usable Components** &gt; **Content Categories**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  Fill in the following fields:
