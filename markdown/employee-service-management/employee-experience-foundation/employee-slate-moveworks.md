# Employee Slate for Moveworks

Employees search and take action across ServiceNow workflows and connected business applications from one AI front door on web and mobile.

Employee Slate for Moveworks combines the Moveworks AI assistant with key Employee Slate features. These features include the conversation-first home page, the interactive split view, Enterprise Search, and the Inbox. You start any request from one chat bar and complete the request in the same panel, working across ServiceNow workflows and connected business applications without switching tools, opening separate forms, or filing tickets.

## What employees can do with Moveworks

From the chat bar on any Employee Slate page, employees complete the following workflows in one conversation:

-   Update personal HR records, such as home address and emergency contact, in the connected system of record.
-   Research prospects and topics by combining internal knowledge with an opt-in external large language model.
-   Generate pre-populated documents, such as an NDA, and send the document through a connected signing service.
-   Search enterprise content using AI summaries and filtering options, then transfer search context to the AI assistant for additional questions and actions.
-   Search enterprise content with an AI-generated summary, filters for source, date, and type, and a handoff to the AI assistant.
-   Act on notifications — approvals, announcements, and ticket status updates — in one panel.
-   Continue the same conversations on a mobile browser.

For an end-to-end walkthrough of a sample day, see [Employee Slate for Moveworks experience journey](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/empworks-experience-journey.md).
