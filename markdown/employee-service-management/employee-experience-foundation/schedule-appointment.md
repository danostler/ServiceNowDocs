# Schedule an appointment

Schedule a virtual or in-person appointment by selecting a reason and providing your contact information.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  You can schedule an appointment from the following entry points.

    Some entry points might not be available based on the configuration set by the admin.

3.  On the Schedule appointment form, fill in the fields.

    For a description of the field values, see [Schedule appointment form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/schedule-appointment-form.md)

4.  Select the check box to agree to receive messages and updates about your appointment.

5.  Select the check box to update the contact information in your profile.

6.  Enter additional details about your appointment.

7.  Select **Schedule appointment**.
