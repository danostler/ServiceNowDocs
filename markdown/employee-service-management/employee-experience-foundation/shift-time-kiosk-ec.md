# Schedule in Employee Center Pro Kiosk

The schedule details in Employee Center Pro Kiosk is a one-stop destination to check the shift scheduling details for the deskless workers.

Employee Center Pro Kiosk provides a centralized access to the deskless workers on their everyday activities at work.

Track and manage your shift schedule with a simplified home page optimized for the kiosks.

The **Schedule** tab displays the scheduling calendar. The Calendar header includes:

-   Date picker
-   Button that brings you back to the present date.
-   Keyboard accessibility icon button to provide keyboard-only navigational tooltip.
-   Option to navigate back and forth in time depending on the chosen view.

    **Note:**

    -   The default view of the calendar displays a month-view. You can change it to the day or the week view.
    -   Only the day view is available in mobile.

The calendar legends include:

-   Shift: Work shift type.
    -   Regular: Regular allotted shift.
    -   Transfer: Transferred shift apart from the regular assignment.
    -   Break: Pre-determined breaks during the shift.
-   Holiday: Designated holidays allotted to an employee
-   Time off: Paid time off allotted to an employee

    You can select each event type on the calendar to see the details of that event.

    **Note:** Customize the color of the calendar legends by modifying the **Employee Center Pro Kiosk Theme**. For more information, see [Customize Employee Center portal theme](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/customize-theme.md).
