# Enable Now Assist genius results

Enable the Now Assist in AI Search setup to use Now Assist genius results.

## Before you begin

Role required: esc\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **AI Search** &gt; **Now Assist in AI Search Setup**.

2.  Enable the Now Assist Genius Results by selecting the check boxes for each application and select **Save changes**.
