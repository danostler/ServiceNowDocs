# Modify the Featured News widget

The Featured News widget displays featured articles on company news and announcements.

## Before you begin

Role required: sp\_admin or admin

Learn more about the news widgets: [News articles](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/employee-experience-foundation/ec-news-articles.md)

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

    The Employee Center home page is displayed.

2.  Select **News Center**.

3.  Press and hold the control key, right-select the Featured News widget, and then select **Instance Options**.

4.  Configure the widget:
