# Subflows

Use the subflows to pull data from the Ultimate Kronos Group application into a ServiceNow application.

## Before you begin

Role required: None

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Process Automation** &gt; **Flow Designer**.

2.  In the Subflow lists, view the following:

    |Subflow|Description|
    |-------|-----------|
    |Employee Accrual Balance|Pulls the accrual leave balances \(by type\) of an employee from the Ultimate Kronos Group application into a ServiceNow application.|
    |Extract Employee Schedule|Pulls the schedule of an employee from the Ultimate Kronos Group application into a ServiceNow application.|
    |Extract Hours Worked|Pulls the number of hours that the employee has worked from the Ultimate Kronos Group application into a ServiceNow application.|
    |Extract Time Off Requests|Pulls the future time off requests of an employee from the Ultimate Kronos Group application into a ServiceNow application.|
