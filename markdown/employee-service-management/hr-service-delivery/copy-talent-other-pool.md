# Copy talent to pool

Use existing talent pools to associate suitable talent profiles from the pools to your talent pool.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.recruiter

## About this task

Copy the suitable talent profiles to associate them to your pool from another talent pool. You can also directly associate the talent profiles to your pool.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Recruitment workspace**.

2.  Go to the **Talent pool** tab.

    **Note:** You see a pool in the drop-down list only if you're the owner of the pool or one of the collaborators.

4.  Select **Done** to finish.


## Result

You have associated the required talent profiles from one Talent pool to another.
