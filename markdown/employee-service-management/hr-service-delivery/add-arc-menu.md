# Add ARC catalog items to the Employee Center

Give employees access to the Anonymous Report Center \(ARC\) by adding the catalog items to the Employee Center. Once added, employees can report misconduct or discrimination or open the ARC homepage from the **Human Resources** &gt; **Employee Conduct** page or via search.

## Before you begin

Role required: taxonomy\_admin

## About this task

The Anonymous Report Center plugin includes three out-of-the-box catalog items:

-   Report Misconduct
-   Report Discrimination
-   Visit Anonymous Report Center

\[Omitted image "arc-employee-conduct.png"\] Alt text: Once the catalog items are added, employees can report misconduct or discrimination or open the ARC homepage

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Content Taxonomy** &gt; **Topics**.

2.  Open the **Employee conduct** topic.

3.  Repeat the following steps for each Anonymous Report Center catalog item that you want to add under the **Employee Conduct** page:

    1.  Click **New**.

    2.  In the **Content type** field, select `Catalog Item`.

    3.  In the **Catalog item** field, select the Anonymous Resource Center catalog items.

    \[Omitted image "arc-taxonomy.png"\] Alt text: Once the steps above are complete, the ARC catalog items appear in the Connected Content tab for the Employee conduct topic

4.  After adding the catalog items, verify that they appear as intended by navigating to the Employee Center to the **Human Resources** &gt; **Employee Conduct** topic.
