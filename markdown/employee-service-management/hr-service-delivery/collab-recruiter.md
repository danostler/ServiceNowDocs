# Collaborate with the recruiter

Collaborate with the recruiter for the job requisition on the activity stream.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.hiring\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Your team** &gt; **Hiring**.

2.  Search for the job requisition, select it.

3.  In the Activity stream of the job requisition details page, send a message to the recruiter, using the **Comments** tab.

    The hiring manager can also see comments from the recruiter and the approver of the job requisition, in the Activity stream


## Result

The recruiter receives an email notification with the details of the comment.
