# View employee details

Use the employee modal for a quick overview of your employee's details.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_mh.manager\_hub\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

    The Employee Center portal opens.

2.  From the **Your team** drop-down list, select **Team members**.

3.  Select an employee from the employee list.

4.  View the details on the card.
