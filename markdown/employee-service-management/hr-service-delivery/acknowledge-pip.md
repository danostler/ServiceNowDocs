# Acknowledge a PIP request

Acknowledge the PIP request that is initiated by your manager. Complete other tasks that have been assigned to you.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **My tasks**.

2.  Sign the PIP acknowledgement form that is sent by your manager.

3.  Complete the other PIP tasks as and when they are assigned to you.
