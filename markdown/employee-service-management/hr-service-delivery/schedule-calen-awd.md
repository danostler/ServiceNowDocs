# Work schedule calendars

Pull work schedule calendars from Workday into the ServiceNow application.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_workday.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Workday Advanced Use Cases** &gt; **Time Off Request** &gt; **Work Schedule Calendar**.

2.  Click **Get Work Schedule Calendars** to run a flow that pulls schedule calendars from Workday into the ServiceNow application.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Work schedule calendar|Name of the work schedule calendar. For example, Standard 40 Hour Week.|
    |Work schedule calendar ID|Unique identifier of the work schedule calendar that is pulled from Workday.|
    |Work week start day|Day in a week when the work schedule calendar begins.|
    |Work week start day ID|ID of the day in a week when the work schedule calendar begins.|
    |Start time|Time when the work schedule begins for the day.|
    |End time|Time when the work schedule ends for the day.|
    |Day of week|Day in a week in a work schedule calendar.|
    |Day of week ID|ID of a day in a week in a work schedule calendar.|
    |Domain|Reference to domain.|

3.  Click **Submit**.
