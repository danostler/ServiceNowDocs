# Activate the Adobe Sign spoke

Activate the Adobe Sign spoke in IntegrationHub from the ServiceNow Store, and enable the signing of adhoc documents \(HR PDF document templates\) and Adobe Sign templates.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the ServiceNow Store.

2.  In the ServiceNow Store, search for the Adobe Sign spoke.

3.  Click the application tile.

    Detailed information about the application you are installing is displayed. Consider reading the Other Requirements and Dependencies sections, as applicable.

4.  Click **Get** and enter your HI login credentials.

5.  Enter the **Instance Name** and **Reason for request**, and click **Validate Instance**.

    You will receive an email with detailed installation instructions.

6.  Log in to the instance and set up the Adobe Sign spoke.
