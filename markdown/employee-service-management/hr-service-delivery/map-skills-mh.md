# View the skill distribution of your team

Get a deep-dive understanding of the skills on your team. Get an overview of different skills that the team members possess along with the count of people across each skill in the team.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_mh.manager\_hub\_user

**Note:** Employees must add proficiency levels to their skills. This data helps you to view details of skill distribution.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center**.

    The Employee Center portal opens.

2.  Open **Your team** &gt; **Skills**.

3.  In the Skills distribution section, select **View all**.

    The Skills distribution page opens.

4.  If you have team members reporting from different job functions, you can filter the skill distribution for each job function from the View list.

5.  In the **Skill view** tab, you can perform the following actions:
