# View HR task information

There are multiple ways to find a specific HR task.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_core.case\_writer

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **HR Case Management** &gt; **HR Tasks**.

    -   **Assigned to me:** All tasks assigned to the current user.
    -   **Open:** All open tasks.
    -   **Open - Unassigned:** All open tasks that have not been assigned.
    -   **Closed:** All closed tasks.
    -   **All:** All tasks regardless of state or assignment.
2.  Select the task to view.
