# Activate a schedule flow

Activate the schedule flow that interacts with Magnit to pull contingent workers from Magnit into the ServiceNow AI Platform instance.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_magnit.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Flow Designer** &gt; **Designer**.

2.  Open **Run HRSD Magnit Integration**.

    Alternatively, you can click **Run Job** in [Source for HR Service Delivery Integration with Magnit](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/hr-service-delivery/source-magnit.md) to pull contingent workers from Magnit into the ServiceNow AI Platform instance.

3.  Click **Activate**.

4.  In the **Trigger** field, specify the time and interval at which you want to automatically run the scheduled flow.
