# Create an HR knowledge article

Create or modify a knowledge article within an HR knowledge base to share HR information across your organization.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_core.kb\_writer

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **HR Administration** &gt; **Knowledge Management** &gt; **Create New Article**.

2.  Fill in the fields on the form.
