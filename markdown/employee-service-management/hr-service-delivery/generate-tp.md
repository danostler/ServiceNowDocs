# Generate talent profiles

Generate the talent profile creation after you opt in.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_ta\_hiring\_core.admin/ sn\_ta\_tp.talent\_profile\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Talent Definition**.

2.  Select **Generate Talent Profiles**.


## Result

You've created the talent profiles.
