# HR services for Employee Relations

List of default HR services for Employee Relations.

|HR service|Description|
|----------|-----------|
|Accommodations Request|Service for employees requesting an accommodation or modification to a working arrangement.|
|Anonymous Discrimination|Service to report any discrimination observed at work to the HR team anonymously.|
|Anonymous Report Misconduct|Service for employees to report misconduct or inappropriate behavior to HR anonymously|
|Performance improvement Plan \(PIP\)|Service to manage employee performance issues.|
|PIP Assessment|Service to manage employee PIP assessment issues|
|Report Discrimination|Service for employees to report discrimination or inappropriate behavior to HR.|
|Report Misconduct|Service for employees to report misconduct or inappropriate behavior to HR.|
|Declining performance|Service to assign a task to the manager where the manager mentions the employee name and performance issue.|
|PIP status report|Service that automatically creates a draft document which the employee has to sign during Performance Improvement Plan.|
