# Submit a direct deposit query in HR Service Delivery Advanced Integration with Oracle HCM

Submit a query related to your direct deposits in the Oracle HCM system from a ServiceNow instance.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

You will not be able to see your direct deposit accounts and its details if your employee number and Person ID \(Correlation\_ID\) are missing in your HR profile.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Employee Center** &gt; **Catalog** &gt; **Payroll** &gt; **Direct Deposit Inquiry**.

2.  View details of all your direct deposit accounts.

3.  Select an account for which you want to submit the query.

4.  In **How can we help you with your current direct deposits?** field, type in your query.

5.  Click **Submit**.

    An HR case is created under the HR service, Direct Deposit Inquiry - Oracle HCM Cloud.
