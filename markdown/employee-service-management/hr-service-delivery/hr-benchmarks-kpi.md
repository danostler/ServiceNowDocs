# HR Benchmarks KPIs

You can enable or disable a benchmark KPI, and customize KPI conditions. Integration with Performance Analytics provides daily data collection and drill down capabilities on KPI data.

|KPI|Description|
|---|-----------|
|Average age of closed onboarding cases|\[\[Summed duration of closed cases &gt; Lifecycle Event Type.Event type = Onboarding\]\]/\[\[Number of closed HR Cases &gt; Lifecycle Event Type.Event type = Onboarding\]\]/24|
|Average age of closed cases for COE HR talent management|\[\[Summed duration of closed cases &gt; COE = HR Talent Management Case\]\]/\[\[Number of closed HR Cases &gt; COE = HR Talent Management Case\]\]/24|
|Average age of closed cases for COE HR total rewards|\[\[Summed duration of closed cases &gt; COE = HR Total Rewards Case\]\]/\[\[Number of closed HR Cases &gt; COE = HR Total Rewards Case\]\]/24|
|Average age of closed cases for COE HR workforce administration case|\[\[Summed duration of closed cases &gt; COE = HR Workforce Administration Case\]\]/\[\[Number of closed HR Cases &gt; COE = HR Workforce Administration Case\]\]/24|
|% of HR cases resolved using KB articles|\[\[HR cases closed using a KB\]\]/\[\[Number of closed HR Cases\]\] \* 100|
|Average case survey score|HR.Case.Survey.Metric|
|Average age of open cases|\[\[Summed duration of open cases\]\]/\[\[Number of open cases\]\]/24|
|Average age of closed cases|\[\[Summed duration of closed cases\]\]/\[\[Number of closed HR Cases\]\]/24|
