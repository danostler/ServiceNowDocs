# Sign an HTML document

Complete your document task by signing and generating a PDF copy of the HTML document.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

    Alternatively, you can complete your document tasks by navigating to **Self-Service** &gt; **My Document Tasks**.

2.  Select the document template task \(signing\) that is assigned to you.

3.  Add your signature by typing or drawing.

    **Note:** If you do not agree with the content in the document, you can select the **I don't want to sign this document** option. You must enter your reason for declining, followed by **Decline and complete** to complete the task. The option to decline signing appears only when the **Signing Type** is **ServiceNow**.

4.  Click **Clear Signature** if you want to clear the signature and sign again.

5.  To view your signature in the document, click **Generate**.

6.  Click **Submit**.

    The task is complete and appears in your Completed To Dos section. You can view and print the document with the signatures.
