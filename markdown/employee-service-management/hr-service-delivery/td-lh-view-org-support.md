# View organizations you support

View your designated leader's organization so you can provide support in the capacity required for the organization to succeed. Supporters to whom the leader has delegated tasks and responsibilities can access Leader Hub to provide support accordingly.

## Before you begin

Role required: organizational supporter \[sn\_egd\_lh.supporter\]

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Employee Center**.

2.  From the mega menu, select **Orgs you support**.

    The Orgs you support page appears.

3.  In the Orgs you support widget, select the leader for whom you are providing support.

4.  Select the **Org talent** tab.

5.  Use the organizational chart or the **Search for employees in your org** field to select an employee who you must contact on behalf of your leader.

6.  Select the **View menu** icon on the card for the employee that you selected.

7.  Select **Send email** to send a message to the employee who you have been requested to contact.
