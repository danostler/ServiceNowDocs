# Secure HR case attachments

Add an extra layer of security on attachments that are linked to a case. Grant access to attachments with sensitive or private data to groups, users, user criteria or roles.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hr\_core.basic or sn\_hr\_core.case\_writer

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **HR Case Management** &gt; **All HR Cases**.

2.  Open an HR case that is assigned to you.

3.  Select a document from the **Documents** related list.

4.  In Access Settings, enable the **Sharing Permissions** option.

    The following related lists appear. Perform the required actions:
