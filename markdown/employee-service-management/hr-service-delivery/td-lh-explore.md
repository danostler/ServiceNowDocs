# Exploring Leader Hub

The Leader Hub app provides organizational and HR leaders with visibility into their organization's talent so they can comprehensively understand the landscape of their workforce. This application helps leaders gauge their team's strengths and deficiencies to confirm their employees have the appropriate skills and expertise to succeed in the present and future.

## Leader Hub overview

Leader Hub gives organizational leaders the tools to grasp their teams' capabilities, recognize skill gaps, and implement changes that support their workforce while advancing key business objectives.

## Leader Hub users

|User|Description|
|----|-----------|
|Organizational or HR leaders|Organizational and HR leaders use Leader Hub to analyze data associated with their organization's talent. Equipped with this data, they can make informed decisions that shape the culture, productivity, and overall success of their organization.|
|Organizational supporter|Organizational supporters entrusted with tasks and responsibilities use Leader Hub to view their designated leader's organization. With access to Leader Hub, they can provide support in the capacity required for the organization to succeed.|
