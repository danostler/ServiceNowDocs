# Submit a gifts and entertainment disclosure

Submit a disclosure to obtain the legal clearance for gifts and entertainment.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_lg\_ops.legal\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Help center** &gt; **Legal services** &gt; **Ethics and compliance**.

2.  Select **Gifts &amp; Entertainment Disclosure**.

3.  **Note:** For description on the field values, see [Gift and entertainment disclosure form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/employee-service-management/legal-gifts-and-entertainment/lsd-ge-disclosure-form.md)

    On the form, fill in the fields and select **Submit**.


## Result

-   A gifts and entertainment disclosure request is created in the New state. If the value of the disclosure is within the maximum amount allowed, the request is auto-approved and the state is updated to Approved.
-   The approval flow is triggered under the following conditions.
    -   There are no gift policies defined or there are no active policies.
    -   The value of the disclosure is more than the maximum amount allowed.
    -   The external company name is not available in the list and was manually entered.
-   The Standard Ticket page displays the following tabs to perform different tasks:
    -   Activity: Track the activity history of the request and post messages for the fulfiller working on the request.
    -   Attachments or Documents: Upload and manage the documents in the legal request.

        **Note:** If the external storage option is enabled, the **Documents** tab is displayed. Otherwise, the **Attachments** tab is displayed.

    -   Request details: Review request details.
