# Approve registration for a business contact or organization in Grants Management Portal

As an admin, you can approve or reject the registration of a new business contact or organization, submitted through the Grants Management Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self Service** &gt; **My approvals**.

2.  Select **Requested Approvals**, where you’ll find the organization registration that has requested to be added.

3.  Select the record to open the detail view, where you can see the business information and approval fields.

4.  You can choose to **Approve** or **Reject** registration for the business contact or organization.

    Your selection will be reflected in the **State** field.
