# ICM Entity Management Vehicle form

The Vehicle form allows you to create a new vehicle entity record within an investigative case, and capture as many identifiable details about the vehicle as possible. Not all fields are mandatory, but a minimum amount of information is required for the record to be searchable within ICM.

| | |
|---|---|
|Vehicle|Search for an existing record in the master index to auto-fill its information. If the vehicle record has not yet been created, skip this and enter the details manually.|
|Short Description|Short Description|
|License plate number|License plate number|
|State/Country of Registration|State/Country of Registration of the vehicle.|
|Make|Make of the vehicle.|
|Model|Model of the vehicle.|
|Year|Year of the vehicle.|
|Color|Color of the vehicle.|
|VIN|VIN number of the vehicle, if any.|
|Type/Body Style|Type/Body Style of the vehicle.|
|Country of Registration|Country the vehicle is registered in.|
|Status|Status of the vehicle.|
|Manufacturer Name|Manufacturer of the vehicle.|
|Information Completeness|Not all fields are mandatory, but a minimum amount of information is required for the record to be searchable within ICM. The information completeness field indicates whether or not that minimum requirement is met. Displays as Partial or Complete.|
|Notes|Any additional information.|
|Source From| |
|Relationship Details|A brief description of the vehicle’s relationship to the case.|
