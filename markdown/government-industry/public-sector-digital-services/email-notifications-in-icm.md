# Email notifications for the Investigative Case Type

Email notifications are automatically triggered by the Investigative Case Management. In Public Sector Digital Services, notifications can result from an agent action, a response needed from an agent or constituent, or from an update on a case status.

Email notifications in the Investigative Case Management workspace are automatically sent to the investigator \(case agent\) when:

-   A case is assigned to the agent.
-   The assigned to field is modified.
-   The case has been inactive for 10 days.
