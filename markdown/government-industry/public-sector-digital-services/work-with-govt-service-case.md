# Manage your government service cases from the Government Service Portal

Create, accept, assign, escalate, de-escalate, cancel a case, and even more actions for various government services all from the Government Service Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_gsm.constituent\_agent, sn\_gsm.business\_agent, sn\_gsm.agency\_agent, sn\_gsm.relationship\_agent, and sn\_gsm.service\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the Government Service Portal.

2.  Do one of the following actions that are related to your case:
