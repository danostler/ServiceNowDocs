# Zurich Public Sector

- [Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/government-industry/public-sector-digital-services/bun-public-sector-landing-page.md) -- Extend beyond the packaged government case types, leveraging the government data model to create your own workflows with low-code tools. Fulfill public service requests end-to-end with pre-packaged playbooks. Deliver a personalized experience to take care of common constituent requests using resources like the portal, service catalog, virtual agent, and online communities. Use the ServiceNow Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS) application to serve constituents, businesses, and agencies on a single platform.
  - [Explore](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/government-industry/public-sector-digital-services/exploring-public-sector-digital-services.md) -- Learn about Public Sector Digital Services (PSDS) features to help create a seamless support experience for constituents, businesses, internal agencies, and external agencies.
    - [Data management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/government-industry/public-sector-digital-services/psds-explore-data-management.md) -- Data management refers to the processes and tools used to collect, organize, secure, and use constituent-related data effectively so that government service agent teams can deliver better service experiences. You can give your managers and agents a comprehensive view of constituent data, including constituent details, products and services, assets, service contracts, and entitlements.
    - [Portals](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/government-industry/public-sector-digital-services/portals-psds-exploring.md) -- A portal provides an end user, in this case a constituent or applicant, with step-by-step guidance through submitting a public service request case. Use Portals to submit requests for licenses and permits, government records and public information, non-emergency services, or to submit applications for social benefits or grants opportunities.

      - [Reviewer Service Portal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/government-industry/public-sector-digital-services/psds-rsp-overview.md) -- Enable merit reviewers to track, score, and monitor grant proposals via a dedicated Reviewer Service portal.
      - [Grants Management Portal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/government-industry/public-sector-digital-services/psds-gmp-overview.md) -- Constituents and business contacts can find, apply for, and track the status of their grant applications using the Grants Management Portal.
