# Exploring digital integration management in Enterprise Architecture Workspace

Exploring digital integration management in Enterprise Architecture Workspace.

Digital Integration Management in Enterprise Architecture Workspace enables you to effectively model integrations and their related business applications to uncover insights and drive business value.

The digital integration represents the integration between two business applications. In a typical scenario there would be a consuming business application, a provider business application, and an interface that is provided by the provider business application. The digital integration contains the metadata on the integration, including name, version, type, data flow direction, middleware used, owners, and so on.

-   Digital Integration:
    -   Represents details of the integration between two applications.
    -   Consists of a providing business application, an interface from the providing application, and is consumed by another business application.
-   Digital Interface:
    -   Defined by the providing business application.
    -   Outlines a business application model for interacting with another application \(frequently an API\).
