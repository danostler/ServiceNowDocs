# Manage goals

Manage goals to track and optimize your investments in business applications. Add a quantitative and qualitative targets to the goals in the Enterprise Architecture Workspace.

-   **[View all goals](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-view-all-goals.md)**  
You can view the list of all available goals in the Enterprise Architecture Workspace.
-   **[Add or edit a goal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-add-or-edit-a-goal.md)**  
Create or edit a goal to track and optimize your investments in business applications.
-   **[Add a quantitative target to a goal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-add-a-quantitative-target-to-a-goal.md)**  
You can add a quantitative target to track the progress of achieving a goal. It’s a numbers-based measurable target set to track the performance of a goal.
-   **[Add a qualitative target to a goal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-add-a-qualitative-target-to-a-goal.md)**  
You can add a qualitative target to track the progress of achieving a goal. It's a non-measurable and interpretation-based target, set to track the performance of a goal.
-   **[Create a sub-goal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-create-a-sub-goal.md)**  
You can create sub-goals that are associated with your main goals. Sub-goals are more specific in nature and consist of actionable items that contribute to the completion of the parent goal.
