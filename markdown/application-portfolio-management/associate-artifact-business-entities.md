# Associate an artifact to a business entity - Legacy

Associate an architectural artifact to existing elements in the ServiceNow AI Platform, such as business capabilities or business applications. The association creates a relationship between the artifact and related entities.

## Before you begin

**Important:**

Starting with the Xanadu release, the legacy architectural artifacts module is moved to the Enterprise Architecture Workspace. To learn more, see [Add a related entity to an architectural artifact](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-add-a-related-entity-to-an-architectural-artifact.md).

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Enterprise Architecture** &gt; ** Architectural Artifacts** &gt; **Artifacts**.

2.  Select the name of the artifact for which you want to add a related entity.

3.  In the displayed details form, select the  **Related Entities ** tab.

4.  Select **New**.

5.  On the form, fill in the fields.

    For field information, see [Create related entities form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/related-entities-form.md).

6.  Select **Submit**.
