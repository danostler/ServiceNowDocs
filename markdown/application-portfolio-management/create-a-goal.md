# Create a goal for an application strategy - Legacy

After assessing the applications and deciding on strategies, set concrete goals to maximize or minimize depending on the indicators for the selected fiscal period.

## Before you begin

**Important:**

Starting with the Xanadu release, the legacy goals module is moved to the Enterprise Architecture Workspace. To learn more, see [Exploring goals](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-goals.md).

Role required: user\_admin, pps\_admin

## About this task

To understand how your organizational strategies are performing, see the [Strategic Spend Tracking for PPM dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/it-business-management/ppm-collaboration/ppm-strategic-spend-tracking-dashboard.md) topic. It provides comprehensive visualization to help you understand how the planned costs, actual costs, and benefits for projects aligned to your organization's strategies trend over time.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Enterprise Architecture** &gt; **Home** and click **Create** in the **Opportunities &amp; Solutions**, Goals section.

    You can also navigate by any of the following steps:

    -   **Enterprise Architecture** &gt; **Application Portfolio Analysis** &gt; **Goals** and click **New**.
    -   **Organization** &gt; **Goals** and click **New**.
2.  Fill in the form fields.

    For field information, see [Goal form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/new-goal-form.md).

3.  Click **Save**.

4.  In the Recent Goals section, click the goal that you created and update the following fields:

    For field information, see [Update Goal form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/update-goal-form.md).

5.  Click **Save**.

    You can view all the goals from the list in the Recent Goals section of the portal by clicking **View all**.


## What to do next

You can [create a program](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/create-a-program.md) to execute the goal.
