# Open a Configurable Workspace experience task conkeyrefs

Access your Configurable Workspace experience in UI Builder for editing.

## Before you begin

Role required: ui\_builder\_admin or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Now Experience Framework** &gt; **UI Builder**.

2.  Select your experience.

3.  Select **Experiences**.


## Result

Your Configurable Workspace experience opens in UI Builder.
