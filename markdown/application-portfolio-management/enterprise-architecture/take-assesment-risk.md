# Respond to a risk assessment questionnaire - Legacy

Respond to a risk questionnaire to provide risk-related information for your business application to a risk manager.

## Before you begin

Role required: grc\_business\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Enterprise Architecture** &gt; **Application Portfolio** &gt; **All Business Applications**.

2.  Open a business application.

3.  Select the **Risk Questionnaire** related list.

4.  Click the **Take assessment** link.

5.  Enter your responses in the form.

6.  Click **Submit**
