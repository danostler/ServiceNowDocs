# Manage the artifacts of a business application - Legacy

View and manage the artifacts that are associated with a business application.

## Before you begin

**Important:**

Starting with the Xanadu release, the legacy Architectural Artifacts module is moved to the Enterprise Architecture Workspace. To learn more, see [Create an architectural artifact and associate it with a business application](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-portfolio-management/eaw-assoicate-artifact-ba.md).

Role required: sn\_apm.apm\_analyst

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Enterprise Architecture** &gt; **Application Portfolio** &gt; **All Business Applications**.

2.  Click the name of the business application to view the associated artifacts.

3.  In the Related Links section, select the **Architectural Artifacts** tab.

    A list of artifacts associated with the business application is displayed.

4.  Create or edit an artifact.

    -   To create an artifact, click **New**.
    -   To edit an existing artifact, click **Edit**.
    You can also download or remove the selected artifact as per your requirement.
