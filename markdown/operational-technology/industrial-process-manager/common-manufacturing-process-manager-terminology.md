# Industrial Process Manager common terminology

Before getting started with the Industrial Process Manager, let's look at some common terminology and acronyms that are used in this content.

</th><th>

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Operational Technology

</td><td>

OT

</td><td>

Technology that is used for industrial automation to control physical processes.

**Note:** Operational Technology is not the Internet of Things \(IoT\).

</td></tr><tr><td>

International Society of Automation

</td><td>

ISA

</td><td>

Organization that publishes the standards for industrial enterprises, including the ISA-95 equipment model.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Extract, Transform, Load

</td><td>

ETL

</td><td>

Common term that is used for taking data from a source system, transforming it, and then uploading it to a target system.
