# Operational Technology Management licensing and subscriptions

ServiceNow® OTM licensing is a crucial aspect of Operational Technology Management as it calculates and presents the usage of OTM subscriptions based on subscription units, which could encompass factors like the number of devices monitored or the duration of the subscription.

## Operational Technology Management licensing and subscriptions overview

This enables organizations to ensure compliance, allocate resources effectively, and make informed decisions about scaling their OTM capabilities to safeguard their operational technology systems and adapt to changing security and operational needs. Use the OTM licensing module to access subscription-related details for the OTM products: ServiceNow OT Foundation, ServiceNow OT Visibility, and ServiceNow OT Vulnerability Response.
