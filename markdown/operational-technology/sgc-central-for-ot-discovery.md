# SGC Central

Use SGC Central to set up your Service Graph Connector for ServiceNow OT Discovery, then import data from your OT Discovery environment into your ServiceNow instance.

SGC Central is recommended for setting up the Service Graph Connector for ServiceNow OT Discovery. It simplifies connector deployment with intuitive setup options and step-by-step playbook guidance. Alternatively, you can use the original Guided Setup. See [Service Graph Connector for ServiceNow OT Discovery Guided Setup](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/operational-technology/sgc-ot-discovery-guided-setup.md).

SGC Central is available as a view in the CMDB Workspace or can be searched for in the **All** menu. The application enables you to discover and install Service Graph Connectors, and then effectively manage the full life cycle of creating, editing, monitoring, and debugging connections. The SGC Central application also verifies that connectors remain supported and up-to-date with certified partners.
