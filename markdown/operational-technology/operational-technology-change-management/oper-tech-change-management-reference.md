# Operational Technology Change Management reference

Reference topics provide additional information about the Operational Technology Change Management application.

-   **[Change categories](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/operational-technology/operational-technology-change-management/ot-change-categories.md)**  
By categorizing the Operational Technology \(OT\) change requests, you can group and narrow the search for specific OT change requests. Categories also help stakeholders know what the change is about.
-   **[Components installed with Operational Technology Change Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/operational-technology/operational-technology-change-management/components-installed-with-operational-technology-change-request.md)**  
Several types of components may be installed with activation of the Operational Technology Change Management \(sn\_ot\_chg\_mgmt\) application, including the user roles.

Find more information about the OT extension classes and related applications.
