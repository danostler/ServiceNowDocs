# Test the connection to a credential store

You should test the connection to a credential store after you configure a new credential store or when users experience problems that might involve the connection.

## Before you begin

Role required: password\_reset\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Password Reset** &gt; **Credential Stores** and then open the credential store record.

2.  In the header bar, click **Save and Test Connection**.

    A progress page displays the result of the test.
