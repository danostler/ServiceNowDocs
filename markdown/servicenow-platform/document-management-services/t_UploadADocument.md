# Upload a document

Upload a document into a saved record. You can upload text files, spreadsheets, presentations, PDF files, and more.

## Before you begin

Role required: document\_management\_admin

or

Role required: document\_management\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the document record.

2.  Click the **Upload/Check In Revision** related link.

3.  Click **Choose File** and navigate to the file.

4.  Click **Open**.

5.  Check the Name and Revision Number.

6.  Click **OK**.

    After the initial document is uploaded, subsequent revisions can be created by checking out the document.
