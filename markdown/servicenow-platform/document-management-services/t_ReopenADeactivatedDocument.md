# Reopen a deactivated document

A document that has been deactivated can be reopened.

## Before you begin

Role required: document\_management\_admin

or

Role required: document\_management\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the document record.

2.  Right-click the header bar and select **Reopen Document**.
