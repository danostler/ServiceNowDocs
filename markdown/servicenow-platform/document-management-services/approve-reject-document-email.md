# Approve or reject a document version through email

Approve or reject a document version request directly from your email.

## Before you begin

Role required: approver\_user

## Procedure

1.  Open the approval request email.

2.  Select **Click here to approve** to approve the document version or select **Click here to reject** to reject the document version.
