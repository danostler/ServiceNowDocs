# Publish a document in Managed Documents

After the document is reviewed and approved, publish the document within Managed Documents.

## Before you begin

Role required: document\_management\_admin

or

Role required: document\_management\_user

## Procedure

1.  Open the document record.

2.  In the Document Revisions list, click the name of the revision that was approved and is listed in the Ready for Publishing stage.

3.  In the header bar, click **Publish Revision**.

4.  In the Publish revision dialog box, check that the name and revision number are correct and click **Publish**.

    In the Document Revisions list, the revision you just published is now in the Published stage.
