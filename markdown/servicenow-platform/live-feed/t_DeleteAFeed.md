# Delete a feed

If you are a feed administrator, you can delete the feed.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  Click either **Group Feeds** or **Record Feeds**, and then select the feed name.

3.  Click **Delete**.

4.  Click **Yes** to confirm deletion.

    The feed is deleted from Live Feed. Messages posted to a private feed are deleted. Messages posted to a public feed remain on the Company Feed without a feed link.
