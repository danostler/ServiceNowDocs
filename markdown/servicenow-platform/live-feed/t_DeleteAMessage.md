# Delete a message

Users can delete any of their own posts or replies. A conversation administrator can also delete messages.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Hover over the message you want to delete.

2.  Click the trash can icon in the upper-right-hand corner of the message box.

3.  Click **Delete** in the confirmation box.

    The message disappears from the feed.
