# Follow other Live Feed users

As you use Live Feed, you can follow the message threads of other users.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to a thread that the user you want to follow is engaged in.

2.  Click that user's name or point to the user's picture.

    A **Follow** link appears in the user's profile.

3.  Click **Follow**.

4.  To stop following the user, navigate to **People** &gt; **Following**.

5.  Click **Unfollow**.

6.  To view people who are following you, navigate to **People** &gt; **Followers**.
