# View a team feed

View a team feed to see messages belonging to that team.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  In the sidebar, click your profile picture, then select the **Teams** tab.

    -   To view a team you belong to, select the team name.
    -   To view a public team you do not belong to, select **All Teams** and then click the team name.

        You must be a member to view the feed for a private team.
