# Subscribe a team to feed notification

A team administrator can subscribe a team to specific feeds so the members of the team receive email notifications.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  Click **Teams** and select the team name.

    You can use the advanced search to locate team of which you are the administrator.

3.  Click **Feeds**.

4.  Click the subscribe icon beside the feed name.

    You can click the unsubscribe icon to stop receiving email notifications.
