# View a team member

View a list of members belonging to a Live Feed team.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  In the sidebar, click your profile picture, then select the **Teams** tab.

3.  From the team list, select the team name.

    You must be a member of the team to view the members.

4.  Click **Members**.

    The list of team members appears, organized by team administrators and team members.
