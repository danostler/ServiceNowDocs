# Remove a member

If you're a team administrator, you can remove any member from the team.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  In the sidebar, click your profile picture, then select the **Teams** tab.

3.  From the team list, select the team name.

4.  Click the **Members** tab.

5.  Click **Remove** below the member name.

6.  Click **Remove** again in the confirmation box that opens.

    **Note:** You can remove members regardless of their current state. That is, you can remove members who have already joined the team or whose membership is pending.
