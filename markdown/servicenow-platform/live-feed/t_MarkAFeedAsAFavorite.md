# Mark a feed as a favorite

Top-level messages in feeds can be marked as favorites on a per user basis only.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

## About this task

You can also search for messages marked as favorites, and remove the favorite designation as needed.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Live Feed** &gt; **Live Feed**.

2.  Click either **Group Feeds** or **Record Feeds**, and then click **All Feeds**.

3.  In the list of feeds, click the star iconadjacent to the feed name you want to mark as a favorite.

    To unfavorite a message, click the star iconof a message that is already a favorite.
