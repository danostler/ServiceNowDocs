# Using human readable URLs for the Knowledge article page

Human readable URLs include more readable keywords to the URL structure.

Human readable URLs are short and simple web addresses that use relevant keywords from the knowledge article page that are easier to understand. Users have a better idea of what they will find when they access the link.

The benefits of using human readable URLs rather than technical URLs include:

-   Improves the readability of the URL to make it easier for a human to understand
-   Improves search engine optimization \(SEO\) rankings and the click-through rate
