# View request status

View the status of a request to see its progress, and determine when it may be fulfilled.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **Requested Items**.

2.  Click your order number to view details.

3.  View the order's progress in the **Stage** field.

    \[Omitted image "RequestStatus.png"\] Alt text: Requested Item form

4.  Check the **Backordered** field, and if the item is backordered, review comments for information about its return to stock.
