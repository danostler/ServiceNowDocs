# Exclude relationships from the relations formatter view

Create a list of relationships that should not appear in the relations formatter view on CI forms.

## Before you begin

Role required:

-   sn\_cmdb\_editor or itil: View the relationship type exclusion list
-   sn\_cmdb\_admin or itil\_admin: Create, update, or delete the relationship type exclusion list

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Configuration** &gt; **Relationships** &gt; **Relationship Type Exclusion List**.

2.  In the CI Relation Filters list view, click **New**.

3.  Fill out the CI Relation Filter form to specify the relationship that you want to exclude from view.

4.  Click **Submit**.

    Excluded relationships do not appear in the relations formatter view on CI forms.
