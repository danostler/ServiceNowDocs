# Initiate a conference call from incident communication plan

Use Notify to initiate a conference call from an incident communication plan by inviting one or more users.

## Before you begin

Role required: notify\_view, or notify\_setup\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Incident Communications Management** &gt; **All**.

2.  Open the relevant incident communication plan.

3.  Click the **Conference** tab.

4.  For the relevant incident communications task, under **Actions**, click **Start Conference Call**.

5.  Select the conference bridge and the participants for the conference call.

    Available options in the conference bridge depend on the conference providers configured. For more information, refer [Configure a provider in Notify](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/servicenow-platform/notify/configure-providers-for-provider-selectors.md).
