# Join a conference call

You can join an active conference call from an incident to discuss about the resolution of an incident.

## Before you begin

Role required: notify\_view, notify\_admin, major\_incident\_manager, or communication\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Incident** &gt; **Major Incidents** &gt; **Open**.

2.  Open the relevant major incident.

3.  Select **View Workbench**.

4.  Select **Collaborate** tab.

5.  Select **Join Call**.
