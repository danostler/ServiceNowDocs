# Functionality enhancements in Test Management 2.0

Test Management 2.0 offers a few functionality enhancements over Test Management 1.0.

## Integration with Agile Development 2.0

Integration of Test Management 2.0 with Agile Development 2.0 provides embedded testing capabilities for scrum teams. Test Management 2.0 enables you to create tests for stories of the sprint, and also track the execution and completion of those tests. As a scrum lead or product owner, you gain an insight of the stories that are tested and are to be closed.
