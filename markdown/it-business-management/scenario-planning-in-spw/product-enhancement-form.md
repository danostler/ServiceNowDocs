# Product Enhancement form

Learn about the fields of the product enhancement form. Use this form to create or edit the details of a product enhancement in Strategic Planning.

These fields are displayed on the full details page of the product enhancement.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Product|Name of the product that this product enhancement is created for.|
|Product feature|Product feature that this product enhancement is associated to.|
|Version|Version of the product enhancement.|
|Name|Name of the product enhancement.|
|State|State of the product enhancement.|
|Assignment group|User group that is assigned to the product enhancement.|
|Assigned to|User who is assigned to the product enhancement.|
|Description|Brief description of the product enhancement.|
|Start date|Start date for the product enhancement.|
|End date|End date for the product enhancement.|
