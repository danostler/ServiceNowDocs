# Define a SAFe team

From SAFe, create an agile team and associate it to an ART.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Scaled Agile Framework \(SAFe\)** &gt; **SAFe Teams**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields:

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Name|Suitable name for the group.|
    |Manager|Designated servant leader of the team.|
    |Group capacity \(points\)|Total capacity of the team per sprint measured in story points.|
    |Group email|Email distribution list of the group.|
    |Description|Suitable description for the group.|

4.  Click **Submit**.

5.  Navigate to **Scaled Agile Framework \(SAFe\)** &gt; **Agile Release Trains**.

6.  Select an ART to which you want to associate the SAFe team.

7.  Click **Edit**, to you associate your team to the ART.

8.  Click Save.
