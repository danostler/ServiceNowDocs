# Customize indicators of your Essential SAFe dashboard reports

Configure indicators to reflect your customizations in Essential SAFe dashboards.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or pa\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Performance Analytics** &gt; **Indicators** &gt; **Automated Indicators**.

2.  Search for and open the required indicator.

    For example, if you want to customize the SAFe Burnup report, open SAFe: Sum of story points of all stories in the feature in current sprint from the list of indicators.

3.  In the Additional conditions section, modify the conditions according to your preferences.

4.  Click **Update**.
