# Configure a widget for the financial metrics of a demand

Configure a widget to view and track the financial metrics of a demand on the Demand Financials page.

## Before you begin

Role required: pps\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Project Administration** &gt; **Widgets**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the Widget form, fill in the fields.

    For a description of the field names, see [Widget form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/demand-management/widget-form.md).
