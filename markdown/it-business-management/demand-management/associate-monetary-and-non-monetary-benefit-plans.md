# Associate monetary and non-monetary benefit plans

Associate monetary and non-monetary benefit plans, so that you can capture the potential benefits \(financial and non-financial\) accrued by the demand.

## Before you begin

Role required: it\_demand\_manager

## About this task

You can associate a monetary benefit plan with a non-monetary benefit plan and vice versa.

**Note:** When you delete a benefit plan, its relationship with the associated benefit plan \(if any\) is also removed.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Demand** &gt; **Demands** &gt; **All**.

2.  Open the required demand form.

3.  Select the Monetary Benefit Plans or Non-monetary Benefit Plans related list.

4.  Select the name of a benefit plan that you want to associate.

5.  Associate a monetary or non-monetary benefit plan.
