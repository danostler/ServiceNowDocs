# Enable lean budgeting for demands

Enable the Investment Budget option to allocate budgeting for demands at fiscal period.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Demands** &gt; **Demand** &gt; **All**.

2.  Open the required demand.

3.  Select the Additional actions and navigate to **Configure** &gt; **Related Lists**.\[Omitted image "config-invest-budget.png"\] Alt text: List of options available in Additional actions.

4.  Select **Investment Budget** from the Available column and add it to Selected column.

5.  Select **Save**.


-   **[Manage lean budgeting for demand](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/demand-management/lean-budgeting-demand-management.md)**  
Allocate budget for demands at each fiscal period instead of the entire fiscal year to reduce the overhead costs associated with traditional approach.
