# View a demand summary

Hover over a bubble in the demand workbench bubble chart to display a summary of the demand information.

## Before you begin

Role required: it\_demand\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Hover over a demand in the bubble chart to highlight the bubble and its label.

    A bubble summary window also shows the demand name and the risk, value, and size data. Hovering over the bubble also highlights the corresponding demand row in the list view. Hovering over a demand in the list view highlights the corresponding bubble in the bubble chart.

    \[Omitted image "demand-bubble-chart-view.png"\] Alt text: Hover over bubble view with bubble information such as risk and size values.

    **Note:** When hovering over a bubble, scroll down in the list view to see the corresponding highlighted demand row. If necessary, adjust the height of the list view or the bubble chart.

2.  For multiple demands, some bubbles or labels overlap and are difficult to see.

    Hover over a label to highlight the corresponding bubble.

    Use when:

    -   Two or more bubbles have the same metrics and overlap directly.
    -   Several bubbles intersect and overlap.
    -   A bubble is only partially visible on the edge of the bubble chart.
