# Export to PowerPoint for Strategic Portfolio Management

The ServiceNow® Export to PowerPoint for Strategic Portfolio Management add-in helps you to generate and download project status reports from your instance as a Microsoft PowerPoint file. You can use this file to share it across the stakeholders or teams for collaboration.
