# Create an investment

Create an investment to fund a target.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_invst\_pln\_v2.investment\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Investment Funding** &gt; **My Funds**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.
