# Create and manage pages and subpages for demands

Flexibly organize information for your planning items by creating, duplicating, and deleting pages and subpages in Next Experience for Demand Management.

## Before you begin

Role required:

-   Create and manage pages and subpages - it\_demand\_user, it\_demand\_manager, demand\_approver
-   Read pages and subpages - apw\_read, ppm\_read

## Procedure

1.  Open a demand from the home page of Next Experience for Demand Management.

    For more information, see [Access demands](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/portfolio-planning/access-demands-ppw.md).

2.  Select **Docs** from the L-2 \(level 2\) navigation menu.

3.  Create or manage docs in any of the following ways.
