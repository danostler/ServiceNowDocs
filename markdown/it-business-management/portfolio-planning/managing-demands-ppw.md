# Managing demands in Next Experience for Demand Management

Next Experience for Demand Management features support end-to-end planning and management of your demands.

## All Demands list view

When you navigate to the Next Experience for Demand Management, you land on the **All Demands** home page. This page displays the demands in a list view. You can create a demand, edit a demand, or export demands from this page. For more information, see [Demands list view](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/portfolio-planning/demands-list-view-ppw.md).
