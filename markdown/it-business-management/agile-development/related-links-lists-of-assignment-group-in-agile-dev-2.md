# Related links and lists of assignment group in Agile Development 2.0

Use the related lists in the Group form for the actions that you need to perform for your assignment group in Agile Development 2.0, such as adding sprints and editing group members.

|Name|Description|
|----|-----------|
|Create Sprints|Create sprints for the team. New sprints are listed in the Sprints related list of the group.|
|Sprint Planning|[Plan your sprint activities in Agile Development 2.0](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/plan-sprint-activities.md).|
