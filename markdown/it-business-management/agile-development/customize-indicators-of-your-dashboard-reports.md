# Customize indicators of your Agile 2.0 dashboard reports

Configure indicators to reflect your customizations on the Agile 2.0 dashboards.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or pa\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Performance Analytics** &gt; **Indicators** &gt; **Automated Indicators**.

2.  Search for and open the required indicator.

    For example, if you want to customize the Sprint Burnup report, open Scrum: Sum of story points of completed stories in current sprint from the list of indicators.

3.  In the Additional conditions section, modify the conditions according to your preferences.

4.  Click **Update**.
