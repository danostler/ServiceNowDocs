# Migration from Agile Development 1.0 to Agile Development 2.0

Migrate from Agile Development 1.0 to Agile Development 2.0 for enhanced agile capabilities and features.

**Important:** Agile Development 1.0 and its features such as Sprint burndown chart and release burndown chart are deprecated and no longer available. [Agile Development 2.0](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/agile-landing-page.md) provides the latest experience for supporting your Agile work methodology.

Apply the following migration steps on a non-production instance, verify if the migration is completed as intended, and then perform the migration steps on a production instance.

-   [Manage customizations before upgrading to Agile Development 2.0](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/manage-customizations.md).
-   [Delete customizations](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/delete-customizations.md).
-   [Activate Agile Development 2.0](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/activate-agile-development.md).
-   [Complete the prerequisites for converting teams to Agile Development 2.0 groups](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/steps-prior-conversion.md)
-   [Convert Agile Development 1.0 teams to Agile Development 2.0 groups](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/convert-teams-groups.md)
-   [Verify the conversion of Agile Development 1.0 teams to Agile Development 2.0 groups](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/verify-after-conversion.md)

-   **[Manage customizations before upgrading to Agile Development 2.0](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/manage-customizations.md)**  
You can take complete advantage of enhanced agile functionality by managing your customizations before upgrading to Agile Development 2.0.
-   **[Delete customizations before upgrading to Agile Development 2.0](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/delete-customizations.md)**  
Take complete advantage of enhanced functionality by deleting all your customizations before upgrading to Agile Development 2.0.
-   **[Convert Agile Development 1.0 teams to Agile Development 2.0 groups](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/convert-teams-groups.md)**  
Complete most of the migration steps by converting teams in Agile Development 1.0 to groups in Agile Development 2.0.
-   **[Agile Development 2.0 enhancements over Agile Development 1.0](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-business-management/agile-development/appendix.md)**  
Agile Development 2.0 offers a few enhancements over Agile Development 1.0.
