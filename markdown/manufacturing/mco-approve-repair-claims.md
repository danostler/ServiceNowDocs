# Reviewing and approving repair claims

Agents portal or workspace enables you to review and approve the claims submitted by a dealer.

From the Activities menu, select **Review &amp; approve**.

|Tasks|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|[Reviewing and approving repair claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-approve-repair-claims.md)|Approve all claim job expenses.|
|[Reject all claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/reject-all-claims.md)|Reject all claim job expenses.|
|[Partially approve a claim](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-partially-approve-claims.md)|Partially approve the job claim expenses.|
|[Send back a claim](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-send-back-claims.md)|Send back the claim for additional information.|

-   **[Approve all claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-approve-all-repair-claims.md)**  
As an OEM claims agent, approve all the claim job expenses that were raised by a dealer.
-   **[Reject all claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/reject-all-claims.md)**  
Rejects all the claims job expenses raised by a dealer.
-   **[Partially approve a claim](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-partially-approve-claims.md)**  
As an OEM claims agent, partially approve the claims job expenses raised by a dealer.
-   **[Send back a claim](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-send-back-claims.md)**  
As an OEM claims agent, send back the claims request to the dealer for additional information.
