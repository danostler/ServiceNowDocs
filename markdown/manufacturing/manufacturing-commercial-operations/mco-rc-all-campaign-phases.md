# Create a campaign phase

Create a recall campaign phase claim assigned to those logged in to the workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **CSM/FSM Configurable Workspace** &gt; **Lists** &gt; **Recall Management** &gt; **All Campaigns** &gt; **All Campaign phases**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  Create a recall campaign phase by referring to [Create My campaign phases](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-rc-my-campaign-phases.md).
