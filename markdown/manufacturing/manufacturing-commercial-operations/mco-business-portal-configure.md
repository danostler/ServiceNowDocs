# Configure the Business Portal for Manufacturing Commercial Operations

Configure the business portal to enable your customers to submit orders through self-service.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Service Portals** &gt; **Portals**.

2.  On the Service Portals page, in the Title column, enter `*business portal`.

3.  Select **Business Portal**.

    A page opens in your instance where you can configure the Business Portal.

4.  Select **Inactive**.

    **Note:** The Business Portal is inactive by default.

    \[Omitted image "enable-business-portal-mco.png"\] Alt text: Enable business portal

5.  Select **Update**.


## Result

The Business Portal is now active.
