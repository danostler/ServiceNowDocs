# Recall management

Recall management enables the OEMs to identify and remove or replace the faulty products sold to the customers.

|[View All campaigns](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-rc-all-campaigns.md)|Displays all the recall campaign that are created by the OEM.|

|[Create a campaign phase](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-rc-all-campaign-phases.md)|Displays all the recall campaign phases that are created by the OEM.|
