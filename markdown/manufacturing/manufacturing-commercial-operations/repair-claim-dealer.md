# Repair claim for the dealer

The dealer portal enables the dealers to raise a claim request for the repair work that was performed for a product. The repair could have been done either under warranty or after the recall of the product.

You can perform the [Submit a repair claim for warranty](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-submit-repair-claim.md) task for repair claim on the dealer portal:
