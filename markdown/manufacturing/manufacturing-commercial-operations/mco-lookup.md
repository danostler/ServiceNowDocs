# Look up an asset or claim by its serial number

Look up the serial number of the asset or claims details in the dealer portal. View the item for recall and sales promotion claims.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_dealer\_mgmt.dealer\_service\_advisor

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Dealer Portal** &gt; **Lookup by serial number**.

2.  Enter the serial number.

3.  Select **Search**.

    The page displays the claim details of that particular serial number. It includes **Submitted claims**, **Active recalls**, and **Details**.

4.  View the relevant details.

    -   To view the claims submitted for that product or asset, select **Submitted claims**.
    -   To view if any recall is performed on the asset or product, Select **Active recalls**.
    -   To view the install base form view, select **Details**.
