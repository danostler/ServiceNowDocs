# Repair form

Includes all the forms related to repair and recall.

-   **[Campaign tasks form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-campaign-tasks-form.md)**  
Campaign tasks form enables you to add the recall campaign details.
-   **[Corrective action form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-corrective-action-form.md)**  
The corrective action form enables the OEM to address the asset issue, either by eliminating or replacing the asset.
-   **[Corrective action charges form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco_corrective_action_charges_form.md)**  
Corrective action charges form include the expenses related to addressing a non-conformance like labor charges.
-   **[Claim details form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-claim-details-form.md)**  
The Claim details form enables the dealer to enter the claim details for the repair work performed.
-   **[Phase task form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-phase-task-form.md)**  
Phase task form enables you to add the details of the recall phase.
-   **[Recall campaign form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-recall-campaign-form.md)**  
The recall campaign form provides the original equipment manufacturer \(OEM\) with the required information to initiate a recall campaign.
-   **[Recall campaign phase form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-recall-campaign-phases-form.md)**  
The recall campaign phase form enables you to add the recall campaign phases details.
-   **[Repair claim form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/repair-claim-form.md)**  
The repair claim form enables you to enter the warranty and recall claim details.
-   **[Pre-authorization form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-pre-auth-form.md)**  
The pre-authorization form enables you to enter the pre-authorization details.
-   **[Pre-authorization job details form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/manufacturing-commercial-operations/mco-pre-auth-job-details-form.md)**  
The pre-authorization job form enables you to enter the warranty, labor, and part details.
