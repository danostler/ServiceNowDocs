# Reviewing and approving the sales promotion claims

Agents workspace enables a claims agent to review and approve the sales promotion claims submitted by the dealer.

| | |
|---|---|
|[Approve all claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-approve-sp-claim.md)|Approve each claim case line.|
|[Reject a claim](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-reject-all-sp-claims.md)|Reject each claim case line.|
|[Partially approve a claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-partially-approve-spclaims.md)|Partially approve the claim case line.|

**Note:** Review and approve activity is visible to the claims agent. All other activities are performed by the dealer sales agent.

-   **[Approve all claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-approve-sp-claim.md)**  
As an OEM claims agent, approve all the claim request raised by the dealer.
-   **[Reject a claim](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-reject-all-sp-claims.md)**  
Reject a claim that was raised by the dealer.
-   **[Partially approve a claims](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/manufacturing/mco-partially-approve-spclaims.md)**  
Partially approve a sales promotion claim by updating the approved amount, which is less than the claimed amount.
