# Notes form

Notes form enables you to add the watch list details.

|Field| |
|-----|---|
|Watch list|Add names of users who receive notifications and can view the case task.|
|Watch notes list|Add names of users who receive notifications and can view the work notes of the case task.|
|Work notes \(Private\)|Enter the work notes.|
|Additional comments \(Customer visible\)|Enter additional comments for customer, if any.|
