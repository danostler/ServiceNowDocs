# View a publication on the Consumer Portal

View publications on the Consumer Portal to read targeted communications and download any associated attachments.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice.consumer or sn\_customerservice.unified\_consumer

## About this task

The **Publications** link on the Consumer Portal displays a list of current publications that is sorted by publication number.

**Note:** After a publication reaches its expiration date, it’s automatically removed from the Publications list.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the Consumer Portal.

2.  Navigate to **Resources** &gt; **Publications** in the header.

    This displays a list of current publications. The information displayed for each publication includes the title, publish date, expiration date, and a brief description.

3.  Search the Publications list by entering a search term in the search field.

4.  Select a publication name to open.

5.  Select the attachments to download from the pop-up window, if the publication has attachments.

6.  Close the pop-up window.

7.  Select the return arrow at the top of the publication to return to the Publications list.
