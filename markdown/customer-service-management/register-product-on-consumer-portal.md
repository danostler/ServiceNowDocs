# Register a product from the Consumer Portal

Register a product on the Consumer Portal to track it in your account and access product-specific support.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice.consumer

## Procedure

1.  Log in to the Consumer Portal.

2.  Navigate to **My hub** &gt; **Register product** in the portal header.

3.  Enter a search term in the search field and press Enter to display a list of products.

4.  Select the **Select** button on the card for the product.

5.  Enter the **Serial Number** and the **Purchase Date** for the selected product.

6.  Select **Register**.


## Result

After the product is successfully registered, it appears in the Manage products list.
