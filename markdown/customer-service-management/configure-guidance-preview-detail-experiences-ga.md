# Configure guidance detail experience

Configure guidance title and description that are displayed to users in recommended actions or playbooks.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_gd\_guidance.guidance\_manager

## About this task

The detail information appears in playbooks, or in a contextual side panel or in a subtab for Recommended Actions for Service.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Guided Decisions** &gt; **Guidances**.

2.  Select a guidance from the Guidances list.

3.  Configure the detail experience for the guidance in the Detail Experience tab.

    The detail experience is a message that is displayed for guidances that opens in the contextual side panel or in a subtab.

    1.  In the **Title** field, add a title for the guidance in the detailed view.

    2.  In the **Guidance Message** field, provide a message for the detail view.

4.  Select **Submit**.
