## Associate company records with a business location

Associate your company records with a business location to enhance service organization data model adoption across different industry verticals.

### Before you begin

Add the **Company** field to an internal or an external business location form using the form layout. For more information, see [Add fields to a form layout in Table Builder](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/application-development/form-builder-glide-family-release/add-form-fields.md).

Role required: admin

### Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Customer Service** &gt; **Service Organizations** &gt; **Internal/External Business Locations**.

2.  Select the internal or external business location from the list that you want to associate a company record to. 

3.  Select the company record previously created from the Company field.

4.  Select and hold \(or right-click\) in the header and select **Save**.


### Result

The company record is successfully associated with a business location.
