# Move forum content

Move community content posts from one forum to another.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.admin, sn\_communities.community\_moderator, sn\_communities.forum\_admin, sn\_communities.forum\_moderator

## About this task

Access to content is based on permissions to a specific forum. If content is moved, the author of the content and other community users might lose access to it, depending on their forum permissions.

**Note:** Draft content cannot be moved. If the content type is not supported in the new forum, it cannot be moved.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the community homepage.

2.  Click the content you want to move.

3.  Click **...** and **Edit**.

4.  Select the forum you want to move the content to.

5.  Click **Save**.

    An email notification is sent to the author of the content, subscribers to the content, and subscribers to the forum.
