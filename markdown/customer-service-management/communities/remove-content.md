# Remove content

Remove content that does not meet community standards.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Community** &gt; **Content** &gt; **Contents**.

2.  Click the content to remove.

3.  Click **Actions on selected rows** &gt; **Delete** and confirm that you want to delete the item.
