# Create a community profile field

Create a field to display on the community profile.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Community** &gt; **User Profile** &gt; **Profile Fields**.

2.  Click **New**and then complete the Profile Fields form.
