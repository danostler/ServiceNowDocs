# Approve forum memberships

Manage forum membership approvals when registered community users request membership to a forum.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.admin or sn\_communities.forum\_admin

## About this task

There are two ways of approving forum memberships.

## Procedure

1.  Perform one of the following actions.

    -   Navigate to **Community** &gt; **Forums**.
        1.  Choose a forum from the list that has been configured for membership.
        2.  Click the **Membership Approvals** related list.
    -   Navigate to **Self-Service** &gt; **My Approvals.**
2.  Accept or reject the membership requests.

    Only one approval is required for each task.

    Once the task has been approved or rejected, an email notification is sent to the user.
