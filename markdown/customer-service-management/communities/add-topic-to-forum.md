# Add a topic to a forum

Add a topic to a forum so that users can associate content to that topic.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_communities.forum\_admin

## About this task

A topic can belong to more than one forum.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Community** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Forums**.

2.  In the list, click the forum name.

3.  Click the **Forum Topics** related list.

4.  Click **New**.

5.  From the **Topic** list, select a topic.

6.  Click **Submit**.

    The selected topic is added to the Forum Topics list in the forum. The topic is available to users in this forum.
