# Review expense lines created from time cards

Review the expense lines created from the time cards and time sheets submitted by customer service agents.

## Before you begin

Role required: financial\_mgmt\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Cost** &gt; **Costs** &gt; **Expense Lines**.

2.  Select the desired expense from the Expense Lines list and view the details.
