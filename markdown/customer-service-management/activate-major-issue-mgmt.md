# Activate Major Issue Management

Activate the Major Issue Management plugin to use the major issue management feature.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## About this task

This plugin requires:

-   Customer Service \[com.sn\_customerservice\]
-   Targeted Communications \[com.sn\_publications\]
-   Task Communications Management \[com.snc.task\_communication\_management\]

Major issue management uses the Targeted Communications recipients lists feature to identify impacted customers.

**Note:** If you have installed the Proactive Customer Service Operations plugin, the Major Issue Management plugin is activated by default.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System Definition** &gt; **Plugins**.

2.  Search for the plugin com.sn\_majorissue\_mgt.

3.  Select **Activate**.
