# Configure an ongoing notification for email interactions

Configure an ongoing notification for email interactions. Configure the ongoing menu item on the toolbar in the CSM Configurable workspace for agents.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Advanced Work Assignment** &gt; **Settings** &gt; **Presence States**.

2.  From the Service channels section, from the Available list, select **Email** \(or a custom email channel\) and **Chat – Asynchronous**.

3.  Add the selected items to the Selected list.

4.  Select **Update**.
