# View team members assigned to an account

View the team members for a specific account.

## Before you begin

Role required: One of the following roles:

-   sn\_crm\_foundation\_admin
-   sn\_crm\_foundation\_data\_manager
-   sn\_crm\_account\_relationship\_data\_manager
-   sn\_crm\_consumer\_relationship\_data\_manager
-   sn\_crm\_consumer\_data\_manager
-   sn\_crm\_account\_data\_manager
-   sn\_customerservice\_agent
-   sn\_customerservice\_manager
-   admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Customer Service** &gt; **Customer** &gt; **Accounts** or **Partners**.

2.  Select an account.

3.  Select the **Account Team Members** related list.

    This list displays the employees who have been assigned to the team as well as their responsibilities.
