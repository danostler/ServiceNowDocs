# View a list of products from the Consumer Portal

View the list of products registered to your account from the Consumer Portal and access details for each product.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice.consumer

## Procedure

1.  Log in to the Consumer Portal.

2.  Navigate to **My hub** &gt; **Manage products** in the portal header.

    This displays a list of products registered to the current user.

3.  If required, select a product to display more information about the product.
