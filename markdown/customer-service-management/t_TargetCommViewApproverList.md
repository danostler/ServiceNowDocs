# View approvers for a publication

Access and review the assigned approvers for a publication sent for review in Targeted Communications.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_publications.author or sn\_publications.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to one of the following modules:

    -   **Targeted Communications** &gt; **Active Publications**
    -   **Targeted Communications** &gt; **Draft Publications**
2.  Open the desired publication.

3.  Select the tab for the **Approvers** related list.

    The Approvers list includes the users with the publications approver role that have been added as approvers to the publication workflow.
