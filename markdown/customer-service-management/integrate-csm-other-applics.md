# Integrating Customer Service Management with other applications

Extend the capabilities of Customer Service Management and connect with other departments to assist with case resolution by integrating with other applications.

Customer Service Management provides integrations with the applications listed in the following table:

|Step|Description|
|----|-----------|
|[Integrating withIT Service Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/integrating-with-it-service-management.md)|Integrate with Incident, Problem, Change, and Request Management applications.|
|[Integrating with Service Portfolio Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/csm-spm-integration.md)|Get visibility into sold products and their associated service offerings with this integration.|
|[Integrating with Continual Improvement Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/csm-continual-improvement-mgmt.md)|Get help with setting goals, organizing effort, and completing tasks.|
||Create and view all FSM work orders and appointments as a Customer Service Management user.|
|[Integrating with IT Operations Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/integration-event-proactive.md)|Create cases proactively from alerts and track the corresponding install base items affected by the alert.|
|[Integrating with Customer Project Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/csm-ppm-integration.md)|Use this integration to create projects and project tasks for your customer accounts.|
|[Integrating with Financial Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/csm-integration-financial-mgmt.md)|Allocate, track, and get report on expenses in your organization using this integration.|
|[Integrating with Adobe Experience Manager](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/aem-integration.md)|Deliver a pre-integrated web experience built on AEM by embedding Customer Service Management and Knowledge Management capabilities.|
|[Integrating with Computer Telephony Integration \(CTI\)](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/cti-csm-integration.md)|Enable the Customer Service application to support inbound and outbound telephone calls with this integration.|
|[Integrating with Microsoft Outlook](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/integrating-with-microsoft-outlook.md)|Manage contacts and cases from within Microsoft Outlook using this integration.|
|[Integrating with Safe Workplace applications](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/csm-integration-safe-workplace.md)|Enable contacts or consumers to safely enter locations and comply with health and safety policies after emergencies and pandemics|
|[Integrating with consumer messaging apps](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/messg-integrate-channels.md)|Let customers use their choice of messaging apps to message anytime to your company's account.|
|[Integrating with Process Mining](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/process-opt-csm.md)|Analyze processes relevant to your KPIs, and identify bottlenecks associated with customer service cases.|
|[Integrating with contact centers](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/contactcenter-integration.md)|Integrate with your contact center as a service \(CCaaS\) provider.|
