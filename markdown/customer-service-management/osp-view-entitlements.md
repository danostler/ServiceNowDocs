# View list of entitlements

As an OSP manager or agent, you must view entitlements for an account or consumer to create or fulfil cases.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_csm\_ocs.ext\_manager or sn\_csm\_ocs.ext\_agent

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Outsourced Customer Service** &gt; **Cases** &gt; **All**.

2.  Open a case and click the preview icon next to the **Account** field.

    The customer account page is displayed.

3.  Click **Entitlements** related list.

4.  Open an entitlement and view the asset details to fulfill a case.
