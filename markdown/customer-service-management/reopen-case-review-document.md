# Re-open a post case review

Re-open an approved post case review document if you need to make changes or update information.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_customerservice\_agent, sn\_customerservice\_manager, or admin

## About this task

## Procedure

1.  Open a customer service case.

2.  In the Related Records form section, access the record in the **Post Case Review** field.

3.  Click **Re-open** on the Post Case Review form.

    The status of the record reverts to **In Progress**.

4.  Make the necessary changes to the fields on the Post Case Review form.

5.  Click **Preview** to generate the review document and view it in the Preview Document pop-up window.

6.  Click **Request Approval**.

7.  Click **Publish to Case**.
