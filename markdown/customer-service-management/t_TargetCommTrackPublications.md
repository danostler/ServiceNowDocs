# Track a publication

Access a list of recipients for a publication and see which recipients have viewed the content.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_publications.author or sn\_publications.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Targeted Communications** &gt; **Active Publications**.

2.  Open the desired publication.

3.  Click the **Publication Recipients** related list.

    For each recipient on this list, you can see if the customer has viewed the article.
