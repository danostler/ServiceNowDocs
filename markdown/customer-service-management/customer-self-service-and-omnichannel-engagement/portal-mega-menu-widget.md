# Portal Mega Menu widget

The Portal Mega Menu widget displays a hierarchical menu on the header of the portal home page to enable navigation to different pages. You can also display a cart, wish list, guided tour, and scripted list such as notification, approval, and survey on the header of your portal.

\[Omitted image "mega-menu-widget.png"\] Alt text: Portal Mega Menu widget showing different pages on the portal home page

For more information about how to configure the Portal Mega Menu widget, see [Configure the category of a Portal Mega Menu widget](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/customer-service-management/customer-self-service-and-omnichannel-engagement/conf-cat-mega-menu.md).
