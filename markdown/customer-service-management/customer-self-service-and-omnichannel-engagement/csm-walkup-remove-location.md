# Remove a Walk-up Experience location

If you permanently close down a physical Walk-up Experience location, you can also remove the location from the Walk-up Location Queues table.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or sn\_csm\_walkup.walkup\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **CSM Walk-up Experience** &gt; **** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Walk-up Locations**.

2.  Click the name of the walk-up location you want to remove to open the location queue record.

3.  Click **Delete** in the form header.

    A confirmation pop-up appears ensuring you want to delete the record.

4.  Click **Delete**.

    The Walk-up Experience location queue is removed from the application.
