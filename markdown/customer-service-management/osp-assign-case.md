# Assign a case

Assign a case to yourself or another Outsourced Service Provider \(OSP\) agent within the organization to start working on the case or to fulfill the case.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_csm\_ocs.ext\_agent

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Outsourced Customer Service** &gt; **Cases** &gt; **All**.

2.  Click and open a case from the available list.

3.  Click **Assign to me**.

    **Note:** If the case is already assigned, the **Assign to me** button isn’t available.
