# Administer Customer Service Management

Use a number of features and tools to administer the Customer Service Management application.

|Feature or application|Description|
|----------------------|-----------|
|[Anonymous chat](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/anonymous-chat.md)|With anonymous chat, consumers can chat with a consumer service agent without logging in to the Consumer Service Portal.|
|[Customer Service Management Workflow Studio actions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/csm-flow-designer-actions.md)|Use Flow Designer actions as building blocks to create Customer Service Management business processes.|
|[Domain separation and Customer Service Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/domain-separation-customer-service.md)|Domain separation enables you to separate data, processes, and administrative tasks into logical groupings called domains. You can control several aspects of this separation, including which users can see and access data.|
|[Extension points in Customer Service Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/extension-points-customer-service.md)|Use extension points to call custom scripts in the Customer Service Management application.|
|[Knowledge product entitlements](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/c_KnowledgeProductEntitlements.md)|The Knowledge Product Entitlements application enables administrators to designate the knowledge bases and knowledge articles that customers can view from the Customer Service Portal.|
|[Limit knowledge base access to internal users](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/t_LimitKBAccessToInternalUsers.md)|Limit access to the information in a knowledge base to internal users.|
|[Quick start tests for Customer Service Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/quick-start-tests-csm.md)|Use quick start tests to validate CSM functionality after making any configuration changes, such as applying an upgrade or developing an application.|
|[Routing and assigning customer service cases](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/c_CaseRouting.md)|The case routing feature uses matching rules and assignment rules to identify customer service cases that meet certain conditions and then route those cases to customer service agents.|
|[Assignment workbench](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/case-assignment-workbench.md)|Customer service managers can use the assignment workbench to view a list of agents,evaluate skills and availability, and assign tasks efficiently and intelligently.|
|[Special handling notes](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/c_OnScreenAlerts.md)|Use the Special Handling Notes application to create notes that bring important information about individual records to the user's attention.|
|[Case and account escalation](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/customer-service-case-escalation.md)|Use the escalation feature to highlight specific cases or accounts and raise awareness of important customer issues.|
|[Administering case action status](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/customer-service-case-action-status.md)|Use the case action status feature to indicate the status of cases in the Case list. Agents can easily identify cases that need attention and quickly prioritize their work.|
|[Major issue management overview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/major-issue-management.md)|Major issue management enables customer communication for issues that impact a wider audience. Use this feature to proactively identify impacted customers, provide information to these customers, and manage the resolution process.|
|[Automatically close customer service cases](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/auto-close-customer-service-case.md)|Automatically close cases in the Resolved state if customers do not take any action.|
|[Administer Customer Contracts and Entitlements](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/customer-service-management/administer-pss.md)|Customer service managers can perform tasks to maintain service contracts, contract lines, and entitlements.|
