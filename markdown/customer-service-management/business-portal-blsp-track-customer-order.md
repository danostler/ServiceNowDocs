# Customer Orders form

The following table describes the fields available on the Customer Orders form.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Keyword Search|Search bar to filter customer orders using keywords like Order Number or Name.|
|Number|Unique identifier for each customer order.|
|Short description|Brief summary for the order.|
|Account|Customer account that is linked to the order.|
|Contact|Primary contact person for the order.|
|Channel partner|Channel partner associated with the customer order.|
|Order date|Date and time when the order was created.|
|Status|Current status of the order.|
