# ServiceNow pages in Adobe Experience Manager

Use pages provided on the ServiceNow site in the Adobe Experience Manager \(AEM\) as a reference to organize your AEM-based websites and provide a consistent user experience.

An AEM page houses containers, which in turn contain components. By manipulating the layout of the page and the components within it, you can construct a consistent user experience.

By default, ServiceNow sites and pages aren't accessible to public users, which are anonymous AEM users who are not logged in. For information about how to publish them and make them available to anonymous AEM users, see the Adobe documentation topic [Publishing pages](https://helpx.adobe.com/in/experience-manager/6-5/sites/authoring/using/publishing-pages.html).
