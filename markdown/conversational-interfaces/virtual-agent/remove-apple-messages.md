# Remove the integration for Apple Messages for Business

To remove the integration of Apple Messages for Business with your ServiceNow® instance, you need to log in to your Apple Messages for Business account, and then unlink your Apple Messages for Business partner account from your ServiceNow® instance.

## Before you begin

-   Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Go to [Apple Messages for Business](https://register.apple.com/messages) \(register.apple.com\).

2.  Click **Messages for Business Accounts** to go to your business account.

3.  Select the Business Account that you want to unlink from your ServiceNow® instance.

4.  Click **Test your Messaging Service Provider connection**, and **Connect**.

    You are redirected to your Now Support portal. Log in if necessary.

5.  On the **Remove Apple Messages for Business Account connection** page, click **Delete connection**.

    A new tab opens to confirm you want to remove the connection.

6.  Click **Remove connection**.

7.  Upon successful deletion, you are redirected to your the instance homepage and shown this message: “Business account registration was successfully deleted."
