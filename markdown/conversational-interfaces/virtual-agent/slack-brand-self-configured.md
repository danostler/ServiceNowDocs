# Configure branding for your self-configured bot

Configure your self-configured Slack app's branding. You have the flexibility to customize the default ServiceNow branding for the Slack self-configured bot name and icon.

## Before you begin

Ensure that you have completed integrating Virtual Agent with a Self-configured bot in Slack before customizing the branding options.

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Log in to the [Slack admin center](https://app.slack.com/).

2.  Select **Your apps** and select a bot from for which you want to change the branding settings.

3.  Navigate to **Settings** &gt; **Basic Information** &gt; **Display Information**.

4.  In the Display Information section, you can update the following details:

    1.  **App name**: Name of your Self-configured bot.

    2.  **Short description**: A short sentence describing your Self-configured bot.

    3.  **App icon &amp; Preview**: Image representing your Self-configured bot on the conversation body.

        **Note:** Ensure that the image you would like to upload as the app icon is in squares between 512 px by 512 px and 2000 px by 2000 px.

    4.  **Background color**: Background color of your bot icon while on the conversation body.

    5.  **Long description**: Description of your Self-configured bot to show on your App Detail page.

5.  Select **Save Changes**.
