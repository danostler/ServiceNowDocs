# Exploring Conversational Interfaces Console

The Conversational Interfaces applications enable your users to engage with live agents, virtual agents, or both. These interfaces provide tools for creating and managing optimal conversational experiences for your users, in the chat channels that they prefer.

If you are new to Conversational Interfaces and haven't implemented or used Virtual Agent yet, you can access the Conversational Interfaces Console application to explore all available features.

For existing users, you can access the Conversational Interfaces console application to discover added enhancements or advanced-level features.

## Conversational Interfaces console advantages

Implement Conversational Interfaces to support your users with virtual and live agents through the chat channels they prefer. Increase the productivity of your IT organization by deflecting the more common, easier to resolve user issues, to a 24/7-available Virtual Agent bot. The Conversational Interfaces console makes it easy for you to quickly get started and work towards achieving positive outcomes with your implementation.

Navigate through an intelligent experience that is uniquely tailored to where you are as a Conversational Interfaces user. The Conversational Interfaces console is ideal for first-time users who have never installed or configured Conversational Interfaces, to the most experienced user looking to expand full capabilities and maintain existing implementations.

Conversational Interfaces console provides the following features:

-   Guided experience modules that walk you through essential tasks, such as adding your bot to a portal, branding and customizing your bot, setting up bot greetings, collecting metrics, and more.
-   Access to helpful resources and videos to help you plan your virtual or human agent conversational experience.
-   General settings for both Virtual Agent and Agent Chat, as well as settings specific to each application.
-   Bot performance metrics for recent conversations.
-   Progress panel that shows you what has been configured on your instance.

Navigate to **All** &gt; **Conversational Interfaces** &gt; **Home** to start exploring its features.

-   **[Channels in Conversational Interfaces](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/conversational-interfaces/ci-channels-overview.md)**  
The various ways in which your end users engage with virtual and live agents are called channels. In Conversational Interfaces, users and agents converse through channels that have specific communication modes: chat mode for real-time conversations and messaging mode for long-running conversations.
