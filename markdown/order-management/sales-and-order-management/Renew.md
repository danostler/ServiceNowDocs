# Renew

Renew customer contracts, contract lines, and entitlements.

Renewing a customer contract, contract line, or entitlement initiates the configured renewal workflow and generates a new renewal record as applicable. The workflow carries forward relevant terms, pricing, and relationships to ensure continuity. Renewal maintains life cycle history while enabling ongoing service and billing processes.
