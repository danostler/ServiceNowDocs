# Channel Partner components on workspace

Partner Overview

</td><td>

Information associated with the channel partner. You can view the following information in the **Overview** tab of the record.-   Point of contact - Designated point of contact for the channel partner.
-   Staff - Staff member associated with the channel partner.
-   Products installed - List of products that are installed at the channel partner location.
