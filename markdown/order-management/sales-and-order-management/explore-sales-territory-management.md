# Sales Territory Management​

Sales territory management enables you to structure and optimize the sales efforts of your organization based on territories. It focuses on dividing CRM entities, such as accounts, leads, opportunities, and consumers, into manageable segments \(geographical, product, or industry-based\) to promote efficient coverage and accountability.

## Sales Territory Management​ overview

Sales Territory Management​ executes and refines the territory plan so that the sales representatives cater to the correct leads and opportunities, optimizing sales efforts. It involves overseeing daily sales activities, adjusting team assignments to complete customer coverage, monitoring sales performance against goals, and updating territory assignments as markets change. Sales Territory Management​ plugin supports managing and distributing accounts, consumers, leads, and opportunities based on conditions to different sales territories.

## Sales Territory Management​ users

|User|Description|
|----|-----------|
|Sales Territory Admin|Manage and view sales territory and CRM like Account, Consumer, Lead, and Opportunities.|
|Sales Territory Viewer|View sales territory.|
|Sales Territory Manager|Manage members and their roles in the territory hierarchy.|

## Sales Territory Management​ benefits

|Benefits|Details|
|--------|-------|
|Efficient territory allocation|Helps allocate sales regions efficiently to match business complexity and sales team distribution to avoid team overload.|
|Equitable distribution|Verifies fair distribution of sales among sales representatives. Automating territory assignments reduces manual tasks, enabling sales agents to focus more on selling.|
|Clear sales accountability|Assigning defined accounts or regions clarifies sales responsibilities and improves team accountability.|
|Segmentation|Based on the potential of each territory, you can effectively organize sales teams, avoid competing priorities for sales representatives, and set targets accurately.|
|Data security and compliance|Territory-based data sharing rules promote only authorized users access specific records, maintaining security and compliance.|
|Enhanced reporting and forecasting|Offers insights on sales team performance, enabling better forecasting and resource allocation decisions.|
|Adaptability and scalability|Adapts easily to organizational changes for long-term effectiveness.|
|Systematically rule-based approach|Offers a structured, rule-based method ideal for managing large sales teams efficiently.|
