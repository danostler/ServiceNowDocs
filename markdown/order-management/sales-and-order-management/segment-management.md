# Segment Management

Group entities effectively based on value, performance, or relevance using the Segment Management application \(com.snc.segment\_mgmt\) to make sure that all customers or entities are treated equally.

Define segments and set specific criteria levels to limit the ability to differentiate support levels, incentives, or even visibility. Segmentation enables organizations to engage entities effectively and offer incentives based on the criteria mapped for each segment.

Create and manage various segments to enable structured customer groupings. You can assign different segment structures to different programs and define criteria to drive consistent customer progression.
