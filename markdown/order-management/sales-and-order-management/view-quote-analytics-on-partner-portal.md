# View quote analytics on Partner portal

View detailed analytics related to all the quotes associated to a channel partner on the Partner portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_prm\_qm.quote\_partner\_ui along with and one of the following roles:

-   sn\_prm.external\_partner\_manager
-   sn\_prm.partner\_sales\_manager
-   sn\_prm\_sales.partner\_b2b\_sales\_rep
-   sn\_prm\_sales.partner\_b2c\_sales\_rep

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the Partner portal.

2.  From the portal header, select **Sales** &gt; **Quotes**.

3.  In the **Quote Analytics** you can view an overview of all the quotes by stage and revenue.\[Omitted image "quote-analytics.png"\] Alt text: Quote analytics page
