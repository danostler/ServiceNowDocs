# Summarize a quote using quote summarization

Summarize a quote to generate a consolidated report of a quote record to quickly understand the quote without manually reviewing multiple fields, line items, or related records.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_sales\_common.sales\_agent

## Procedure

1.  In the CSM Configurable Workspace, select the List \[Omitted image "list-outline-24.svg"\] Alt text: icon.

2.  Navigate to **Quotes** &gt; **All** and select the quote to summarize.

3.  In the **Quote summary** field, select **Summarize**.

    \[Omitted image "na-cpq-summarize-quote.png"\] Alt text:

    The **Quote summary** field appears above the Quote section inside the **Details** tab. The component is collapsed by default and expands to display the summary. For longer summaries that don't fit in the window, select **Show more** and use the scroll bar to view the rest of the content.

    **Note:** Generating and displaying the summary may take several seconds.

4.  When you're finished summarizing a quote, you can save it to the work notes, expand or collapse it, provide feedback, copy it, or view information about it.
