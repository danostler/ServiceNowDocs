-   **[Create job requisitions](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=request-job-hiring&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Create a job requisition for a vacancy that you want to fill in your organization.

-   **[Track job requisition](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=job-req-details-re-workspace&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Track a job requisition and view the job details, associated applications, hiring team, interview phases, approvers, and prospects.

-   **[Manage hiring team](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=manage-hiring-team&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Add, edit, or remove hiring team members from a job requisition.

-   **[Add job description](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=add-job-description&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Manually add a description to a job requisition or use predesigned templates to fill it out faster.

-   **[Manage the job requisitions as a recruiter](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=manage-job-reqs&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Update or duplicate a job requisition, put it on hold, send it for approval, or close it from the Recruitment workspace.

-   **[Tracking an application](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=application-record-details-re-workspace&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Track an application for a job requisition and its corresponding applicant and interview details as a recruiter or recruitment coordinator.

-   **[Hiring tasks](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=applicant-tasks-recruitr&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Create and assign tasks to your job applicants or internal stakeholders, and manage them within the Recruitment workspace.

-   **[Collaborate with the hiring manager](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=collab-hiring-manager&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Collaborate with the hiring manager on the job requisition in the activity stream.

-   **[\[Placeholder link text to key bundle-emplsm.send-targeted-emails\]](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=send-targeted-emails&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Send targeted emails to the internal and external talent types from different access points in the Recruitment workspace for effective communication.

-   **[View the Talent Acquisition Dashboard](https://servicenow-staging.fluidtopics.net/access?context=access-kpi-ta&family=zurich&ft:locale=en-US)**

Understand the hiring trend in your organization with the Talent Acquisition dashboard.
