# Using ITOM/OT SU Licensing

Ensure optimal utilization of your organization's ITOM subscriptions by analyzing the resource usage of your ITOM products.

## Using ITOM/OT SU Licensing overview

Explore how to view and track subscriptions for ITOM applications, analyzing resource usage against allocated subscription units to ensure optimal utilization. Verify the count of Configuration Items \(CIs\) used for ITOM subscriptions and identify which CI types specifically require ITOM subscription units. Finally, generate lists showcasing the current countable CIs for each ITOM application, allowing you to visualize their consumption of subscription units.
