# Netware discovery

Discovery identifies and classifies information about Netware.

## Data collected

**Note:** See the knowledge article [KB0687582](https://support.servicenow.com/kb_view.do?sysparm_article=KB0687582) for information on model\_id and manufacturer.

|-----|----------|----------|------|
|Name|cmdb\_ci\_netware\_server|name|snmp|
|Serial number|cmdb\_ci\_netware\_server|serial\_number|snmp|
|OS Version|cmdb\_ci\_netware\_server|os\_version|snmp|
|RAM|cmdb\_ci\_netware\_server|ram|snmp|
