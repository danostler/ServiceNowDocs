# Create a shift form in Service Reliability Management

Set up an on-call schedule for your team to ensure that the right person is always available to quickly respond to incidents and critical alerts.

## Create a shift form

For more information, see [Create an SRM on-call schedule](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-operations-management/service-reliability-management/sr-create-on-call-schedule.md).
