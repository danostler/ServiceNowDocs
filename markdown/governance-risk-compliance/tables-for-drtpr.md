# Tables installed with Digital resilience third-party registers

Specific tables are installed with Digital resilience third-party registers.

|Table label|Name|Description|
|-----------|----|-----------|
|Contract|\[ast\_contract\]|Stores information for the contract records.|
|Company|\[core\_company\]|Stores information for the core company records.|
|Assessment of the ICT service|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_assessment\]|Stores the assessment of the ICT service for DORA regulation requirement.|
|Branch|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_branch\]|Stores the branch records for DORA regulation requirement.|
|Digital operational resilience choice|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_choice\]|Stores the Digital operational resilience choice tables for DORA regulation requirement.|
|Contractual arrangement|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_contract\]|Stores the contractual arrangement records for DORA regulation requirement.|
|Digital operational resilience core information|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_core\_info\]|Stores the Digital operational resilience core information for DORA regulation requirement.|
|Legal entity|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_entity\]|Stores the legal entity records for DORA regulation requirement.|
|Entity making use of the ICT services|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_entity\_using\_service\]|Stores info about the entity making use of the ICT services.|
|Function|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_function\]|Stores the function records for DORA regulation requirement.|
|ICT service supply chain|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_ict\_service\_supply\_chain\]|Stores the ICT service supply chain records for DORA regulation requirement.|
|Entity signing contract to provide services|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_m2m\_entity\_providing\_service\]|Stores the entity signing contract to provide services|
|Entity signing contract to use services|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_m2m\_entity\_signing\_contract\]|Stores the entity signing contract to use services|
|Entity making use of services|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_m2m\_entity\_using\_service\]|Stores the entity making use of services|
|Intra-group contractual arrangements|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_m2m\_intra\_group\_contract\]|Stores the intra-group contractual arrangements|
|Third-party engagement signing contract to provide services|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_m2m\_third\_party\_engagement\_providing\_service\]|Stores the third-party engagement signing contract to provide services|
|Third party signing contract to provide services|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_m2m\_third\_party\_providing\_service\]|Stores the third party signing contract to provide services|
|Excel download/upload request|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_report\]|Stores the Excel download/upload request records for DORA regulation requirement.|
|ICT third-party service provider|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_third\_party\]|Stores the ICT third-party service provider records for DORA regulation requirement.|
|ICT third-party engagement service provider|\[sn\_dora\_accel\_third\_party\_engagement\]|Stores the ICT third-party engagement service provider records for DORA regulation requirement.|
