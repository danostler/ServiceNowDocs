# Close the assessment

Close the inactive Importance and impact tolerance assessment in the Operational Resilience Workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_oper\_res.manager

## About this task

The owner of the assessment typically closes the inactive assessments.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Operational Resilience Workspace** &gt; **List** &gt; **Importance and impact tolerance assessment** &gt; **All assessments**.

2.  As the owner, select the inactive assessment you wish to close from the list.

3.  To close the assessment, select **Close**.

    The state of the assessment is updated to **Closed**.
