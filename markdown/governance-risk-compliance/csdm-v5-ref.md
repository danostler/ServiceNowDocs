# Tables relevant to CSDM

Reference topics provide additional information about CSDM, including the associated roles, scheduled jobs, and tables. This section lists the tables relevant to CSDM.

## Tables for CSDM

|Table label and name|Description|
|--------------------|-----------|
|Application Service \[cmdb\_ci\_service\_auto\]|Application service which extends the Service table.|
|Business process \[cmdb\_ci\_business\_process\]|Business process and its details stored in the table.|
|Business service \[cmdb\_ci\_service\_business\]|Business service and its details stored in the table.|
|CI relationships \[cmdb\_rel\_ci\]|Configuration item \(CI\) relationships table.|
|Contract \[ast\_contract\]|Details of the Contracts table.|
|Contract CIs \[contract\_rel\_ci\]|Details of the Contract Configuration item \(CIs\) table.|
|Core company \[core\_company\]|Details of the Core company table.|
|Entity types \[sn\_grc\_profile\_type\]|Details of the Entity types table.|
|Location \[cmn\_location\]|Details of the Location table. Includes location details of the object.|
|Pillars \[sn\_grc\_choice\]|Details of the Pillars table.|
|Profile \[sn\_grc\_profile\]|Details of the Profile table.|
|Profile profile \[sn\_grc\_m2m\_profile\_profile\]|Details of the type of relationship configured in the Many-to-many Profile table. The table contains all the history dependencies.|
|Profile profile type \[sn\_grc\_m2m\_profile\_profile\_type\]|Details of the type of relationship configured in the Many-to-many Profile type table.|
|Service \[cmdb\_ci\_service\]|Details of the Service table.|
|Service Offerings \[task\_service\_offering\]|Details of the Service Offerings table.|
|Sys relationships \[sys\_relationship\]|Details of the System relationships table.|
|Vendor \[sn\_risk\_asmt\_vendor\_engagement\]|Details of the vendor engagement covered in the Vendor table.|
