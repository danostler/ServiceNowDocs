# Overview page of a breach assessment

After a breach assessment is completed, the overview page of the assessment displays the summary of the relevant and critical information for the privacy analyst to review.

The following sections are displayed in the overview page of a breach assessment.

-   Description: Displays a brief description or summary of the breach.
-   State: Displays the **Review** state the of the assessment when the privacy analyst must review the assessment.
-   Impact summary: Displays key information such as the number of individuals impacted by the breach, the categories of the breach such as visual, verbal, electronic, or paper, and the impacted jurisdictions and regions.
-   Impact details: Displays in detail the items captured in the Impact summary widget.
