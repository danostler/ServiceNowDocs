# Review and close a case task

Review a case task after a case task owner provides their responses and observations for the case.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_privacy\_case.privacy\_case\_analyst

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Privacy Management** &gt; **Privacy Workspace**.

2.  From the **Tasks** module, open the task to review.

3.  Review the case task and then do one of the following.
