# Modify email templates

You can modify the email templates to align the messages with your organization’s branding, or to add disclaimers required by regional regulations.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_grc\_pdr.pdr\_admin

## About this task

The following email notifications are sent to the requesters when they use the external facing PDR form:

-   **Verification email**

    Sends verification code to validate their email address.

-   **Acknowledgment email**

    Notifies requesters that their privacy request has been received and is being processed.

-   **Proof of authorization email**

    Requests proof of authorization from authorized agents who submit requests on behalf of someone.


## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System Mailboxes** &gt; **Outbound** &gt; **Outbox**.

2.  Select the email template \(verification, acknowledgment, or proof of authorization email\) that you want to add.

3.  After making the changes, select **Preview email** and review the changes.

4.  Save the changes by selecting **Update**.
