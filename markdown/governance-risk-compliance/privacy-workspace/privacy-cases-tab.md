# Privacy cases tab

The Privacy cases tab on the privacy management dashboard serves as a centralized interface for monitoring, analyzing, and managing privacy-related incidents and requests within an organization.

The Privacy cases tab on the privacy management dashboard helps organizations achieve and maintain compliance with data protection regulations. It supports adherence to laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation \(GDPR\). It also includes the California Consumer Privacy Act \(CCPA\) and other global privacy frameworks.

The dashboard empowers privacy managers, analysts &amp; compliance teams, to manage cases efficiently. It helps quickly identify critical cases needing immediate attention. The dashboard also reveals emerging trends and recurring issues over time. It uses historical data analysis and graphical visualizations for deeper insights. You can evaluate cases using multiple criteria like severity and priority. They can also review the breach status and root cause for each case. This view helps enable effective resource allocation and timely risk mitigation.

By consolidating case data into an intuitive and interactive interface, the Privacy Cases Dashboard enhances operational efficiency. It supports data-driven decision-making. It fosters a proactive, rather than reactive, approach to privacy incident management. It helps to ensure that all privacy-related inquiries, complaints, or breaches are addressed in accordance with internal policies and external regulatory requirements.

This dashboard displays the following widgets.

-   **Needs attention**

    This top-level summary section highlights cases that require immediate or near-future attention. It comprises three tiles: Overdue cases, Cases due in 7 days, and Unassigned cases. You can drill-down into the privacy cases from each card.

-   **Case overview**

    This section offers a high-level graphical breakdown of existing cases based on various parameters like by state, by breach status, by priority. You can drill-down into cases from the chart.

-   **Cases**

    This section displays cases by subtypes by default. You can filter the cases by impacted business units or by locations.

-   **Cases by primary cause**

    This section displays a breakdown of cases based on their root or primary cause.

-   **Opened and closed cases in last 12 months**

    This widget offers historical context by showing trends in opened and closed privacy cases over the past year. This graph helps identify patterns and workload over time.

-   **Issues**

    This widget displays a pie chart summarizing open issues related to privacy cases. The pie chart is categorized by severity levels like Green \(low\), Yellow \(medium\), Orange \(high\), and Red \(critical\). This section enables teams to monitor the volume and severity of issues, confirming timely follow-up and resolution. You can drill-down into cases from the chart.


You can create a processing activity, privacy case, or an issue using the **Create** button. Additionally, you can filter the privacy cases assigned to all analysts or me.
