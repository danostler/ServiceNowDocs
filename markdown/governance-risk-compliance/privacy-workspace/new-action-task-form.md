# New action task form

Use the new action task form to create action tasks for the appropriate owners.

The following table lists the descriptions of the field values.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Name|Name of the action task. For example, `Verification of Requester's Data and Disclosure of Usage`.|
|Description|Brief description of the action task. For example, `Verification of Requester's Data and Disclosure of Usage: Specify Locations and Purposes of Personal Information Utilization`.|
|Priority|Priority of the task.|
|IT asset|IT asset that is used in the request.|
|Assignment group| |
|Assignment|
|Assignment group|Group responsible for working on the task.|
|Task owner|User who owns the task.|
|Watch list|Users who must be notified of the task and its progress.|
|Schedule|
|Due date|Date for completion of the task.|
|Summary|
|Observations|Any observations of importance for the task owners.|
|Activity|
|Additional comments|Additional comments or information for the task owners.|
