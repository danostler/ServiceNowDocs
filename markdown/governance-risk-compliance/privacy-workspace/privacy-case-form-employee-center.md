# Employee Center privacy case creation form

On the new privacy case form in the Employee Center, fill in the fields as appropriate.

<table id="table_rsx_syv_nvb"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Title

</td><td>

Name of the case. For example, `Customer records compromised by unprotected back-up database`.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Description

</td><td>

Brief description of the case.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Case type

</td><td>

Nature or type of the case. This field is automatically set to **Privacy case**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Case subtype

</td><td>

Subtype of the case. For example, **Documentation Loss**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Date of occurrence

</td><td>

Date the case occurred. For example, the case may have occurred on 18-02-2022.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Date of discovery

</td><td>

Date the case was discovered by you. For example, the case may have occurred on 18-02-2022, but was discovered only on 12-03-2022.

</td></tr><tr><td class="sub-head" colspan="2">

Personal information

</td></tr><tr><td>

Categories of data subjects/individuals impacted

</td><td>

People impacted by the case. The choices are:-   **Customers**
-   **Employees**

</td></tr><tr><td>

Number of impacted individuals

</td><td>

Number of people impacted by the case.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Select the personal information attributes involved

</td><td>

Attributes of personal information that have been compromised in the case.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Provide additional personal information

</td><td>

Details of other personal information that is impacted by the breach.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Select the key stakeholders who own this application

</td><td>

Key stakeholders of the application.

</td></tr><tr><td class="sub-head" colspan="2">

Case origin

</td></tr><tr><td>

Impacted business unit

</td><td>

Business unit that is impacted by the breach.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Impacted department

</td><td>

Department impacted by the breach.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Where the case occurred \(location\)?

</td><td>

Location of the case occurrence. For example, the location is **Japan**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Where the case occurred \(Sub-location\)?

</td><td>

Sub location of the case occurrence. For example, the sub location is **Tokyo**.

</td></tr></tbody>
