# Regulatory agency form

Use the Create Regulatory Agency form in the Privacy Workspace to create a regulatory agency. The regulatory agency is a new record type that handles regulatory changes.

<table id="table_aqy_k2x_rvb"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Name

</td><td>

Name of the regulatory agency. For example, `Australian Prudential Regulation Authority`

</td></tr><tr><td>

Summary

</td><td>

Brief summary of the regulatory agency. For example, `The Australian Prudential Regulation Authority (APRA) is a statutory authority of the Australian Government and the prudential regulator of the Australian financial services industry`.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Description

</td><td>

Detailed description of the regulatory agency.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Acronym

</td><td>

Acronym of the regulatory agency, if any. For example, `APRA`

</td></tr><tr><td>

Logo

</td><td>

Logo of the regulatory agency.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Executive owner

</td><td>

Person who is the head of the agency.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Website link

</td><td>

Link to the official website of the agency

</td></tr><tr><td class="sub-head" colspan="2">

Classification

</td></tr><tr><td>

Category

</td><td>

Category for classifying the regulatory agency. The choices are as follows.-   **Regulatory**
-   **Legislative**
-   **Governmental**
-   **Others**

</td></tr><tr><td>

Scope

</td><td>

Areas in which an agency operates and its specific products or services. For example, **Bill**, **Decisions**, **Circulars**. You can select more than one scope in this field.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Sector

</td><td>

Segment of the economy where different agencies or businesses engage in similar activity or provide similar products or services. For example, **Banking**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Theme

</td><td>

Subject that is the core idea on which the agency is focused. For example, **Anti-money laundering**.

</td></tr><tr><td class="sub-head" colspan="2">

Key dates

</td></tr><tr><td>

Establishment date

</td><td>

Date the agency was established. Future dates are not allowed in this field.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Created

</td><td>

Date the agency is created.

</td></tr><tr><td class="sub-head" colspan="2">

Settings

</td></tr><tr><td>

Functional domain

</td><td>

Functional domain of the regulatory agency. This field is automatically set to **Privacy** when the regulatory agency is created from the Privacy Workspace.

</td></tr></tbody>
