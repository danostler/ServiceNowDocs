# Compliance Case Management

Report, investigate, analyze, and resolve a compliance case or raise a compliance request by using the ServiceNow® GRC: Compliance Case Management application.

The Compliance Case Management application helps you to report, investigate, and resolve compliance cases, such as complaints and breaches. The Compliance Case Management application provides a highly configurable workflow that is based on a compliance case or request.
