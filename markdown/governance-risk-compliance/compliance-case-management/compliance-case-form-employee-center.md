# Report a Compliance Case form

Use the Report a Compliance Case form in the Employee Center to report a compliance case.

See the following table for a description of the field values.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Name|Name of the case. For example, Verdict of a lawsuit leaked.|
|Description|Brief description of the case.|
|Case type|Type of the reported case. This field is automatically set to **Compliance case**.|
|Case sub-type|Subtype of the reported case. For example, Data theft/Loss.|
|Date of occurrence|Date when the case occurred. For example, the case may have occurred on 18-02-2024.|
|Date of discovery|Date when the case was discovered. For example, the case may have occurred on 18-02-2024, but is discovered by you on 12-03-2024.|
|Case origin|
|Impacted business unit|Business unit that is impacted by the reported case or event.|
|Impacted department|Department that is affected by the case. For example, Customer support.|
|Location of occurrence|Location of the case occurrence. For example, the location is Japan.|
|Sub-location of occurrence|Sub-location of the case occurrence. For example, the sub-location is Tokyo.|
|Add attachments|
|Add attachments|Choose a reference file or copy and paste clipboard files|
