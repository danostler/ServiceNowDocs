# Install GRC content packs and integrations

All ServiceNow integrations are available on the ServiceNow Store. Core applications, such as Policy and Compliance Management, are visible in the **ServiceNow Products** tab on the store. Content pack and integration add-ons are visible in the **Certified Apps** tab.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

Sore installation requires a Now Support account and have permission to request applications for the instances under consideration.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **System Applications** &gt; **All Available Applications** &gt; **All**.

2.  Locate the application you want to install and click **Install**.

    -   If you are installing the app to a sub-production instance, the application installs on the instance.
    -   If you are installing the app to a production instance, and you have a valid license \(that is, you have entitlement to the application\), the application installs on the instance.
    -   If you are installing the app to a production instance, and you do not have a valid license, a message is shown. Perform the following steps.
    1.  Click **Find in Store** to open the ServiceNow Store.

    2.  Locate the application and click its tile.

    3.  Click **Get** and enter your Now Support login credentials.

    4.  Click **Request Install**.

        **Note:** For store applications developed by ServiceNow, the **Get** button is shown.

    5.  Click **Request**.

        Enter the instance name and a reason for the request, then click **Request**. You are sent an email with detailed installation instructions.

    6.  Navigate to **System Applications** &gt; **All Available Applications** &gt; **All**.

        A list of applications available for installation is displayed.

    7.  Click **Install**.

        Locate the application, select it, and click **Install**. Your application is automatically installed on your instance.
