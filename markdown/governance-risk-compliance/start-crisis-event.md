# Start a crisis event

Unlike an exercise event, a crisis event strikes suddenly. Your business must be equipped to face the challenge and must be able to recover the critical business functions quickly to save you from business loss.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_bcm.planner, sn\_bcm.program\_manager

## About this task

You may not have a plan ready for a crisis as it strikes suddenly without prior notice. However, you can have an event ready and add tasks to the crisis event depending on the requirement.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Business Continuity** &gt; **Business Continuity Workspace**.

2.  Click the **Start a Crisis Event** link in the **Actions** section.

3.  On the modal form, fill in the fields.

    |Field|Description|
    |-----|-----------|
    |Short description|Name of the crisis event.|
    |Assigned to|Auto-populates the name of the user who created the crisis event. As a BCM program manager you can update the field.|
    |Description|Detailed description of the crisis event.|

4.  Click **Submit**.

    The crisis event is created and is in **Pending** state.
