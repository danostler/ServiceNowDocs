# Tables installed with AI Risk and Compliance

Tables are added with the activation of the AI Risk and Compliance application.

See the following table for a description of the tables.

<table id="table_kf4_nqk_qjb"><thead><tr><th>

Table

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

AI system

 \[sn\_grc\_ai\_gov\_ai\_system\]

</td><td>

Captures AI use cases submitted via the Employee Center, serving as a central repository for all submitted requests.

</td></tr><tr><td>

AI system entity

 \[sn\_grc\_ai\_gov\_ai\_system\_entity\_map\]

</td><td>

Maintains entity information for AI systems created through the intake form, including linked details about associated AI models and datasets.

</td></tr><tr><td>

AI system task

 \[sn\_grc\_ai\_gov\_ai\_system\_task\]

</td><td>

Tracks AI impact assessments and system-related tasks, such as Legal Review, Architecture Review, Security Review, and other required evaluations.

</td></tr><tr><td>

AI case

 \[sn\_ai\_case\_mgmt\_ai\_case\]

</td><td>

Consolidates AI-related cases and inquiries, providing a comprehensive view of all AI engagements and support requests.
