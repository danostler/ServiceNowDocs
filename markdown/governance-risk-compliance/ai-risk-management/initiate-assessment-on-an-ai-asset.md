# Initiate AI assessment on an AI asset

Initiate AI assessment for the whole AI asset.

## Before you begin

Role required: AI Asset Owner or AI Risk and Compliance Analyst \[sn\_grc\_ai\_gov.ai\_risk\_and\_compliance\_analyst\]

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **AI Risk and Compliance** &gt; **AI Risk and Compliance Workspace**.

2.  Select the list icon \[Omitted image "list-icon-airc-ws.png"\] Alt text:.

3.  From the List, open the AI asset for which you want to initiate AI assessment.

4.  For the More actions menu, select **Initiate assessment**.

5.  On the Send AI assessment dialog box, fill in the fields.
