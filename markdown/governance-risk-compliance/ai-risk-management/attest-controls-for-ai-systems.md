# Create control attestations for AI asset

Create control attestations for AI assets to document the existence, implementation, and effectiveness of controls that govern the AI asset’s behavior, data usage, performance, and risk posture. These attestations serve as formal evidence that the AI asset adheres to relevant regulatory, ethical, security, and operational standards.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_grc\_ai\_gov.ai\_risk\_and\_compliance\_analyst

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **AI Risk and Compliance** &gt; **AI Risk and Compliance Workspace**.

2.  Select the list icon \[Omitted image "list-icon-airc-ws.png"\] Alt text:.

3.  From the Lists, open the AI asset for which you want to create control attestations.

4.  Navigate to **Applies to** &gt; **Controls**.

5.  Select the controls from the list.

6.  Select **Attest**.

    **Important:** You can create attestations only for controls in the Draft or Monitor state.
