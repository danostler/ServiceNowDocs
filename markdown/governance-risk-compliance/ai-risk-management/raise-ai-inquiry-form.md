# Raise an AI inquiry form

Use the Raise an AI inquiry form in the Employee Center to submit your questions about AI systems, models, or datasets and their business applications.

See the following table for a description of the field values.

<table id="table_l35_zwl_kkb"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td class="sub-head" colspan="2">

Details

</td></tr><tr><td>

Name

</td><td>

Unique name for the AI inquiry. For example, `Am I allowed to use sensitive personal data for training our AI model, and what precautions should be taken to ensure data privacy?`

</td></tr><tr><td>

Description

</td><td>

Description about the AI inquiry in detail. For example, `Gaining clarity on the permissible use of sensitive data and the necessary measures to safeguard it during AI model training.`

</td></tr><tr><td>

Priority

</td><td>

Urgency of the AI inquiry. It allows the AI inquiry team to triage requests effectively and responds based on how critical the request is. The options are as follows:-   **Critical**
-   **High**
-   **Moderate**
-   **Low**
-   **Planning**

</td></tr><tr><td>

Requested By

</td><td>

Name of the person who raised the request.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Requested on behalf of

</td><td>

Name of the person that you created the request for.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Business Unit

</td><td>

Business unit that is affected by the AI inquiry. For example, Sales.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Location

</td><td>

Location where the request was raised. For example, Japan.
