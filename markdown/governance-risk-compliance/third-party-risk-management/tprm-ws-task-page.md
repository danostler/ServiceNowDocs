# TPRM Task page

The task page gives you access to tasks that are assigned to you and to members of your group. You can further filter the lists of tasks by due diligence requests, type of third-party action, and by risk or tiering assessment.

## Accessing the page

To open the Task page, select **Workspaces** &gt; **Vendor Management Workspace** and on the **Risk** tab, select the task icon \[Omitted image "icon-tprm-ws-tasks.png"\] Alt text:.
