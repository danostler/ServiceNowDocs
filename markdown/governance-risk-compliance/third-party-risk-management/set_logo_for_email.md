# Update the header and footer for email notifications

Update the header and footer images used in email notifications by modifying image records for Third-party Risk Management.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## About this task

You can update the header and footer images that appear in email notifications. These images are shared across email notifications and templates. Any changes to **sn\_vdr\_risk\_asmt.tprm\_email\_header.png** or **sn\_vdr\_risk\_asmt.tprm\_email\_footer.png** apply to all notifications using TPRM templates. To verify which templates are in use, navigate to **All** &gt; **System Notification** &gt; **Email** &gt; **Email notifications**.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **System UI** &gt; **Images**.

2.  Update the header or footer image by completing one of these actions.
