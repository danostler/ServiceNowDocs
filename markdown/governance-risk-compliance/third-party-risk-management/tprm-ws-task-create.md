# Create a task for a third party or engagement

Create a task to help ensure that a user at your organization or the third-party contact responds to your concerns about questionnaire responses or requested documents during the due diligence process.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_vdr\_risk\_asmt.vendor\_assessor

## About this task

In an iterative process, before the TPR manager closes an assessment, the TPR manager can generate non-compliance issues and tasks. The TPR manager communicates with the TP contacts and engagement contacts by using comments to close the issues and tasks. The TPR manager can also assign different contacts as needed.

## Procedure

1.  In the **Quick actions** box on the TPRM home page \[Omitted image "icon-tprm-ws-home.png"\] Alt text:, select **Create task**.

2.  Fill in the form and then select **Save**.
