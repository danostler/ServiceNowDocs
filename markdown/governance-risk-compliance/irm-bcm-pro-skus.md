# Applications provided with IRM Pro and BCM Pro SKUs

This section outlines the applications required for the IRM Pro SKU \(stock keeping units\) and BCM Pro SKU \(stock keeping units\).

## Applications provided with IRM Pro SKU and BCM Pro SKU

-   **IRM Pro SKU**

    The IRM Pro SKU contains the following applications.

    1.  GRC: Advanced Risk
    2.  GRC: Policy and Compliance Management
    3.  GRC: Risk Management
-   **BCM Pro SKU**

    The BCM Pro SKU contains the GRC: Business Continuity Management applications.
