# Start an exercise event

Create an exercise event to test your business continuity and recovery plans on a planned date and monitor the completion of the event tasks.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_bcm.planner, sn\_bcm.program\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Business Continuity** &gt; **Business Continuity Workspace**.

2.  Click the **Start an Exercise** link in the **Actions** section of the workspace.

3.  On the modal form, fill in the fields.

Method to implement the event. The methods can be Simulation, Tabletop, or Walkthrough.-   Simulation: An environment that simulates an actual disaster with necessary equipment, supplies, and personnel.
-   Tabletop: An exercise done in a room with a team analyzing over the plan, looking for gaps.
-   Walkthrough: Each member of the team walks through a component of the plan in detail to identify weaknesses.
-   Functional: Option to include ad hoc tasks that are not part of a plan.
