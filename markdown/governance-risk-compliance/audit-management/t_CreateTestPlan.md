# Create an audit test plan

Test plans can be created from scratch or based on test templates and describe how a feature is to be tested.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_audit.user, sn\_audit.external\_auditor

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Audit** &gt; **Audit Testing** &gt; **Test Plans**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.
