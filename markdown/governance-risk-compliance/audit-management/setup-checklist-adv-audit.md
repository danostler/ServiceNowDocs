# Setup checklist for the GRC Advanced Audit application

Complete the checklist that includes the setup tasks required to complete in your ServiceNow AI Platform® instance. When you have completed these tasks, the base system is ready for operation.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

Consider creating and printing a PDF of this checklist topic. You can then check off tasks as you complete them.

## Procedure

1.  To generate a PDF, click the save as PDF icon \(\[Omitted image "pdf.png"\] Alt text: Save as PDF icon.\) at the top of the topic.

2.  Click the **Selected topic**.
