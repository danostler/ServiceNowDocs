On the Create New Company form, fill in the fields for the third party.

Computed risk rating

</td><td>

Calculated risk rating. This field is auto-filled.

 The value is overall risk rating for the company, calculated after the assessment by rolling up all of the TPR assessors' risk ratings.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Override risk rating

</td><td>

Option to override the computed risk rating.

 If you select the check box, then specify the following values:

-   **Overridden risk rating**: Select the new risk rating.
-   **Overridden on**: Date that overridden risk rating or justification is updated.
-   **Justification**: Enter the reason for overriding the value.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Overridden risk rating

</td><td>

Rating that has been manually changed by a user, overriding the rating that was calculated by a system. Available options are:-   **1 - Very High**
-   **2 - High**
-   **3 - Moderate**
-   **4 - Low**
-   **5 - Very Low**

</td></tr><tr><td>

Overridden on

</td><td>

Date when the overridden risk rating or its justification was last updated.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Justification

</td><td>

Reason for overriding the risk rating.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Assessment risk rating

</td><td>

Risk level for the assessment. This field is auto-filled.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Engagement risk rating

</td><td>

Risk level for the engagement. This field is auto-filled.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Subsidiary risk rating

</td><td>

Risk level for the subsidiary. This field is auto-filled.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Risk intelligence rating

</td><td>

Score that reflects the probability and impact of a risk event. This field is auto-filled.
