# Business Continuity Management

ServiceNow® Business Continuity Management application gives your organization the capability to continue to deliver products and services at an acceptable level when a disruptive incident occurs. The ongoing activities of this application are aimed to reduce the operational risks and improve your organizational ability to respond, react, and recover from issues and disruptions.

## Business Continuity Management overview

The Business Continuity Management product provides a framework to perform continuous management activities. The Business Continuity Management application helps your organization to achieve the objectives:

-   Establish a clear governance structure.
-   Define a consistent classification of business processes, interdependencies, and recovery objectives based on impact analysis and risk to the business.
-   Develop and provide continuous improvement to the business continuity plans.
-   Identify, analyze, and manage process continuity during and following a disruptive event.

For more information on Business Continuity Management, see [Exploring Business Continuity Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/exploring-bcm.md).
