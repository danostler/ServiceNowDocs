# Assign the reported vulnerability to an analyst

Assign a reported vulnerability to an analyst in the Operational Resilience Workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_oper\_res.admin

## About this task

Administrators of the Operational Resilience Workspace can assign a reported vulnerability to an analyst within the application.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Operational Resilience Workspace** &gt; **Unassigned Operational Vulnerabilities**.

2.  Select the reported vulnerability from the list.

3.  Assign it to an analyst.

    Details on the form are shown in the example.

    \[Omitted image "op-vul-saved.png"\] Alt text: Details.

4.  Select **Save**.
