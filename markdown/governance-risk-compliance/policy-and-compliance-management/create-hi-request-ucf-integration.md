# Create a Now Support Case for UCF-CCH account integration information

After establishing your UCF CCH account, use the Now Support Service Portal to initiate the account integration process.

## Before you begin

Sign up for a UCF CCH account and customize your basic subscription to include API Access. A user with the UCF administrator role must log into the CCH account and generate the API key.

**Note:** The previous arrangement for free access to UCF content inclusive of your GRC license ended November 30, 2018. All customers must purchase a subscription from Unified Compliance directly.

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  After activating the Compliance UCF plugin, sign in to the [Hi Service Portal](https://support.servicenow.com/nav_to.do?uri=%2Fhisp).

2.  Click **Get Help**.
