# Deactivate a control objective

Deactivate control objectives that are no longer relevant to their citation or parent control objective.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_compliance\_admin or sn\_compliance\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Policy and Compliance** &gt; **Policies and Procedures** &gt; **Control objective**.

2.  Open a control objective.

3.  In the control objective, clear the check box marked **Active**.

4.  Click **Update**.
