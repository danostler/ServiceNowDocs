# Work with acknowledgements using the Service Portal

After you have received a request to provide acknowledgement for a policy, you have the option of working with the request in the ServiceNow AI Platform, or you can perform your actions in the ServiceNow Service Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_grc.business\_user, sn\_grc.business\_user\_lite, sn\_grc\_emp\_user.grc\_employee

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the ServiceNow Service Portal.

2.  In the header bar, navigate to **GRC** &gt; **My Policy Acknowledgements**.

    All pending policy acknowledgement requests assigned to you appear.

3.  To view details of the request, click **View**.

    \[Omitted image "view-policy-ack.png"\] Alt text: View a policy acknowledgement request on the service portal
