# Policy Exception Overview Performance Analytics dashboard

The Policy Exception Overview Performance Analytics dashboard provides views into the number, severity, and source of policy exceptions. It also shows exempted controls.

## Policy Exception Overview dashboard

**Important:** Starting with version 18.1.1 of the Policy and Compliance Management application, the Policy Exception Overview dashboard is available in the Next Experience UI Framework.

If you are on Vancouver or Washington DC, you can view the dashboard in the Next Experience UI Framework.

\[Omitted image "policy-excep-overview-db-pc.png"\] Alt text: Policy Exception Overview dashboard in the Next Experience UI Framework

The following roles and tasks are associated with this solution:

-   Audit Manager \(sn\_policy.manager\): To have clear visibility into the overall state and volume of vulnerabilities within the organization.
-   Audit Administrator \(sn\_policy.admin\): To pinpoint areas of concern quickly.
-   Audit Analyst \(sn\_policy.user\): To prioritize which risks or tasks to focus on based on criticality to the organization.

To open the dashboard, navigate to **All** &gt; **Policy and Compliance** &gt; **Policy Exceptions** &gt; **Analytics Overview**.
