# Enable the GRC Approval Configurator

Enable the GRC Approval Configurator so that you can manage policy exceptions and extensions with granular, multi-level approval flows.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_compliance.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Policy and Compliance** &gt; **Properties**.

2.  Locate the **Enable GRC Approval Configurator instead of legacy and verification rules for policy exception** property, and select **Yes**.

3.  Select **Save**.
