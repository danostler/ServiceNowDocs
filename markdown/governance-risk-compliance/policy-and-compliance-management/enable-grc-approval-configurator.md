# Enable GRC Approval Configurator

Enable the GRC Approval Configurator from the Policy and Compliance Properties page.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_compliance.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Policy and Compliance** &gt; **Properties**.

2.  Select **Yes** for the **Enable GRC Approval Configurator instead of legacy and verification rules for policy exception** property.

3.  Select **Save**.
