# Create a GRC article template

Policy and Compliance managers can create templates for policy article publishing.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_compliance.developer

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Policy and Compliance** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Article Templates**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.
