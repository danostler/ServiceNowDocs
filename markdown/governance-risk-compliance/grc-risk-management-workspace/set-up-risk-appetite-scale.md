# Set up a risk appetite scale

Set up a risk appetite scale in the Advanced Risk application to define the qualitative risk appetite within your organization.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_risk.admin

## About this task

Use a risk appetite scales to define the qualitative risk appetite on the risk statement, entity, and risk records. The risk appetite scales that are shipped by default are as follows:

-   **1-Averse**
-   **2-Minimalist**
-   **3-Cautious**
-   **4-Open**
-   **5-Hungry**

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Advanced Risk Assessment** &gt; **Administration** &gt; **Risk Appetite Scales**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.
