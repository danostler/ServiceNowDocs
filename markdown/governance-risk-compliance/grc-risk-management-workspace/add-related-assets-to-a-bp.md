# Add related assets to a business process

Add related business assets to a business process to gain visibility into the IT assets and their performance.

## Before you begin

Role required: Enterprise architect or service owner or itil

## About this task

A business process is supported by various assets such as business applications, network servers, application servers and their services and so on. It is important that a process owner is able to establish that relationship and then have visibility into these IT assets and their performance. For example, assume that employee leave management is a business process. This business process depends on an application to automate it. The application, in turn, depends on servers to run it. Thus, if you relate the assets to a process, you can track the overall performance of your processes.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk Workspace** &gt; **Lists** &gt; **Business Processes**.

2.  Open the business process to which you want to add assets and click the Related assets related list.

3.  Click **Add**.

    The Related Assets pop up appears.

4.  Select the related assets you want to add to the business process.

5.  Click **Add**.

6.  Click **Save**.
