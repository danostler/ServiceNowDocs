# Create a risk framework in the Risk Workspace

Create risk frameworks to group risk statements into manageable categories and generate risks. After the risks are generated, you can identify methods to mitigate them.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_risk.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk** &gt; **Risk Workspace** &gt; **Library** &gt; **Risk frameworks**.

2.  Click **New**.

3.  On the form, fill in the fields.
