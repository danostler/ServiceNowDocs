# Copy a factor

Create a copy of a published factor to make minor modifications and then reuse the factor.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_risk.admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Advanced Risk Assessment** &gt; **Administration**

2.  Open the factor you want to copy.

3.  Click **Copy**.


## Result

A copy of the factor is created.
