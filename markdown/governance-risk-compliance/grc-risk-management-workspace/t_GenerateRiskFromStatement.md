# Generate a risk from a risk framework

Making associations with risk frameworks automatically creates risks.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_risk.admin and sn\_risk.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk** &gt; **Risk Library** &gt; **Risk Framework**.

2.  Open the risk framework record.

3.  In the **Entity Type** related list, click **Edit**.

4.  Select the entity types to associate to the risk framework, and click **Save**.

    All risk statements are associated to the entity type and a risk is created for every risk statement against every entity in the entity type.
