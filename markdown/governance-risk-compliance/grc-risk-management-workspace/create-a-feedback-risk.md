# Create feedback in Advanced Risk

Create feedback on a record or fields in a record in the Advanced Risk application. The feedback enables you to recommend improvements related to data integrity, compliance, operational procedures, and other pertinent areas.

You can create feedback by using one of the following options:

-   [Create feedback in the Risk Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/governance-risk-compliance/grc-risk-management-workspace/create-feedback.md)
-   [Create Feedback from the record side panel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/governance-risk-compliance/grc-risk-management-workspace/create-feedback-from-record-side-panel.md)

-   **[Create feedback in the Risk Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/governance-risk-compliance/grc-risk-management-workspace/create-feedback.md)**  
Create feedback on a record or fields in a record, to recommend improvements related to data integrity, compliance, operational procedures, and other pertinent areas, such as disposition and accountability.
-   **[Create Feedback from the record side panel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/governance-risk-compliance/grc-risk-management-workspace/create-feedback-from-record-side-panel.md)**  
Create feedback on a record or fields in a record from the record side panel using the Advanced Risk application. The feedback enables you to recommend improvements related to data integrity, compliance, operational procedures, and other pertinent areas.
