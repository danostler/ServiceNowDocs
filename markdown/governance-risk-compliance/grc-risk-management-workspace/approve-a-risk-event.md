# Approve a risk event

A user with the sn\_risk.manager role must approve a valid risk event. If any risk approver rejects the event, the state of the risk event changes to rejected.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_risk.manager

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **My Approvals**.

2.  Click the record in the list with your name as the approver.

3.  Click **Approve**.
