# Respond to an application questionnaire

Respond to a questionnaire about an application for risk assessment.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_grc.business\_user or sn\_grc.businesss\_user\_lite

## About this task

The Risk Identification form contains multiple stages for the workflow. When an application is created in GRC as an entity, a questionnaire is sent to the IT application owner. The questionnaire is useful for risk managers to identify threats and mitigating controls.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Self-Service** &gt; **My Assessments &amp; Surveys**.

    Alternatively, you can also navigate to **Risk identification classic questionnaires** or **Risk identification questionnaires** from the My Tasks page to access the questionnaire. Select **Risk identification questionnaires** for the smart assessment questionnaire.

2.  Open the record with the assessment that you must respond to.

3.  Enter your responses.

4.  Select **Submit**.


## Result

The state of the risk identification record moves from the Information Gathering state to the Review state.

## What to do next

The IT Risk Manager can review the responses and can either send the questionnaire back or accept the responses.
