# Reopen a closed risk event

Reopen closed risk events to identify and address overlooked or underestimated risks, updating existing risk events instead of creating another risk event.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_risk\_advanced.risk\_event\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk** &gt; **Risk Workspace**.

2.  Select the list icon \[Omitted image "list-icon-riskworkspace.png"\] Alt text: List icon. and navigate to the closed risk events.

3.  Open the risk event that you want to reopen.

4.  Select **Reopen**.

5.  Select a reason and provide justification in the **Additional Comments** field.

6.  Select **Confirm**.

    The risk event moves to the Analyze state.
