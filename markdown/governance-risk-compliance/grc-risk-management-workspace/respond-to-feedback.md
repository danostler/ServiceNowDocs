# Respond to the feedback

Provide comments or explanations regarding the submitted feedback using the Advanced Risk application. You can either remediate or reject the feedback, provided that there’s proper justification.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_rvw\_feedback.respondent

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk** &gt; **Risk Workspace** &gt; **My tasks**.

2.  Open the Feedback that you want to respond.

3.  To respond to the feedback from the record side panel, select the list \[Omitted image "side-panel-list-icon.jpg"\] Alt text: side panel list icon. icon on the record and do the following:

    1.  Provide comments or explanations regarding the feedback submitted in the **Response** field.

        **Response** field is required to reject a feedback item.

    2.  Select **Remediate** or **Reject**.

4.  To respond to the feedback on the record, navigate to the **Response** section and do the following:

    1.  Select the response in the **Response** field.

    2.  Provide comments or explanations regarding the feedback submitted in the **Respondent comments**.

        The **Respondent comments** field becomes required when you select the response as **Rejected**.

    3.  Select **Save**.

5.  To respond to the feedback items, select the **Items** related list and do the following:

    The **Items** related list appears only when the reviewer adds feedback items while providing the feedback.

    1.  Select the feedback items.

    2.  Select the response in the **State** field.

    3.  Provide comments or explanations regarding the feedback submitted in the **Respondent comments**.

        The **Respondent comments** field becomes required when you select the response as **Rejected**.

    4.  Select **Save**.

6.  Select **Send for review**.

    You must close all the open items to send the feedback to the reviewer.
