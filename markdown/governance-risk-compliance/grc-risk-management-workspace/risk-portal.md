# GRC Portal

The GRC Portal is a simplified interface that enables the GRC business users to view their tasks and their group's tasks. The GRC Portal also enables users to report risk events.

To access the GRC Portal, navigate to **Risk** &gt; **Risk Portal**. To use this portal, you must have the sn\_grc.business\_user role. On the Risk Portal you can do the following:

-   **My pending tasks**: View the tasks that are pending and are assigned to you. For example, if you have a risk assessment assigned to you, you can launch it from the GRC Portal.
-   **My group's tasks**: View the tasks assigned to your group.
-   **My items**: View the requests you have raised. For example, issues, policy exceptions, remediation tasks and so on.
-   **Report**: Report a risk event. To see how you can report a risk event from the GRC Portal see, [Report a risk event from the Risk Portal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/governance-risk-compliance/grc-risk-management-workspace/report-risk-event-risk-portal.md)
