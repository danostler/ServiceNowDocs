# Crisis Management map

You can integrate the crisis management application with the Crisis Management map to receive alerts as the alert feeds on possible threats in continuing the business operations of your organization.

A threat feed is the data intelligence information of a potential or current threat that can pose a danger to an organization and stop its business routine or disable its business processes. Threat feeds can include but not limited to:

-   Severe weather or tornado
-   Hurricane
-   Earthquake
-   Wildfire or red flag warnings
-   Flood
-   Civil unrest or riots
-   War or military conflict

As a BCM administrator, you can enable or disable threat feed integrations provided by the base system. You can also configure additional threat feeds in to the Crisis Management application. The integration and configurations that you set up alert the crisis manager of the threats and aid to take appropriate action on time.
