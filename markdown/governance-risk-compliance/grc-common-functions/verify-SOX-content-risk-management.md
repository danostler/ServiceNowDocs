# Verify the SOX Content Pack in Risk Management

After importing the SOX Content Pack, verify and edit the entity types, entities, risk statements, risks, and assessments within the Risk Management application.

## Before you begin

Role required: Risk Admin \(sn\_risk.admin\)

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Risk** &gt; **Scoping** &gt; **Profile Type**.

    A **SOX Processes** entity type is added and is used to generate the SOX entities:

    -   Accounts Receivable
    -   Accounts Payable
    -   Commissions
    -   Equity
    -   Entity Level
    -   Financial Reporting
    -   Fixed Assets
    -   General Ledger
    -   Human Resources
    -   In Scope Application
    -   Inventory
    -   ITGC
    -   Payroll
    -   Purchasing
    -   Revenue Recognition
    -   SAP
    -   SDLC
    -   Tax
    -   Travel and Expense
    -   Treasury
2.  Under the Profile filters related list, a filter is added to query the SOX processes table for the generation of SOX profiles.

3.  Navigate to **Risk** &gt; **Risk Library** &gt; **Risk Statements**.

    All SOX risk statements have been added in the Draft state and the Risk Assessment is assigned to them.

4.  Navigate to **Risk** &gt; **Risk Register** &gt; **Risks**.

    All SOX risk statements have been added in the Draft state and the Risk Assessment is assigned to them.
