# Create New Agency form

Use the Create New Agency form in Compliance Workspace to create a regulatory agency. The regulatory agency is a new record type that handles regulatory changes or compliance case management.

## Create New Agency form

For a description of the form field values, see the following table.

|Field|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|Name|Name of the regulatory agency.|
|Summary|Brief summary of the regulatory agency.|
|Description|Detailed description of the regulatory agency.|
|Acronym|Acronym of the regulatory agency, if any.|
|Logo|Logo of the regulatory agency.|
|Executive owner|Executive owner of the regulatory agency.|
|Website link|Website link on which the regulatory agency is listed.|
|Additional details|
|Sector|Sector for classifying the regulatory agency.|
|Establishment date|Establishment date of the regulatory agency.|
|Type|Type for classifying the regulatory agency.|
|Theme|Theme of the regulatory agency.|
|Category|Category of the regulatory agency such as regulatory, legislative, assembly, or others. The default value is Regulatory as you’re adding a new regulatory alert.|
|Settings|
|Functional domain|Functional domain of the regulatory agency.|
