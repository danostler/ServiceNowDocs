# Orient target

After you've identified a target, orient the target to use with the NIST CSF framework.

## Before you begin

Policy and Compliance Management Role required: admin.

NIST CSF role required: sn\_irm\_nist\_csf.risk\_executive or sn\_irm\_nist\_csf.security\_officer

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **NIST CSF** &gt; **Framework Profiling** &gt; **Orient Targets**.

2.  Search for the target using the **Name** or **Profile** fields.

3.  Open the record.

4.  Verify that the **Framework** field has **NIST CSF** selected.

5.  Ensure that the NIST CSF form fields are accurate.

6.  Click **Update**.
