# Add a regulatory contact to an agency

Add a regulatory contact to a specific regulatory agency or within a jurisdiction in Compliance Workspace. By mapping a regulatory contact to a specific agency, this contact is notified about your case reporting, case audit, and case closures as they occur.

## Before you begin

Role required: compliance\_case\_manager

## About this task

As a compliance\_case\_manager, you can map one contact or multiple contacts to a regulatory agency. By default, all the regulatory contacts in the system are displayed. When you add a regulatory contact, you must analyze which jurisdiction it belongs to.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Compliance Workspace** &gt; **Lists** &gt; **Regulatory information** &gt; **Regulatory contacts**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  On the Create New Regulatory Contact form, fill in the fields.

    For a description of the fields, see [Create New Regulatory Contact form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/grc-common-functions/create-new-regulatory-contact-form.md).

4.  Select **Save**.


-   **[Create New Regulatory Contact form](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/governance-risk-compliance/grc-common-functions/create-new-regulatory-contact-form.md)**  
Use the Create New Regulatory Contact form in Compliance Workspace to create a regulatory contact to either a regulatory agency or a jurisdiction.
