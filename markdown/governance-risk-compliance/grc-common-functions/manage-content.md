# Manage content

The **Manage content** page displays all the ServiceNow links in your Microsoft Word report, including content inserted using the AI reporting assistant.

## Manage content

The Manage content page is accessible from the Document designer add-in in Microsoft Word. The **AI generated** section appears at the top of the page when AI-generated content is present in the document.

## AI generated section

The **AI generated** section displays cards for each piece of content inserted using the AI reporting assistant during a tracked conversation. Each card includes the following:

-   Title: the name of the AI-generated content, generated by the Microsoft Word reporting agent. Titles are limited to 255 characters. To view the full title, hover over the card.
-   Content type: the type of content inserted, displayed as part of the title for multi-source content. For example: Chart Name, Table Name, Story Name, or Data Name.
-   Link: the source table of the AI-generated content, aligned to the right of the card.
