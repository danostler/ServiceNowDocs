# Configuring your compliance needs and integration with regulatory content providers

Configure the Regulatory Change Management application to manage your compliance needs and integration with regulatory intelligence content providers.

A typical configuration of the Regulatory Change Management application consists of the following steps:

1.  Download the Regulatory Change Management application from ServiceNow Store. For more information, see [Download Regulatory Change Management application](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/governance-risk-compliance/regulatory-change-management-service-portal/activate-grc-rcm.md).
2.  Do the setup tasks for the Administration module. For more information, see [Manage Administration portal](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/governance-risk-compliance/regulatory-change-management-service-portal/manage-admin-portal.md).
