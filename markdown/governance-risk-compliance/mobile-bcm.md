# Managing plans with BCM mobile application

Business continuity plan \(BCP\) managers and viewers can manage and view plans using the Business Continuity Management \(BCM\) application on Android or iOS mobile devices.

## Plan execution with BCM mobile

The Business Continuity Management application extends plan execution to mobile, enabling you to review and act on continuity and recovery plans directly from your mobile devices. Starting with version 9.1.3 of the BCM Mobile application, you can verify that critical decisions and escalations can happen anytime, anywhere—even during a disruption.

The BCM mobile application provides these benefits:

-   Mobile plan execution: Review continuity and recovery plans from your mobile devices.
-   Access on mobile device: Maintain visibility and control even when connectivity is limited.
-   User-friendly interface: Use the streamlined workflow designed for simplicity in high-pressure scenarios.

BCM managers can generate PDFs of the approved plans, which BCM planners can then view and download.
