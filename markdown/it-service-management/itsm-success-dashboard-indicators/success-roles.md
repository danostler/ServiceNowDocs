# Success Dashboard indicators user roles and responsibilities

Leaders and process owners of both ITSM and HR can configure and use the Success Dashboard indicators to measure the success of the implementation.

The Success Dashboard indicators roles and the tasks are uniform across Service groups; ITSM or HR. The following are the user roles and responsibilities:
