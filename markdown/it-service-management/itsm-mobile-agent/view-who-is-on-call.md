# View on-call group members

View the members of your group that are available on call.

## Before you begin

Role required: Itil, sn\_incident\_write

## Procedure

1.  Open the ITSM Mobile Agent app.

2.  In the **On-call Schedule** tab, tap **Who is on-call**.

    All the available groups are displayed.

3.  Select a group and tap **Submit**.
