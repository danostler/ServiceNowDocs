# Settings

View the information related to the settings of your ITSM Agent mobile app.

You can adjust the settings of the ITSM Agent mobile app. The following information is available in the Settings screen of your app.

|Setting|Description|
|-------|-----------|
|Instance|Name of the app version|
|Version|Version of the app|
|Security|If the administrator has set authentication requirements, enter the secure credentials in the field.|
|Analytics|Enable the analytics of the app usage|
|Legal|Read the privacy policies, Open source licence information and End user licence agreement|
|Logout|Logout from the instance|
