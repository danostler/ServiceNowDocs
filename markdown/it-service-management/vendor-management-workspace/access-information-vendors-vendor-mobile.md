# Access information related to your vendors using the Vendor Management Mobile application

Look up information related to your vendors such as incidents or outages created for your vendors or breached SLAs.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_vlm.vendor\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Log into your Vendor Management Mobile application on your mobile device.

2.  Do any of the following.

**Information about your vendors**

Tap **My Vendors**.To contact a vendor, tap the vendor record and then tap **Primary Contact Details**. The vendor's contact appears.

</td></tr><tr><td id="d89519e91">

**Incidents created for your vendors**

</td><td>

</td><td>

</td></tr><tr><td id="d89519e121">
