# Enable Benchmarks

Configure the Benchmarks application for your company to enable your company to compare performance with industry peers. You can opt out of Benchmarks at any time.

## Before you begin

To keep the Benchmarks comparison data clean, you can opt in to Benchmarks only from your production instance.

Role required: sn\_bm\_client.benchmark\_admin

## About this task

Benchmarks is installed on your instance by default. It is also installed with the ITSM Success Dashboard application. Enable Benchmarks by opting in to the application.

Six months of global data is available after opting in. If available, the corresponding data for your instance is also included.

**Note:** Past data for your instance is only available for Benchmarks users upgrading from a previous release.

## Procedure
