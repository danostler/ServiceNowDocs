# Assign a survey to agents or groups

Assign a published survey to agents or groups so that they can take the survey.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_wfo\_cfg\_ws.manager

## About this task

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Manager Workspace**.

2.  Select the Coaching icon \(\[Omitted image "coaching-icon-uib.png"\] Alt text: Coaching icon\).

3.  Assign a survey to an agent or a group.

    **Note:** You can only assign surveys that are in the **Published** state.

    1.  Select **Assign Survey**.

        The **Assign a new survey** pop-up appears.

    2.  Assign the survey to an agent or a group.

        -   To assign the survey to a group, in the **Assign at group level** field, add the groups to which you want to assign the survey.
        -   To assign the survey to agents, in the **Assign to specific team members** field, add the individual members of the team to which you want to assign the survey.
    3.  Select **Assign**.

        The survey is assigned to the agent. When agents complete the survey, you can select **View Scorecard** and analyze reports from the survey.

    Agents receive a notification when a survey is assigned to them.
