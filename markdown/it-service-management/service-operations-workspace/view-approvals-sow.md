# View and approve records in Service Operations Workspace

Access the My Approvals in SOW to manage the approval requests for change, request, catalog task, and requested item.

## Before you begin

Role required: itil, sn\_change\_read, sn\_request\_read

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **Workspaces** &gt; **Service Operations Workspace**.

2.  Navigate to **List** &gt; **Approvals** &gt; **My Approvals**.

3.  Select a record to approve or reject the request.

4.  Select **Approve** to approve the request.

5.  Select **Reject** to reject the request.
