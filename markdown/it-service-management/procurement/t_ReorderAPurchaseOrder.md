# Reorder a purchase order

You can reorder a purchase order that was canceled.

## Before you begin

Role required: procurement\_admin or procurement\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Procurement** &gt; **Orders** &gt; **Purchase Orders**.

2.  Open a purchase order with a status of **Canceled**.

3.  Click **Order**.

    The status changes to **Ordered** for the purchase order and all associated purchase order line items.
