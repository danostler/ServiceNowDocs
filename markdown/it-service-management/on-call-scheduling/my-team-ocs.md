# Manage team with ITSM Mobile Agent

As a shift manager, view your team's schedule and perform activities like approving time-off requests and providing coverage.

|Action|Description|
|------|-----------|
|Tap **Upcoming shifts**|View all upcoming shifts for the current month.|
|Swipe left on the shift card|Request time off for this shift.|
|Tap the shift card|View details for the shift \(like group, roster, start time, and end time\).|
|Tap **Request time-off**|Request a time-off for this shift by providing information on the Request time-off screen.|
|Tap **Provide Coverage**|Provide coverage for a shift member.|
