# Playbooks for Financial Services Operations applications

Several playbooks are installed with Financial Services Operations applications that you can use to run your digitized business process.

By using [Process Automation Designer](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/build-workflows/workflow-studio/process-automation-designer.md), you can review and configure these predefined playbooks for your own business needs.
