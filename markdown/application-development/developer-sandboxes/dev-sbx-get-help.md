# Get help with Developer Sandboxes

To get help with Developer Sandboxes, your ServiceNow instance, plugins, permissions, and more, watch a short video to contact the ServiceNow admin who works in your company. You can also check some helpful resources.

## Get help now from your Now Support administrator

The ServiceNow admin in your company can help you with things like activating plugins, kicking off upgrades, unlocking accounts, updating user permissions, and much more.
