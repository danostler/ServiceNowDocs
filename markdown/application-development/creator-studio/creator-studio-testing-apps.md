# Testing and previewing Creator Studio apps and records

Before you request deployment for your app, you can test out submitting a form and interact with any associated automation results on the record it creates.

-   **[Test one of your forms for an app](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-development/creator-studio/creator-studio-try-it.md)**  
Use the **Try it** button on the **Request forms** tab to submit a request, which triggers any associated playbooks and creates a record that you can work with in Creator Studio.
-   **[Preview how an app's records appear](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/application-development/creator-studio/creator-studio-preview-record.md)**  
You can preview and interact with the submitted records generated by your app directly in Creator Studio, which mimics what the fullfiller sees in the workspace category for the app.
