# Bookmark apps and files in ServiceNow Studio

Easily bookmark apps and app files from several locations for quick access in ServiceNow Studio.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin or delegated developer

## About this task

**Note:** Bookmarks and favorites are distinct ServiceNow capabilities. Bookmarks exist only within ServiceNow Studio, while favorites are accessible throughout your instance. When you bookmark apps or files in ServiceNow Studio, your bookmarks do not automatically become favorites. Favorites also do not automatically become bookmarks.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **App Engine** &gt; **ServiceNow Studio**.

2.  Bookmark your apps and files from different locations in ServiceNow Studio.

    -   To bookmark an open app, select the Add to bookmarks icon \(\[Omitted image "sn-studio-bookmark-icon.png"\] Alt text: add to bookmarks icon\) next to the app name.

        \[Omitted image "sn-studio-bookmark-app.png"\] Alt text: Bookmark an app that's open in the canvas.

    -   To bookmark an app or file in the Navigator panel, hover over it and select the Add to bookmarks icon \(\[Omitted image "sn-studio-bookmark-icon.png"\] Alt text: add to bookmarks icon\).

        \[Omitted image "sn-studio-bookmark-file.png"\] Alt text: Bookmark an app or app file from the Navigator panel.

3.  View bookmarked apps and files in the **Bookmarks** tab of the Navigator panel.
