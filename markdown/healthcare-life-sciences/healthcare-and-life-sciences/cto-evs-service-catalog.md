# Service Catalog installed with Care Team Operations for Environmental Services

The Care Team Operations for Environmental Services service catalog is organized into a hierarchy of EVS-related categories and subcategories that care teams commonly report.

|Name|Short Description|Description|
|----|-----------------|-----------|
|Housekeeping and Sanitation Services|Request daily cleaning, terminal cleaning, and high-touch surface disinfection.|Includes requests for daily cleaning, terminal cleaning \(isolation room, standard discharge, enhanced/STAT\), and high-touch surface disinfection.|
|Waste Management Services|Request regulated medical waste disposal, general trash pickup, and recycling.|Encompasses regulated medical waste disposal \(sharps, trash room maintenance, biohazard pickup\), general trash pickup, and recycling requests.|
|Infection Prevention Support|Request enhanced disinfection and isolation/outbreak cleaning.|Covers enhanced disinfection requests and isolation/outbreak response cleaning for specific areas with detailed precautions.|
|Linen and Laundry Services|Request clean linen delivery and soiled linen pickup.|Involves clean linen delivery and soiled linen pickup requests, detailing linen type, quantity, and handling instructions.|
|Hazardous Materials Handling|Request chemical and biohazard spill cleanup.|Includes chemical and biohazard spill cleanup requests, specifying the nature, size, and urgency of the spill along with PPE requirements.|
|Facility Maintenance Support|Request post-maintenance cleanup and facility issue reporting.|Consists of post-maintenance cleanup requests and facility issue reporting, including area affected and issue description.|
|Patient Room Turnover Services|Request standard and emergency/STAT room turnover.|Encompasses standard and emergency/STAT room turnover requests, detailing room number, urgency, and specific cleaning instructions.|
