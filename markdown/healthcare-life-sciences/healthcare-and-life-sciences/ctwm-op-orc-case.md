# Input Orchestration case details into the Operational Rounding playbook

Complete the **Orchestration case** playbook stage in the Operational rounding playbook.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hco\_orc.loc\_support\_agent or sn\_hco\_orc.plan author

## Procedure

1.  In the **Operational rounding playbook**, navigate to the **Orchestraion case** stage.

2.  Fill in the following fields.
