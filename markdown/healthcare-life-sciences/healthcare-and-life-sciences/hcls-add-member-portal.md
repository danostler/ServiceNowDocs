# Add a team member using the Care Team Portal

Add a member to your team using the Care Team Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hco.care\_team\_member or sn\_hco.care\_team\_manager

## Procedure

1.  In the Care Team Portal, navigate to **My teams** &gt; **Add a member**.

2.  Select the team to which you want to add a member.

3.  Fill in the following fields.
