# Create healthcare organizations for your healthcare IT support teams

Create healthcare organizations to represent your fulfilling organizations for Healthcare IT cases.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Healthcare Operations** &gt; **Healthcare Organizations** &gt; **Hospital Departments**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  In the name field, enter a name that reflects your healthcare IT support organization.

    For example, Healthcare IT.

4.  Fill in the other fields as needed.

5.  Select **Submit**.


## What to do next

Assign members to your newly created healthcare IT support organization.
