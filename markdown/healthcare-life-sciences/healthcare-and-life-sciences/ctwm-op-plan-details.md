# Input plan details into the Operational Rounding playbook

Complete the **Plan details** playbook stage in the Operational rounding playbook.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hco\_orc.loc\_support\_agent or sn\_hco\_orc.plan author

## Procedure

1.  In the **Operational rounding playbook**, navigate to the **Plan details** stage.

2.  Provide a **Plan name**.

3.  Enter a **Plan description**.

4.  Select **Save**.
