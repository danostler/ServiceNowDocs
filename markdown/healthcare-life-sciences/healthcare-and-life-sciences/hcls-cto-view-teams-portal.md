# View your teams using the Care Team Portal

View your teams from directly within the Care Team Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_hco.care\_team\_member or sn\_hco.care\_team\_manager

## Procedure

1.  In the Care Team Portal, navigate to **My teams** &gt; **My teams**.

2.  Select the team that you want to view.
