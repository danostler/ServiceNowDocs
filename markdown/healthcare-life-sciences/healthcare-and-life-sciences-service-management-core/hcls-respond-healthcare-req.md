# Respond to healthcare requests

As a healthcare agent, you can respond to healthcare requests to resolve issues raised by a patient.

Patients can submit a healthcare request via a phone call or a chat. As a healthcare agent, you can respond to healthcare requests by accepting a work item from a patient or accessing the interaction records that store work items. For more information, see [Interaction Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/servicenow-platform/interaction-management/interaction-management.md).
