# Using Vaccine Administration Management

Learn how users, clinicians, and provider admins use Vaccine Administration Management.

-   [Use the Patient Portal to register for a vaccination program](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/hcls-vam-user-experience.md)
-   [Use Clinician Portal to find and manage vaccination appointments for users](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/hcls-vam-clinician-experience.md)
-   [Vaccine Administration Management dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/VAM-dashboard.md)
-   [Manage user appointments as a clinician or a vaccine agent](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/user-appt-mngt-clinicans-agents.md)

-   **[Use the Patient Portal to register for a vaccination program](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/hcls-vam-user-experience.md)**  
As a user with the sn\_vaccine\_sm.user role, you can register for a vaccination program, provide your personal information, schedule and manage your vaccination appointments, provide health updates, and more, all from within a single, self-service portal.
-   **[Use Clinician Portal to find and manage vaccination appointments for users](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/hcls-vam-clinician-experience.md)**  
As a user with the sn\_vaccine\_sm.clinician role, you can find vaccination appointments for users, view all of their upcoming appointments for a particular location, view, and work on a vaccination record, mark an appointment as a no-show, cancel an appointment, and more, all from within a single portal.
-   **[Vaccine Administration Management dashboard](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/VAM-dashboard.md)**  
Use the Vaccine Administration Management dashboard to view vaccine appointments by day, week, and month. You can view scheduled, completed, and no-show appointments, as well as filter appointments by the vaccine center, date, method, and clinician.
-   **[Manage user appointments as a clinician or a vaccine agent](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/vaccine-administration-management/user-appt-mngt-clinicans-agents.md)**  
Manage user appointments as a clinician or as a vaccine agent for creating consumer users, booking vaccination appointments, and choosing a desired slot. To manage user appointments better, some additional configurations are performed for the vaccine agent.
