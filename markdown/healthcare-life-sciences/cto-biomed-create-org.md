# Create healthcare organizations for your biomed support teams

Create a healthcare organization to represent the fulfilling healthcare organization for healthcare biomed cases.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Healthcare Operations** &gt; **Healthcare Organizations** &gt; **Hospital Departments**.

2.  Select **New**.

3.  In the name field, enter a name that reflects your biomed support organization.

    For example, Biomed Support.

4.  Fill in the other fields as needed.

5.  Select **Submit**.


## What to do next

Assign members to your newly created healthcare biomed support organization.
