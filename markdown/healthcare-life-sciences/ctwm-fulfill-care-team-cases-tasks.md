# Fulfill care team case and tasks

Fulfill the care team cases and tasks created from task plans.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_cto.care\_team\_agent or sn\_cto.care\_team\_agent\_manager

## About this task

For more information on care team cases and tasks, see [Care team cases and tasks](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/healthcare-life-sciences/ctwm-care-team-case.md).

## Procedure

1.  In the Healthcare Orchestration Workspace, navigate to **Care Team Case** &gt; **All Cases**.

2.  Update case details as needed.

    **Note:** Care team cases can be reassigned as needed.

3.  Select **Tasks** to view all associated care team tasks.

4.  Select a task and fulfill it as needed.

5.  Use **Close case** or **Close task** to close out the case or task.
