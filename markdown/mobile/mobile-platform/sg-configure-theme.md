# Create a theme for your mobile applications

Use mobile themes to give your applications a unique appearance that works with your company identity.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## About this task

**Important:** Use legacy mobile theming on Tokyo and earlier ServiceNow® instances. For Utah and later releases, see [Next Experience theming for mobile](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/mobile/mobile-platform/explore-ne-theming.md).

**Note:** Certain elements, such as banner text color, are not configurable with legacy theming and require Next Experience theming. For details, see [Next Experience theming for mobile](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/mobile/mobile-platform/explore-ne-theming.md).
