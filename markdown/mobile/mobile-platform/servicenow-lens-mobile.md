# ServiceNow AI Lens for mobile

ServiceNow AI Lens enables you to harness generative AI to scan, extract, understand, and synthesize data directly from your phone or tablet, making it easy to optimize your workflows on the go.

\[Omitted image "na-lens.png"\] Alt text: now assist generating troubleshooting steps based on an image

By capturing data from a variety of visual sources—including images, emails, handwritten notes, websites, and applications—ServiceNow AI Lens streamlines tasks like auto-filling forms, extracting information from multiple screenshots, and generating actionable insights.

ServiceNow AI Lens supports the following file types:

-   PDF
-   DOC
-   DOCX
-   XLS
-   XLSX
-   PPT
-   PPTX
-   JPG
-   JPEG
-   PNG

\[Omitted image "na-lens-autofill.png"\] Alt text: Forms that have been auto-populated.

ServiceNow AI Lens can extract information from images and directly enter the data into forms. To have ServiceNow AI Lens assist you, tap the Lens icon \[Omitted image "na-lens-icon.png"\] Alt text:. After attaching the image you want to extract information from, you can add additional instructions for how ServiceNow AI Lens should extract the data.
