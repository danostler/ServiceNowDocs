# Map screen

Map screens display a map with locations that are associated to the records in a data item.

<table id="table_gn2_g1q_nlb"><tbody><tr><td>

Use a map screen when you need your users be aware of the locations related to the records in your data item. For example, map screens can show your users where their assets are located, or which job locations they need to travel to.
