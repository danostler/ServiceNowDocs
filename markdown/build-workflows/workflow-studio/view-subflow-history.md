# View subflow history

View the change history of a subflow by date and user.

## Before you begin

Role required: flow\_designer or admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Process Automation** &gt; **Workflow Studio**.

2.  On the homepage, select **Subflows**.

3.  Open the subflow whose flow history entries you want to view.

4.  Select **Edit subflow**.

5.  From the sidebar view, select the **History** icon.


## Result

The sidebar view displays a list of subflow history entries.

## What to do next

You can annotate, copy, remove, or restore subflow history entries.
