# Zurich CRM and Industry Products

- [CRM and Industry Products](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/industry-products/industry-products-landing.md) -- ServiceNow Customer Relationship Management (CRM) helps you sell, fulfill and service on one unified platform. ServiceNow CRM consists of Sales Customer Relationship Management (Sales CRM), Field Service Management (FSM), and Customer Service Management (CSM) and their related industry extensions. Industry Products further extend the value of the ServiceNow AI Platform by productizing data models, frameworks, and workflows to address common experience challenges at scale.
