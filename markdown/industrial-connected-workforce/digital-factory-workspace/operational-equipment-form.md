# Operational equipment form

The following table describes the field values for the Operational equipment form.

<table id="table_ujp_34b_1gc"><thead><tr><th>

Field

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Name

</td><td>

Name for the piece of operational equipment that you’re creating.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Description

</td><td>

More detailed explanation of the equipment, including its purpose, location, or other characteristics.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Category

</td><td>

A high-level classification that groups equipment based on its general function or purpose within the manufacturing process.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Subcategory

</td><td>

A more specific classification within a category that provides detailed identification of the equipment type.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Model ID

</td><td>

Internal or system-generated identifier for the model of the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Model number

</td><td>

The manufacturer's model number. This field is used for identifying the version or configuration of the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Manufacturer

</td><td>

Name of the company that produced the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Correlation ID

</td><td>

Unique identifier used to link this equipment record with other systems or records.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Company

</td><td>

Organization that owns or is responsible for the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Managed By Group

</td><td>

Name of the group responsible for the management and maintenance of the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Change Group

</td><td>

Name of the group responsible for approving or implementing changes to the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Department

</td><td>

Department that uses or is assigned the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Support group

</td><td>

Name of the group that provides technical support or troubleshooting for the equipment.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Approval group

</td><td>

Name of the group that must approve actions related to the equipment. For example, for procurement or decommissioning.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Operational status

</td><td>

Operational status of the equipment. Options are:-   Operational
-   Non-Operational
-   Repair in Progress
-   DR Standby
-   Ready
-   Retired
-   Design

</td></tr><tr><td>

Hardware status

</td><td>

Hardware status of the equipment. Options are:-   Installed
-   In Maintenance
-   In Stock
-   Out of Stock
-   In Transit
-   Defective
-   In Disposition
-   Retired
-   On Order
-   Pending Install
-   Pending Repair
-   Pending Transfer
-   Stolen

</td></tr></tbody>
