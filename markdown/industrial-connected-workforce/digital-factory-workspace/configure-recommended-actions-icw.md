# Configure Recommended Actions for the Industrial Connected Workforce

Configure Recommended Actions for the Industrial Connected Workforce. Recommended Actions are based on context, rules, and recommendations and can be configured to support additional use cases. You can create rules and recommendations or modify existing ones to meet the specific requirements of your organization.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_nb\_action.next\_best\_action\_author or sn\_icw.application\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Recommended Actions** &gt; **Contexts**.

2.  Configure Recommended Actions for the Industrial Connected Workforce for either tasks, standard tasks, or rules for recommended actions.
