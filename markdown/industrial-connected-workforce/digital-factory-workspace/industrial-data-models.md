# Industrial Data Models

Use the Industrial Data Models to manage data models for the Industrial Connected Workforce \(ICW\).

-   **[Operational Equipment Model](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/industrial-connected-workforce/digital-factory-workspace/operational-equipment-model.md)**  
Use the Operational Equipment Model to define functional locations and equipment in accordance with the ISA-95 model.
-   **[Industrial Materials](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/industrial-connected-workforce/digital-factory-workspace/industrial-materials.md)**  
Use Industrial Materials to manage the materials used for the Industrial Connected Workforce \(ICW\).
