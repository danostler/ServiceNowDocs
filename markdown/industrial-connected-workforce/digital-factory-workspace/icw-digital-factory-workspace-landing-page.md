# Digital Factory Workspace

Digital Factory Workspace helps standardize processes and improve efficiency on the factory shop floor by bringing tasks, equipment details, and standards together in one place. It provides structured layouts and a Standards hub to support consistent task execution and better operational performance.

Digital Factory Workspace enables you to use access and use:

-   Standards hub for governing operational standards
-   Operational Equipment Model for managing ISA-95 hierarchies
-   Industrial Materials for organizing material classes
-   Manufacturing Standards, Industrial Guided Tasks, and knowledge articles across the shop floor environment.
