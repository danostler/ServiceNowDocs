# Assign roles and personas to groups for Service Exchange

Associate groups with Service Exchange Personas to grant all group members access to remote catalog items, instead of assigning users individually.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## About this task

Roles control which actions are available to users and grant access to remote record producers. In Service Exchange, you assign roles to groups rather than individual users. When a user's job description changes, their roles update automatically based on their group membership. You can also assign Service Exchange Personas to a group, which gives all group members access to the remote catalog items linked to those personas.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **User Administration** &gt; **Groups**.

2.  Select the group that you want to update.

3.  To assign roles and personas to a group, do the following steps.To assign roles to the group, in the Roles related list, select **Edit**, add the roles, and then select **Save**.

## Result

All members of the group can access the roles and remote catalog items linked to the assigned personas. Access updates automatically when group membership changes.
