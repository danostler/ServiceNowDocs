# Access the ServiceNow Store for a regulated environment

Log in to the isolated instance of the ServiceNow Store contained in your regulated environment.

## Before you begin

Role required: none

You must have access to Now Support in a regulated environment.

## Procedure

1.  Log in to Now Support for your regulated environment.

2.  Access the ServiceNow Federal Store by scrolling to the end of the page and selecting the **Visit the ServiceNow Federal Store** button.


## Result

You’re logged in to the federal or regional instance of the ServiceNow Store that corresponds with your regulated environment.
