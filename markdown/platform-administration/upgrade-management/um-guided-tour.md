# Guided upgrade

Upgrade your instance effortlessly with the guided process.

To streamline your upgrade process, follow the guided setup steps outlined in [Access guided upgrade on a non-production instance](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/upgrade-management/um-guided-tour-implement.md) and [Access guided upgrade on a production instance](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/upgrade-management/um-guided-tour-implement-prod.md). Before you begin, ensure you’re logged in as an administrator. This intuitive tool walks you through the necessary pre- and post-upgrade activities and the instance upgrade tasks, ensuring a seamless transition to the latest release.
