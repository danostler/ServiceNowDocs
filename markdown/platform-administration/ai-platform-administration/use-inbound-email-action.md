# Use Inbound email actions

Create inbound email actions and reprocess received emails.

-   **[Create an inbound email action](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/t_CreatingAnInboundEmailAction.md)**  
Create inbound email actions to define the actions that the system takes when an email is received.
-   **[Reprocess received emails](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/reprocess-received-emails.md)**  
When a email has been processed incorrectly, fix the underlying problem, then reprocess the email. You can reprocess emails individually or in a batch.
-   **[Sensitive data redaction](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/sensitive-data-redaction.md)**  
Redact sensitive data such as Social Security number, credit card details, email address, phone number, and other personal information from inbound emails to help protect data and privacy.
