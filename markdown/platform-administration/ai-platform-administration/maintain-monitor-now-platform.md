</table>## Instance Scan

Get detailed analysis of your instance configurations, and stop potential system health issues before they arise. Use ServiceNow's Instance Scan any time and ensure system stability, whether you are developing, releasing, or upgrading your application. Schedule, execute, and track scans from single-point to full-suite tests, and review the data they provide with dashboards.

See [Instance Scan](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/instance-scan/hs-landing-page.md).

## Events

Track every occurrence in your app, from screen loads to user clicks. Usage Insights provides detailed charts and tables for every event, to show you where the most activity goes on in your app. Analyze core processes in your business and improve user efficiency and workflow.

See [System Events](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/system-events/events.md).

## Platform performance

Keep your system optimized and running smoothly. Use diagnostics to track processing and response times, network speeds and behaviors, and browser settings. Monitor platform performance, set quotas and control system bandwidth to ensure stable operations for users, set up test modules, and more.

See [Platform performance](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/platform-performance/platform-performance-landing.md).
