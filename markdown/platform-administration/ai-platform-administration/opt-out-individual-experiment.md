# Opt out of an individual experiment

Opt out of a specific experiment while remaining enrolled in others.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Experimentation Framework** &gt; **All Experiments**.

2.  Select the experiment that you want to opt out of.

3.  Select **Opt Out**.

4.  Select **OK**.

    **Note:** You can opt back in at any time.
