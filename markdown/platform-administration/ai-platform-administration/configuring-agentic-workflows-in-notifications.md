# Configure email agentic workflows in Notifications

Configure email agentic workflow in Notifications for email agentic workflow.

-   **[Activate Notifications Email Agents plugin](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/activate-notifications-email-agents-plugin.md)**  
You can activate the Notifications Email Agents plugin \(sn\_notif\_agents\) for Notifications if you have the admin role.
-   **[Enable intent to action workflow from inbound actions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/enable-intent-to-action.md)**  
Enable and configure intent to action workflow to invoke the agentic workflow from inbound actions.
-   **[Enable intent to action workflow from triggers](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/trigger-intent-to-action.md)**  
Enable and configure intent to action workflow to invoke this agentic workflow from triggers.
-   **[Create email intents](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/create-email-intent.md)**  
Create and define an email intent for analyzing and identifying the primary purpose for an inbound email. Understanding the email's intent helps guide the tone, context, and call-to-action for effective responses.
-   **[Create and associate actions for intent](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/ai-platform-administration/create-actions-for-intent.md)**  
Create and map actions to an intent for executing appropriate actions once the intent match happens.
