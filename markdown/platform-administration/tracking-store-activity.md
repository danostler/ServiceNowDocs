# Tracking your ServiceNow Store activity

The My Store Activity page lets you view important information related to apps and products that you have purchased, trialed, or requested.

## My Store Activity page

You can access the My Store Activity page from your user profile menu after logging in to the ServiceNow Store. From the My Store Activity page, you can select the **In Process**, **Store Applications**, or **ServiceNow Products** tabs to view the following information:

-   Apps your organization has procured
-   Payments \(available to enterprise customers only\)
-   Demo requests \(available to enterprise customers only\)

 From **Payments**, you can export all invoices by selecting **Export as CSV**. You can also view an individual invoice by selecting the transaction ID.

 From **Demo Requests**, you can view information about any trials you have requested, including the amount of time remaining for each trial.
