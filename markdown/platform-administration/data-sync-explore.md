# Exploring Multi-instance Management

Explore the capabilities provided by Multi-instance Management to control how your ServiceNow instances share data across applications. This is useful for synchronizing information between multiple instances in a single customer account.

## Multi-instance Management users

Multi-instance Management has the following roles.

|Users|Description|
|-----|-----------|
|admin| |
|sn\_mif.mif\_admin| |

## Multi-instance Management benefits

|Benefit|Feature|Users|
|-------|-------|-----|
|Provide protected data visibility with a trust configuration per instance and per application|[Basic trust configuration for data sync applications](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/grant-access-other-instances.md)|admin|
|Streamline the management of trust configurations across your entire multi-instance environment|[Cross-instance application trust configuration](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/platform-administration/grant-access-v2.md)|admin or sn\_mif.mif\_admin|
