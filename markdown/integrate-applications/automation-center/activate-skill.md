# Activate skills for Now Assist for Automation Center

Activate the skills available for Now Assist for Automation Center to work with ease and reduce manual effort in creating automation requests and exploring automations.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_generative\_ai.nsa\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Now Assist Admin** &gt; **Skills**.

2.  Select **Other** under **Platform**.

3.  Turn on the skill from the list.

    The skill is activated on your instance.

    -   User Task Summarization skill: Creates automation requests from the Task Mining Workspace
    -   Saving Estimator skill: Explores and discovers automations from your ServiceNow® instance.
