# Zero Copy Connector for ERP Hire to Retire content pack models

The Zero Copy Connector for ERP \(Enterprise Resource Planning\) Hire to Retire content pack contains models that you may need when interacting with an SAP SuccessFactors system. Hire to Retire is a process that manages the employee lifecycle.

## Hire to Retire content pack models

|Model name|Short description|Model type|Methods|
|----------|-----------------|----------|-------|
|Candidate|Retrieve a list of candidates and information such as name, contact details, and location.|ERP|OData V2|
|Employment Information|Retrieve a list of employee profiles and information such as hiring date, position and department.|ERP|OData V2|
|Employee Personal Information|Retrieve a list of employee profiles and information such as address and contact details.|ERP|OData V2|
|Job Application|Retrieve a list of job applications and information such as qualifications and experience.|ERP|OData V2|
|Job Requisition|Retrieve a list of job requisitions and information such as title, location, and description.|ERP|OData V2|
|Position|Retrieve a list of positions \(approved job requisitions\).|ERP|OData V2|
