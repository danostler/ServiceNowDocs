# Uninstall Workplace from Facebook spoke

Log in to Workplace from Facebook account to uninstall the Workplace from Facebook spoke.

## Before you begin

Role required: admin

## Procedure

1.  Log in to your Workplace from Facebook account as an admin.

2.  Navigate to **Admin Panel** &gt; **Integrations**.

3.  Enter password.

4.  Click **ServiceNow Spoke**.

5.  Click **Uninstall**.

6.  In the Uninstall window, confirm your choice by clicking **Uninstall** again.

    The Workplace from Facebook spoke is uninstalled.
