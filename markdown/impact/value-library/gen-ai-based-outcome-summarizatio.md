# Gen AI based outcome summarization

View a concise summary of your operational outcomes in the Impact store application. This summary could include positive or negative outcomes trends, adoption of recommended applications, and consumption of related accelerators.

## Before you begin

**Note:**

Access to the Outcomes summarization feature is determined by your Impact Package or a Pro Plus license for any ServiceNow product.

Role required:Impact App Admin, Impact Platform Owner, Impact Portfolio Owner.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Impact** &gt; **Value Management**.

2.  Select the outcome insight.

3.  In the Quick links section, select the **Outcome details page** link.

4.  In the Summarize this Outcome section, select **Summarize**.

    Now Assist generates and displays summary information for the outcome, as shown in the following example.

    \[Omitted image "outcomes\_summarizationresult.png"\] Alt text: Outcomes Summarization result

5.  Provide feedback for the summary by selecting the thumbs-up \[Omitted image "thumbs-up-outline-24.svg"\] or thumbs-down \[Omitted image "thumbs-down-outline-24.svg"\] icons.

6.  Copy the summary to use its information outside the app, for example, for including in an email or editing before forwarding, by selecting the Copy to clipboard icon \[Omitted image "copytoclipboard.png"\] Alt text: Copy to clipboard.

7.  If you think that data might have changed after you viewed the summary, refresh the summary information by selecting the redo icon \[Omitted image "refresh\_aisummary.png"\] Alt text:.
