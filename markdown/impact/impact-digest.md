# Impact Digest

Get personalized monthly updates in the form of Impact Digest.

Impact Digest is generated automatically on the second Tuesday of every month and offers a personalized Impact consumption snapshot with curated content from Customer Support Managers \(CSMs\) on ServiceNow products, and Impact content. With manual curation of content by a CSM Newsletter team, based on Impact Squad’s opted topics and insightful consumption statistics, recognize and celebrate the Impact journey every month. Access your goals and improve platform health, product adoption, and increase value realized.
