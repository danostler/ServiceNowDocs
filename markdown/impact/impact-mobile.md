# Mobile Executive Experience

The mobile Impact Executive Experience allows customer executives to access Impact from anywhere on their mobile browser.

## Mobile Executive Experience Overview

The mobile executive experience enables CXO to:

-   Quickly view their organization’s performance focusing on the value they have realized from their objectives.
-   Removes the detail layers provided to the platform owner persona, so that the executive does not need to spend any time hunting for the information that matters to them.

**Note:** This experience is only available to users with the executive role.
