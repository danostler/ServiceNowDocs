# Upload document from My Tasks page

Complete the document upload tasks assigned to you from the My tasks page on the Supplier Collaboration Portal.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_slm.contact

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to the Supplier Collaboration Portal home page by accessing your instance URL and adding a /supplier suffix.

    For example, `https://example.com/supplier`.

2.  In the portal header, select **My Tasks**.

3.  Go to the **Open** tab.

4.  Select a document upload task.

5.  In the Attachments section, upload your document.

    \[Omitted image "doc-upload-my-tasks.png"\] Alt text: Attachments section for uploading documents

6.  Select **Submit**.
