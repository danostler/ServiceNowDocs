# Invoice processing case form tabs

The Invoice form includes tabs that store invoice information that an Accounts Payable Specialist can use to perform related tasks.

<table id="table_bv2_qsd_2xb"><thead><tr><th>

Tab

</th><th>

Description

</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td>

Details

</td><td>

Details about the case.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Invoice lines

</td><td>

Invoice lines of the invoice.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Exceptions

</td><td>

Exceptions that are identified on the invoice.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Purchase order lines

</td><td>

Purchase order lines of the purchase order for which the invoice has been raised.This tab is shown only for invoices of type **PO Invoice**.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Tasks

</td><td>

Tasks that have been created for this case by the Accounts Payable Specialist.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Potential duplicate invoices

</td><td>

Invoices that may be a duplicate of the invoice currently associated with this case.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Approvals

</td><td>

Approval requests for the invoice.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Related invoices

</td><td>

Related invoices from the same supplier.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Emails

</td><td>

Emails that are sent or received as part of resolving this case.

</td></tr><tr><td>

Task SLAs

</td><td>

The service level agreements that are associated with this case.
