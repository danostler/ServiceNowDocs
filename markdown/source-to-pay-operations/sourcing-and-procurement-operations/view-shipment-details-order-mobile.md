# View shipment details of your order from your mobile device

View the current status of your ordered items, along with the tracking number and carrier name, in your receipt to-dos, from your mobile application.

## Before you begin

To view the status of your ordered items, you should have received dispatch notice regarding your orders from the supplier.

Role required: sn\_shop.shopper

## About this task

You can view the status, tracking number, and carrier name for your ordered items that the supplier has dispatched from their end.

## Procedure

1.  Log in to your instance in the Now Mobile application.

2.  Select **My Tasks**.

3.  Open the receipt to-do you want to work with.

4.  On the **To-do** tab, select **Shipment details**.

    Details of the dispatched items, including tracking number, carrier name, and status, are displayed.
