# Raise a general inquiry for procurement

As a shopper, raise an inquiry for any pre-existing purchase or for any other queries that you may have regarding any procurement function​​.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_shop.shopper

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Employee Center** &gt; **Purchase and Expense** &gt; **Purchase Requests**.

2.  Select the **Ask Procurement** card.

3.  On the form, fill in the details of the query.

Is this about a specific purchase?

</td><td>

Based on your requirement, select one of these.-   If your inquiry is about a pre-existing purchase, select **Yes**.

**Note:** You can also raise this inquiry from **All** &gt; **ShoppingHub Home** &gt; **My purchases**, by selecting **Inquire** from the more actions icon on a purchase.

-   If your inquiry is about something else, select **No**.

</td><td>

</td><td>

</td></tr></tbody>
</table>    **Note:** Customers can configure the questions in the Ask Procurement form, introduce new questions, or enable additional validations, if required, from the respective record producer records.

4.  Select **Submit** to submit your inquiry, if none of the related search results have answered your query.


## What to do next

A procurement case of type Inquiry is created under **Procurement Cases** in Source-to-Pay Workspace, which is assigned to a fulfiller. The fulfiller can then help you with your inquiry.
