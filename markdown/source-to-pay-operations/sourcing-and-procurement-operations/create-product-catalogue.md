# Setting up your product catalog

You can set up your product catalog by grouping the supplier products that you want to appear on the Shopping Hub portal into product categories and product models.

-   **[Create product categories to group product models](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/source-to-pay-operations/sourcing-and-procurement-operations/create-product-model-categories.md)**  
Create categories for your product models to group your product models.
-   **[Create product models to group supplier products](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/source-to-pay-operations/sourcing-and-procurement-operations/create-product-models.md)**  
Create product models to group your supplier products.
-   **[Add a supplier product](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/source-to-pay-operations/sourcing-and-procurement-operations/add-supplier-product.md)**  
Add supplier products to populate the primary data.
-   **[Add a supplier product bundle](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/source-to-pay-operations/sourcing-and-procurement-operations/add-supplier-product-bundle.md)**  
Add a supplier product bundle, which may include several products or services from the same supplier. You can also add sub-bundles to the main bundle.
