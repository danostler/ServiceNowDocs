# View your assigned In-store operations cases and tasks in Retail Mobile

Review all assigned in-store operations cases and tasks in the My Work tab of Retail Mobile.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_rtl\_instore\_ops.manager or sn\_rtl\_instore\_ops.associate

## Procedure

1.  In **Retail Mobile**, select **My Work**.

2.  Select one of the following tabs:

    -   Cases - Store cases that are assigned to you.
    -   Tasks - Store tasks that are assigned to you.
3.  Select a case or task to review its details and take action as needed.
