# Add members to a retail organization

Add members to your organization to enable them to view and complete tasks within your retail organization.

## Before you begin

Role required: sn\_rtl.retail\_admin

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Retail Service Management** &gt; **Retail Organization**.

2.  Choose the retail organization.

3.  Select **Add Members** under Related Links.

4.  Fill in the following fields.

Ensure that this member has the appropriate role. For more information, see [Assign roles to Retail users](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/australia/markdown/retail-industry/retail-operations/rahi-retail-assign-roles-users.md).
