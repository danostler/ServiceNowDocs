# Migrating to Smart Assessment from survey-based questionnaire

You can configure Smart Assessment questionnaires by migrating from existing survey-based questionnaires and use smart assessments to gather data from users.

The steps to migrate from survey-based questionnaires are:

1.  [Migrate a survey-based questionnaire to Smart Assessment](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/field-service-management/mobile-experience-for-field-service-management-glide-family/migrate-to-smart-assessment-template.md)
2.  [Author and publish a Smart Assessment template](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/field-service-management/mobile-experience-for-field-service-management-glide-family/create-a-smart-assessment-template.md)
3.  [Migrate survey instances to Smart Assessment](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/field-service-management/mobile-experience-for-field-service-management-glide-family/migrate-survey-instances-to-smart-assessment.md)
