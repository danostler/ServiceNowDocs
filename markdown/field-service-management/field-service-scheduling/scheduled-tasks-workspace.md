# Manage scheduled tasks in Dispatcher Workspace

As a dispatcher, you can move scheduled tasks to the Assigned state or move them back to the Pending dispatch state in Dispatcher Workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_dispatcher or wm\_crew\_moderator

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Field Service** &gt; **Dispatching** &gt; **Dispatcher Workspace**.

2.  View the list of all scheduled tasks, including crew tasks, in the dispatcher group by selecting **Scheduled Tasks** in the Tasks filter.

3.  Assign or update a scheduled task.
