# Export saved ad hoc filters

Export ad hoc filters saved in the Dispatcher Workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_dispatcher

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Field Service** &gt; **Dispatching** &gt; **Dispatcher Workspace**.

2.  Select **List**.

3.  Under **Lists**, navigate to **Saved Filters** &gt; **Task Filter**.

4.  Select **Export**.

5.  Select a **File Type**.

6.  Select a **Delivery Type**.

    -   Select **Download** to download locally.
    -   Select **Email**, then enter an email to send to.
7.  Select **Export**.


## Result

The saved filters are formatted and exported to the desired delivery type.
