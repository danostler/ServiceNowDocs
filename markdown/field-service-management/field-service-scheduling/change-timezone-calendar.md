# Change the time zone in Dispatcher Workspace

You can change the time zone that shows on the calendar or hybrid view in Dispatcher Workspace to quickly move between the time zones that your agents are working in.

## About this task

When you change the time zone in Dispatcher Workspace, the agent card shows the difference in hours between the time zone you select and the agent’s time zone if there’s a difference between the two.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_dispatcher

You must have more than one time zone configured. For more information, see the Calendar section in [Enable Dispatcher Workspace settings](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/field-service-management/field-service-scheduling/dispatcher-wrkspc-settings.md).

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Field Service** &gt; **Dispatching** &gt; **Dispatcher Workspace**.

2.  Select Dispatcher Workspace.

3.  Select the **Calendar**, or **Hybrid** view.

4.  Select the time zone drop-down list.

    \[Omitted image "timezone-dropdown.png"\] Alt text: dispatcher workspace timezone drop-down

5.  Select a time zone.


## Result

The time zone that you selected shows in Dispatcher Workspace.
