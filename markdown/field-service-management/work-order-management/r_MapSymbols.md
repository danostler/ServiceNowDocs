# SLA map symbols

Icons in the SLA map and what they represent.

|Icon|Description|
|----|-----------|
|\[Omitted image "GreenTruck.png"\] Alt text: Green truck icon.|Current location of an agent selected in the filter. Agents identified with this icon are on time or ahead of schedule.|
|\[Omitted image "Agent.png"\] Alt text: Blue truck icon.|Current location of an agent who is less than 30 minutes behind schedule.|
|\[Omitted image "YellowTruck.png"\] Alt text: Yellow truck icon.|Current location of an agent who is between 30 and 60 minutes behind schedule.|
|\[Omitted image "AgentBehind.png"\] Alt text: Red truck icon.|Current location of an agent who is at least one hour behind schedule.|
|\[Omitted image "AgentOffSchedule.png"\] Alt text: Gray truck icon.|Last known location of an agent who is not currently on the schedule or working on any tasks.|
|\[Omitted image "IconG.png"\] Alt text: Green marker.|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 0 to less than 25.|
|\[Omitted image "Icony.png"\] Alt text: Yellow marker.|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 25 to less than 50.|
|\[Omitted image "Icono.png"\] Alt text: Orange marker.|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 50 to less than 75.|
|\[Omitted image "IconR.png"\] Alt text: Red marker.|Tasks with a business elapsed percentage from 75 to 100.|
|\[Omitted image "MultiTask.png"\] Alt text: Red indicator with the number of tasks.|Multiple active tasks with SLAs, clustered by proximity.|
|\[Omitted image "MultiAgent.png"\] Alt text: Blue indicator with the number of agents.|Multiple agents, clustered by proximity.|
|\[Omitted image "AgentTaskCombo.png"\] Alt text: Purple indicator with the sum of agents and tasks.|Sum of agents and tasks with SLAs, clustered by proximity.|
