# Manage Field Service agents from Field Service Manager Mobile

Manage Field Service agents from Field Service Manager Mobile and do everything that you can from your computer while on the go.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_manager, sn\_fsm\_tp.fsm\_territory\_resource\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Open Field Service Manager Mobile and tap **Teams**.

2.  Tap the assignment group or territory that the Field Service agent you want to manage is part of.

3.  Tap the more actions icon \(\[Omitted image "more-action-manager.png"\] Alt text: more actions\).

4.  Choose from the following.
