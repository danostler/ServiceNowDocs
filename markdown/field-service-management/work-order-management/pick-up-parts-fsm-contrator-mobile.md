# Pick up parts using the Field Service Contractor for mobile application

Pick up the part by transferring it from an available stockroom when you are at a task site and need a missing part to complete your job.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_ext\_agent, wm\_ext\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Access your instance using the ServiceNow Agent mobile application.

2.  Tap **Inventory**.

3.  Tap **Part Transfers**.

4.  Tap **Pick Up**.

5.  Tap a part for pick up, swipe to the left and select **Confirm pickup**.

    The sourcing stages for the part appears. If the part is in **Received** stage, you can confirm pick up.

6.  Click **Confirm pickup**.


## Result

The sourcing stage moves to Delivered and the part is added to your inventory and is marked as available.
