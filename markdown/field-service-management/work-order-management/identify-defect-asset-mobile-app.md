# Analyze all work orders for a selected asset

Scan an asset to view its work history and upcoming work orders. You can also create a work order for an asset.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_agent or wm\_dispatcher

## Procedure

1.  Open the ServiceNow Agent application.

2.  In the **My Work** tab, tap **Asset Lookup** at the top.

3.  Tap the barcode icon and scan the asset.

4.  Tap the asset record.

5.  Do one of the following.
