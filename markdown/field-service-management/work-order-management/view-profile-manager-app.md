# View your profile in Field Service Manager Mobile

Managers can view their profile to check on their personal details.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_manager, sn\_fsm\_tp.fsm\_territory\_resource\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Open Field Service Manager Mobile.

2.  Tap your profile picture.

    You see your phone number, email address, and location.

3.  Tap **Related** and choose from the following.

    |Selection|Action|
    |---------|------|
    |**Tap __My tasks__**|To see a list of the work order tasks that are assigned to you.|
    |**Tap __My shift schedule__**|To see your assigned work schedule.|
