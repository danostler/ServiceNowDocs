# View analytics from Field Service Manager Mobile

Managers can view analytics related to the teams they manage to see team task load, agent workload, and more.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_manager, sn\_fsm\_tp.fsm\_territory\_resource\_manager

## Procedure

1.  Open Field Service Manager Mobile.

2.  Tap **Analytics**.

3.  Tap any of the analytics charts to drill down into the details of the analytics.

4.  Select one of the priority pill items at the bottom to exclude that priority from the analytics donut.

    **Note:** Select the deselected pill to add the priority back to the analytics donut.
