# Manage time cards in the time sheet

Manage your time cards in the ServiceNow Agent mobile app by performing actions such as submitting or deleting a time card or viewing notes.

## Before you begin

Role required: wm\_agent

## Procedure

1.  Open the Now Mobile Agent application.

2.  Tap the **Time Sheets** tab and open a time sheet.

3.  In the **All Week** tab, tap the action menu for a time card.

4.  Manage your time cards.
