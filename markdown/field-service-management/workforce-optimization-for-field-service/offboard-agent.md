# Offboard an external agent

Offboard external managers or agents of the contractor company to terminate their services.

## Before you begin

Before offboarding a manager or an agent, ensure that they are not assigned to any task and remove them manually from the assigned assignment groups and stockrooms.

Role required: wm\_admin, wm\_contractor\_manager\_int

## About this task

An offboarded manager or agent cannot log in to the Field Service Contractor Portal and do not receive any notification through email or SMS.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Outsourced Field Service** &gt; **Outsourced Service Providers**.

2.  From the list of field service providers, click a contractor company from which you want to offboard an agent.

3.  Click the **Contacts** tab.

4.  From the **Service Organization External Staffs** list, click an agent whom you want to offboard.

5.  Click **Offboard Agent**.


## Result

The external manager or agent is removed from all the assigned groups.
