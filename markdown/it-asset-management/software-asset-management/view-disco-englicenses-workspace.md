# View discovered engineering licenses in workspace

View a list of all the discovered and normalized software for your engineering applications in the Software Asset Workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: sam\_admin or sam\_user

You can view a list of all the normalized publishers and products for your engineering applications. Additional information such as the version, quantity of licenses, and the type of license is also displayed.

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Software asset** &gt; **Software Asset Workspace** &gt; **License operations**.

2.  Select **Discovered engineering license** from the Discovery list view.

3.  You can click a record to view additional details.

    This takes you to the Company page in the classic view.
