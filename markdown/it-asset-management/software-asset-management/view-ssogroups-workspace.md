# View SSO groups in workspace

View details related to all SSO groups that have access to a SSO application in the Software Asset Workspace.

## Before you begin

Role required: sam\_admin or sam\_user

## Procedure

1.  Navigate to **All** &gt; **Software asset** &gt; **Software Asset Workspace** &gt; **License operations**.

2.  Select **SSO groups**.

3.  Select a group record to view additional details such as the users and child groups within that group.
