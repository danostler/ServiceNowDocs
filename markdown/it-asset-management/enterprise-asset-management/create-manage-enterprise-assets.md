# Create and manage enterprise assets

Create and manage enterprise assets throughout their life cycle.

-   **[Create a multi-component asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-multicomponent-asset.md)**  
Track and manage your assets by creating a multi-component asset from a multi-component model.
-   **[Create enterprise assets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-asset-eam.md)**  
Create assets for specific model categories in the Enterprise Asset Management application.
-   **[Create linear assets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-linear-asset.md)**  
Create linear assets to diversify your Enterprise Asset Management portfolio.
-   **[Create linear asset types](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-linear-asset-types.md)**  
Create linear asset types to categorize linear assets.
-   **[Create segments for linear assets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/define-linear-segments.md)**  
Create segments for linear assets to assign work orders to the segments. You can also create maintenance plans for the segments.
-   **[Associate a discrete asset to a linear asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/associate-discreet-asset.md)**  
Associate a discrete asset to a linear asset in order to query and find these assets from the linear asset at a later point of time.
-   **[Find linear asset relationships](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/find-linear-asset-relships.md)**  
You can find relationships between linear assets such as overlapping, continuing, or intersecting assets.
-   **[Create pallet assets in the Enterprise Asset Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-pallet-asset-eam.md)**  
Create pallet assets to track and manage the pallets that can transport and store your enterprise, hardware, base, bundle, and consumable assets.
-   **[Update enterprise assets that have unknown enterprise models](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/updated-enterprise-assets-unknown-enterprise-model.md)**  
If an enterprise asset is associated with an unknown enterprise model, update it with a known enterprise model so that you can accurately categorize and track it.
-   **[Swap assets for parent multi-component asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/swap-assets-eam.md)**  
Swap child assets that are part of a multi-component asset. You can swap out only one child asset at a time.
-   **[Select assets for user-assembled asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/assemble-assets-eam.md)**  
Create a user-assembled asset by selecting assets from the parent asset's stockroom.
-   **[Release assets from the parent asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/release-assets-eam.md)**  
Release a child asset that is no longer required to be associated with its parent asset.
-   **[Create additional child assets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-addon-asset.md)**  
Create additional child assets for the model even if the assets are not defined in the model component.
-   **[Configuring Total Cost of Ownership for enterprise assets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/confifure-eam-asset-tco.md)**  
Configure your Total Cost of Ownership \(TCO\) to track your assets.
-   **[Add calibration attributes to an enterprise asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/add-calibration-attributes-enterprise-asset.md)**  
Add calibration attributes to an enterprise asset to specify and manage the calibrations that are required for the asset throughout its life cycle.
-   **[Clone a calibration attribute to an enterprise asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/clone-calibration-attributes-enterprise-asset.md)**  
Clone an existing calibration attribute to an enterprise asset to add a calibration attribute with similar settings.
-   **[Create a calibration category for your enterprise asset calibrations](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-eam-calibration-categories.md)**  
Create a calibration category to indicate the type of calibration that you need to perform on your enterprise assets.
-   **[Create condition templates for condition attributes](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-condition-template-eam.md)**  
Create a condition template and associate the template to condition attributes for enterprise models and assets.
-   **[Define condition attributes on enterprise models and assets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-condition-attribute-eam.md)**  
Define the conditions to be inspected on enterprise models and assets throughout their life cycle.
-   **[Create an asset group in Enterprise Asset Management](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-asset-groups-eam.md)**  
Create asset groups to create a logical group of entities that includes assets, locations, and users.
-   **[Create consumable assets in Enterprise Asset Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-consumables-eam.md)**  
Create consumables in the Enterprise Asset Workspace to track enterprise assets.
-   **[Use consumable assets in Enterprise Asset Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-eamconsumable-asset.md)**  
To use consumable assets, ensure that they are in the **In stock** state and **Available** substate.
-   **[Create a quality standard for your enterprise asset calibrations](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-eam-calibration-quality-standard.md)**  
Create a quality standard to indicate the requirements and guidelines that you must follow while performing your enterprise asset calibrations.
-   **[Create a service frequency for your enterprise asset calibrations](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-eam-calibration-service-frequency.md)**  
Create a service frequency to specify how often you must perform an enterprise asset calibration.
-   **[Create a calibration attribute library](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-eam-calibration-attribute-library.md)**  
Create a library of common calibration attributes that are not associated with any enterprise models or enterprise assets. Use these attributes to define the details and requirements of your commonly used calibrations.
-   **[Dispose consumable assets in Enterprise Asset Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/dispose-eamconsumable-assets.md)**  
Dispose of those consumable assets that are no longer required in your stockrooms.
-   **[Create an asset onboarding process](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/create-asset-onboarding-task-eam.md)**  
Create an asset onboarding process to walk you through the process of entering important information for assets.
-   **[Track assets using indoor maps in the Enterprise Asset Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/track-enterprise-assets-indoor-maps.md)**  
Use indoor maps to locate and track the enterprise assets, consumable assets, and pallets that are deployed across your organization. Each indoor map consists of an interactive interface that enables you to visualize where your assets are located within your campuses, buildings, floors, and places.
-   **[View the asset hierarchy in the Enterprise Asset Workspace](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/view-asset-hierarchy-eam.md)**  
View the hierarchy details of a multi-component asset in a tree-like format in the Enterprise Asset Workspace.
-   **[View the total time worked on all tasks related to an enterprise asset](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/view-total-time-spent-on-asset-eam.md)**  
Use the Asset task time summary report to see the details of the time spent on all tasks for an asset and its child assets, grouped by task category.
-   **[Monitor enterprise asset performance with Asset KPIs report](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/it-asset-management/enterprise-asset-management/view-availability-for-ent-aaset.md)**  
Track the asset availability and related KPI values in the report to see how well an enterprise asset is performing.
