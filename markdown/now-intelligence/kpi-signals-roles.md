# KPI Signals roles

The following roles apply to KPI Signals

-   **Any role**

    A user with any role can be made responsible for receiving and responding to signal notifications for a specific indicator. This user can also configure signal detection and notification and assign responsibility for the indicators they are responsible for. They must also have access to the indicator.

-   **pc\_kpi\_signals\_admin**

    A user with this role or who inherits this role \(via pa\_admin or admin\) can activate KPI Signals for an indicator. They can also configure signal detection and notification and assign responsibility for any indicator on the instance.
