## CHRO Dashboard overview

-   **Purpose**

    The CHRO Dashboard provides an integrated view of HR operational performance and people strategy, enabling leaders to monitor workforce health, improve employee experience, and accelerate organizational growth.

    It offers actionable insights into onboarding efficiency, case resolution, talent development, diversity, hiring outcomes, retention risks, and compensation trends—supporting both day-to-day HR operations and long-term workforce planning.

    By centralizing key metrics across the employee life-cycle, the dashboard enables HR organizations to proactively address workforce challenges, optimize processes, and align people initiatives with organizational goals.

-   **Target Audience**

    The CHRO Dashboard is designed for HR executives, HR operations teams, people analytics professionals, and business leaders who require real-time visibility into workforce performance and talent outcomes. It supports users who must track hiring pipelines, evaluate service delivery, strengthen engagement, manage risk, and drive diversity and inclusion initiatives.

    The dashboard also serves HR business partners and department heads who rely on accurate HR insights to support organizational priorities and guide workforce decisions.

-   **Primary Stakeholders**
    -   Chief Human Resources Officer
    -   HR Operations and Shared Services Teams
    -   Talent Acquisition Leaders
    -   People Development
    -   Learning and Development Teams
    -   Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion Leaders
    -   Employee Relations and Compliance Teams
    -   HR Business Partners \(HRBPs\)
    -   Finance &amp; Executive Leadership
