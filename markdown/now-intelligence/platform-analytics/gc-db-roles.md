# General Counsel Dashboard roles

Two roles are associated with this dashboard.

## Platform Analytics administrator

The dashboard administrator \(sn\_lsd\_dashboard.gc\_dashboard\_admin\) is responsible for installing, configuring, and maintaining the GC Dashboard and its underlying content.

Key duties include:

-   Installing plugins and assigning the admin roles to configuration owners
-   Defining verticals and business questions \(for example, Value, Operations, Security\)
-   Selecting and tuning KPIs/KRIs, setting thresholds, and mapping data sources
-   Creating and validating Platform Analytics indicators, breakdowns, and data-collection jobs
-   Managing translations, plugin upgrades, and best-practice templates to ensure consistency

## Business user

The user \(sn\_lsd\_dashboard.gc\_dashboard\_user\) role is required to access the dashboard.

Typical activities:

-   Viewing real-time scorecards and visualizations aligned to strategic goals
-   Applying filters, switching time frames, and drilling into underlying records \(incidents, projects, security events\)
-   Exporting charts and reports for executive briefs or stakeholder presentations
-   Subscribing to alerts and notifications when metrics cross defined thresholds
-   Providing feedback on dashboard usability and data relevance to the admin team
