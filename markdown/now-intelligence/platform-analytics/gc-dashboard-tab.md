## GC Dashboard overview

The General Counsel \(GC\) Dashboard provides a unified overview of legal operations performance, consolidating insights into contracts, service delivery, privacy and compliance, digital invoicing, and self-service efficiency across the enterprise.

-   **Purpose**

    The GC Dashboard centralizes the performance indicators of legal operations to provide transparency into contract processing, legal workload, privacy compliance, financial invoicing, and self-service effectiveness. It enables the legal function to make data-driven decisions, improve turnaround times, reduce operational friction, and align legal processes with organizational needs.

    By bringing together insights such as aging requests, MTTR, invoice resolution times, backlog volumes, and top self-service usage, the dashboard helps the legal department proactively manage risk, ensure compliance, and optimize the delivery of legal services across the enterprise.

-   **Target audience**

    The GC Dashboard is designed for legal operations leaders, general counsel executives, privacy officers, and legal service delivery teams who require continuous visibility into legal workload, compliance response, and contract performance.

    This dashboard also supports business leaders who depend on timely legal reviews, contract execution, privacy compliance, and accurate invoicing processes.

-   **Primary Stakeholders**
    -   General Counsel
    -   Contracts and Commercial Legal Teams
    -   Privacy and Compliance Officers
    -   Finance and Legal Invoice Management Teams
    -   Business Stakeholders \(Sales, HR, Procurement, IT, and so on\)
    -   Employees and End Users Using Legal Self-Service
