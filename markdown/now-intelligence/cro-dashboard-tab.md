## CRO Dashboard overview

This dashboard is for the Chief Risk Officer and others who require real-time insights into governance, risk, and compliance.

-   **Purpose**

    The CRO Dashboard acts as a centralized command center for enterprise-wide risk governance, compliance oversight, business continuity readiness, and audit integrity.

    It consolidates indicators such as risk impact, open issues, failed controls, compliance scores, and crisis response events, enabling senior leaders to assess:

    -   Enterprise-wide risk exposure and trends
    -   Audit and control effectiveness
    -   Policy and regulatory compliance maturity
    -   Business continuity preparedness and crisis handling performance
    -   Ownership, aging, and clearance of enterprise issues
    This enables proactive decision-making, faster remediation, and stronger governance maturity.

-   **Target audience**

    The CRO Dashboard is designed for executive leadership and governance stakeholders who need visibility into enterprise-wide risk posture, regulatory compliance, audit rigor, and continuity readiness.

-   **Primary Stakeholders**
    -   Chief Risk Officer \(CRO\)
    -   Chief Customer Operations Officer \(CCO\)
    -   Internal Audit Leadership
    -   Enterprise Risk and Governance Teams
    -   Business Continuity and Crisis Management Teams
    -   Executive Reviewers and Board Members
