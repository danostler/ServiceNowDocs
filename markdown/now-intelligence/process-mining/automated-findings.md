# Automated improvement opportunities

Automated improvement opportunities are the improvement opportunities that are available in Process Mining. Set them to understand the improvement opportunities for your process.

**Note:** You can have a maximum of 15 automated improvement opportunities for a project.

In Process Mining the following improvement opportunities are available:

-   **[Configure ping-pong finding definition](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/now-intelligence/process-mining/automated-builder.md)**  
Configure ping-pong finding definitions to view a pattern where a record bounces back and forth between two steps without interruption.
-   **[Configure extra step finding definition](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/now-intelligence/process-mining/extra-step.md)**  
Configure an extra-step finding definition to view a pattern where routes differ by one additional step.
-   **[Configure slow transitions](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/now-intelligence/process-mining/slow-transitions.md)**  
Configure a slow transitions finding definition to view a pattern where clusters of records have an unusual duration that's higher than the average duration of another group of records.
-   **[Configure extreme duration finding definition](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/now-intelligence/process-mining/extreme-duration.md)**  
Configure an extreme duration finding definition to view a pattern where transitions take significantly longer than the usual duration between the steps.
-   **[Configure extreme repetitions finding definition](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/now-intelligence/process-mining/extreme-repetitions.md)**  
Configure an extreme repetitions finding definition to view a pattern where a transition repeats more than the usual repetition range between the steps.
-   **[Configure repeating pattern finding definition](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/now-intelligence/process-mining/repeating-pattern.md)**  
Configure a repeating pattern finding definition to view a pattern with a series of repeating sequence of steps.
-   **[Configure rework finding definition](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ServiceNow/ServiceNowDocs/zurich/markdown/zurich/now-intelligence/process-mining/rework.md)**  
Configure a rework finding definition to view a pattern where a step in the process is repeated.
